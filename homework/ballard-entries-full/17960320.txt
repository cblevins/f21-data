Clear and Cold. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to meeting at the fort, Nabby to the hook. thee remains of the Widdow Petingail were interd at 5h pm. I was Calld by mr Garish to See mrs Welch who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 7#h$ evening of a Daughter which Shee declared Said Garish was the Father of. I left her as well as Could be expected and returnd home at 10, find mr Allen of Monmoth here. at mrs Welch*s. Birth 13th. Funeralof the Widdow Peting#l$.
