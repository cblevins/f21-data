Clear & pleast. I was Calld at y#e$ 12#th$ h to go to George Bolton*s wife in travil. mr Ballard went after his horse which was lent to Polly Savage. I waited 2 hours & mr Usher Came with Captain Savage*s Mare. I Sett out alone & arivd Safe. my anxciety was great for y#e$ woman, but I found her Safe. Shee was Safe Deld at y#e$ 9#th$ h evening & I returnd at 2. at Green Lodg.
