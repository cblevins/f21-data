Clear. I went to See mrs Burten, find her Cleverly; Dind there. was Calld from there to Jerry White*s wife who is unwell. Set with her till 2 h m, was then Calld to See Captain Hussey*s Lady in Labour; her attenDants were mrs Cox, Hinkley, White, Goodin & Sally, his Sister, & widdo Norcross. at Jerry White*s & mr Burten*s.
