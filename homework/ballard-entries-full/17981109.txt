Clear and pleast. I Sett out for winslow at noon, went as far as mr Capin*s where Son Jonathan & Lambard & wife overtook me. Jonathan and I went on to Son Town*s, they tarried at mr Capin*s. we arivd at 8h 30#m$ evening. mrs Straten and Patty Collard were there, but went home after we arivd. at Son Town*s, Son Jonathan allso.
