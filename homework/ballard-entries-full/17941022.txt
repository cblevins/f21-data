Cloudy. mrs Robbins Linguerd till 4#h$ pm when her illness Came on. Dr Parker was Called but Shee Did not wish to See him when he Came & he returnd home. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son, her first Born, at 10h 30m evening & is as well as Can be Expected. I tarried & watcht. mrs Ney was there and Shee only, Excep#t$ y#e$ famely. at mr Robbins. Birth 42#nt$.
