Clear morn. Showers afternoon. mrs Welch here; had 3 lb of Tow of me & 2 lb 6 oz of flax of Parthenia. Joseph Foster here; says his wife is not So well as Shee was. I went to See Joseph Williams; find him much as he has been. Robbins went from here. Dolly Put in a pease for Jonathan. we had six men to work to Day. I was at mr Williams*s.
