Cloudy & moderate. mr Ballard been up to for#t$, at y#e$ hook. Cyrus to winthrop for Jonathan as hors. I have helped Do house work & knit. Pally Easty warp#t$ & Beemd a web & went to Jonathan a#s$ for hernes. I was Calld at 10h evening to See mrs Robbins who is unwell. I went to bed after 2h morn. at mr Robbins.
