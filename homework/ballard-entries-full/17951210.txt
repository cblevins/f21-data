Cloudy. I have been washing. mr Ben#n$ Brown, his wife & Son Dind here. Captain Hersey here, paid his parrish Tax. mr Ballard been makeing plan. Cyrus went to Jonathan as, bro#t$ 3 lb of tallow. we have had 7 lb before, he brot three pecks apples from there allso; and 1 Bushel Onions from mr Hamlin*s as a presen#t$. at home. mr Ben#n$ Brown, h#s$ wife, Son & Captain Hersey here.
