Cloudy. I was Calld at at [sic] 4h morn to See the wife off Elisha Prescot who was in Labour, & was Safe Deld off a Son at 5h evening. Shee was very ill, her Husband was absent. I was attended by an English young Lady who Lodges at mrs Sherburn*s. I left my Patients Cleverly & arive at home at 9#h$ evening; find my Son, his wife & Son here, all well. at Prescot*s. Birth 37#th$.Son Jonathan & wife here. I Bot a Chees, w#t$ 11 lb @ /7 pr lb.
