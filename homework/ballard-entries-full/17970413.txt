Son Town Calld me out of bed at 1h 5m Morn, I find my Daughter very ill, not able to be removd from bed, Shee was Dild of a Daughter and all performd which her labour required at 1h 20m. no woman arivd till after all was performd but Dresing the infant. this her Eleventh Child, Shee is more Comfortable than I Ever Saw her. at ditto. Birth 20 th. I have been Some unwellthis Day.
