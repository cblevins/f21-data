Clear and Pleasant. we had our Piggs Butchered by Son*s Jonathan & Lambard, Son Town assisted. I was Calld about noon to See mrs Ficky who was in labour and was Dild of a Daughter at 3h pm, the wives of Shubael & James Hinkley attended. I Came home before Sun Sett. at mr Ficky*s. Birth 1#st$ Phillip Sleeps here.
