Clear. we had a great frost this morn. I was Calld to See Polly Densmore foren, administred a Clister, went again at Sun Sett and tarried till 10h evening; performd the opperation twice. Shee Seems a little Easier. mrs Capin, Gill & Daughter Lambard, Augustus Ballard & Son Cyrus Dind here. the Gent#mn$ Came to Tea and waited on their wives home. at mr Densmore*s Twice this Day. we hadCompany.
