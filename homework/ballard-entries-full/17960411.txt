Cloudy. at mr Caton*s ye#t$. his wife is not fitt to be left. he went to the river. Shee wisht to Drink wine and Eat Bisquit, he procured it and Shee Drank Eleven Glasses this Day, and Eat Bisquit and wine at evening 3 times. at mr Caton*s. I finisht knitting my yarn,have knit 1 pair hoes Since I lef#t$ home.
