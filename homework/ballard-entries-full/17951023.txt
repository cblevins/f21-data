Cloudy, raind afternoon. I went to mr Densmore*s <STRIKE>aftern</STRIKE> after Breakfast. Dolly is a little Easier. Dr Coney there. I went from there to Son Pollard*s. my Daughter has a Severe head aick. I gave her a Clister and wint to See mrs Savage, helped her out of Bed and tarrid Some time. Shee is full of Complaints. I returnd, gave my Daughter another Clister and left her. Calld and performd the Same opperation for Polly Densmore and Came home at 8h evening. at Son Pollard*s and other Sick famelies.
