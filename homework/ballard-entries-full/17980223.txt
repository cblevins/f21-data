Clear and very pleasant. mr Soal Calld me to See his wife at 6h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 2 pm of a fine Daughter, her Sixth Child. mrs Smith, Dickman, Pitts and Daughter Lambard [assistants]. I returnd home at 6h evening, left my patients Cleverly. my Son Ephraim Came up from Dresden this day, he Calld here. at [home ]. [ ] 9/ as a reward.
