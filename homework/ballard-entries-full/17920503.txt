A Cloudy, Dull Day. I was Calld by Peter Grand to See his wife. they had Calld Dr Parker before I arivd & he Seemd to Chuse to Perform the opperation which took Place at 1h 11#m$ AM. I lift her & infant Cleverly at 2h and arivd Safe at home about 4. Dolly & Sally Densmore here at evening. I receivd 6/ of mr Grand. at mr Grand*s, Pittst#n$, his first Childwas Born. Dr Parker Performd y#e$ Operation.
