Clear & pleast. mr Hadg worked here half y#e$ Day. mr Ballard returnd. Captain Belcher & Esqr Coney from Boston. <STRIKE>I receivd a letter from Brother Edw#d$ Davis informing of his wifes Death. </STRIKE> I was Calld to See mrs Page in travil at 10h & 30m#Evn$. Shee was Deld of a Daughter at about 11, all Cleaverly. I tarried till morn. at mr Page*s. receivd 6/ for my Servis.
