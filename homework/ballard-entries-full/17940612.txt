Clear morn. Augustus Ballard here. I was Calld to See Sally Williams who is not So well as Shee Could wish. I went to See mrs Pitts, mr Capin*s Child & Daughter Pollard. Dolly is there. mrs Ingraham, Sturges, Crag & Burten, Sally & P Densmore here. at Shubael Pitts & other Neighbor*s. John HoustonExpird at Sandy river.
