Cloudy. mrs Densmore Deliv#d$ of a Son at 1 h morn. I left them Cleverly & returnd at y#e$ 8#th$ h. feel much fatagud, have not Slept but little Since Thurs day night. I Came from mr Densmore*s went to Bed & got a little Sleep. was Calld to up to go and See mrs Wall. I went & find her very ill indead. I returnd in y#e$ evening, was much fatagud. my Girls at Hamlin*s. at ditto. 34#th$. at mr Wall*s.
