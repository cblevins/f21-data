Clear foren. I went to the hook, bot a Brass kettle wt 5 lb at 4/ pr lb of Captain Fillebrown. Cloudy afternoon. I went to mr Pollard*s, was at mr Smith*s, Pitts*s, Gill*s and Son Pollard*s. Came home at 5, Sett Down to write to Dr Barton and was Calld off by mr Hodge*s to See mrs Black. Shee was Deld at 7 of a Daughter, her 4#th$ Child; number of Sex Equal. I tarried all night, my Patient was Exercisd with Some pain. at y#e$ hook & fort, atmr Black*s. Birth 22#nt$. raind at night. fee receivdJune 4th.
