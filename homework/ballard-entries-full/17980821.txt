Clear Except a Shower at evening. mrs Brown was Deld of a Daughter, her 11th Child, at 11h 30#m$. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and reacht home before Sun Sett. mr Brown Gave me 6/ Cash and a Chees, w#d$ 7 lb, for my Service. his Son Natl Came for the hors, he Sleeps here this night. at mr Brown*s. Birth 23#d$. I heard, at mr Thomas, that Daughter Town is no better.
