Clear and pleasant. Dolly is quillting a Coat for her Self. I Combd flax foren, was Calld to See mr Partridge*s Child who is very unwell, has a Cough. I was Calld from there to See Savage Bolton*s wife who was in Labour and was Deld at 6h pm of a Son. I tarried there all night, my Patient Cleverly. at mr Partridge*s & Savage Boltons.Birth 12th. receid 1_ Bushl of apples in the fall, not very good.
