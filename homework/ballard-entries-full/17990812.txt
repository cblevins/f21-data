Clear. mr Ballard Sett out for new Sharon, Cyrus to Son Jonathan*s. a Son of Selvanus [Hatch*s] Calld here. Allen Lambard here this day. Hepsy washt and went home with him. we were informd that a Barn of mr Hollowell*s was Burnt by Litning last evening. I have been at home this day but feel some fatagud and did but little work. my Daughter is Cleverly, mrs Pickford allso. at home. mr Ballard Sett out for new Sharon.
