Clear. mr Ballard went to Varsalboro to Survey for mr Webber. he and Watson washt our Sheep before he Set out. Watson went to work for my Son . Daughter Pollard and Jonathan as wife & Jack Came here afternoon. Daughter Ballard and Jack & Ephraim tarried all night. mrs Livermore here foren. I have had a Sick afternoon. at home. mr Ballard gone to Varsalboro. my DagtsPollard & Ballard here, mrs Livermore allso.
