Cloudy, Some lite Showers. I was Calld at 3h yesterday morn to See Bulah at mr Cumings. Shee was deld at 4 of a Daughter. from there I went to mr Joseph Brown*s to See his Lady who was Safe delivd at 2h pm of a Daughter. I tarried till 4#h$ when his Son Conducted me to mr Shepard by water, from there I took passage with Joseph Prescot to mr Daw*s. walkt from there home, feel very much fatagud. I receivd 6/ of mrs Brown. her Husband is gone Down y#e$ river. at mr Cumings. Birth 26#th$. Birth 27#th$.
