Cloudy morn, raind afternoon. I was Calld at Sun rise to See John Coutch*s wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd of a fine Son at 7h 30m morn. I receivd 6/ as a reward & left my Patients very Comfortable, & returnd home at 10. mr Pollard, Pitts, Son Jonathan, their wives, mrs Burten & Cyrus Dind here. we had a fine Loin of Veal & String Beens. mr Ballard was Calld to mr Kennady*s at evening. at John Coutch*s. Birth 34#th$. my Childnhere. Jonathan*s wife & children tarry all night.
