I was Calld at 5 O Clock yesterday morn to Henry Babcock*s, his wife being in Travil. arivd there about Day Light; found her put to bed, the operation performd By mrs Smith. mrs Babcock I found in Severe pain; her complaints So great & Shee very Desireous I should inquire into y#e$ Cause. I Complyd & found her greatly ingered by some misshap. mrs Smith does not alow that Shee was Sencible of it. however her, Viz mrs Babcock, Distres was so very Severe we were apprehencive Shee was Expiring. Sent for Dr Colman But Dr Williams fortunately Came in & prescribed remidies which aford Some relief. I Left him there & returnd home at 10/PM.
