Clear and very hot till afternoon when it raind a little. mrs Bolton and her Son Dind here. Brother Barton, his wife & Brother Flint, Son Town and mr Diar Calld here. Brother B. and wife gone up to Sydna. I have been doing house work, have had a hard pain in my Stomach all day. Daughter Ballard has had Company this day. mr Ballard & Cyrus got in a Load of hay. at home. Brother and Sister Barton &others Cald here.
