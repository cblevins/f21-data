Clear. I have been So unwell that I Could not Sett up till 2 or 3 o Clock afternoon, whin Jonathan as wife Came in and got water for me to bath my feet and made me a dish of Tea. I had not had So much as a drop of water given me to wet my mouth till my husband Came to dine, when he gave me a little which had been in the house 2 days. at home, very unwell. Hepsy left with out SayingGood by.
