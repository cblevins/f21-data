Clear morn. I was Calld by Ephraim Cowan to Charles Cleark*s wife in travil at 2 h morn. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a Daughter (her 3#d$ Child, all Dagts), at ye 4th hour. I left her Cleaverly at 8 & was Conducted to mr Pollard*s. Came to mr Black*s, his wife & Child unwell. I Calld at mr Savage*s, find him mending. arivd at home at 2 h PM. mr Ballard gone to y#e$ hoock. I was at Charles Cleark*s. 36#th$. I gavethis fee Sep#t$ 5#th$ on acount off y#e$infirmity off their Child who has fitts.
