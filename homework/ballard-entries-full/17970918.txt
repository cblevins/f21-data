Clear and pleast. mr Ballard, EbenZ Coal, Enoch Hoiet and a mr Warin Sett out at noon to do a Job of Surveying at Boudenham. I have been at home. Hepsy washt and Scourd Puter. Cyrus went to Son Jonathan*s, he Saw old mr Hamlin who left Oxford a weak Since; my friends all well. then Luce*s Hamlin arivd this Day with his wife. at home.
