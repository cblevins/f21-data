Snowd all day. Daughter Ballard was in Labour, her women Calld after noone. Shee was Safe Deld between 2 & 3 pm of a Son and aboul 3 pm of another Son. her Labour Severe but Shee & infants Seeme likely to do well. the first born wd 5 & _ lb, the last 6 & _ lb. Shee has now been the Mother of 8 Son*s and 4 Daughter*s. at home. Births Son Jonathan*s twins, 8th & 9th for this year. Abram Field*s & wife Sleep here.
