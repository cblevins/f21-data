Clear. I was at home foren Sewing, was Calld afternoon to See mrs Whilwel*s infant who is unwell with a Cold. I applyd a plaster to the Stomach and Onions to the feet and gave it a Syrrip of mullin. it Soon revivd and I returnd home. Calld at Son Lambard*s, they are well. the midle Pierce of the Kenebec Bridg was finisht and there were 7 Discharges of the field piece and 3 Cheirs. at mr Whilwel*s, his infant unwell. mrs Cypher*s &Savage been here.
