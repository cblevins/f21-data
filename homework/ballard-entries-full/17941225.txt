Clear & remarkable pleast for Christmas. I went to Son Pollard*s, little Jonathan Ballard is unwell, the res#t$ of their famelies are well. Dolly & Sally have washt, Scourd my puter & washt the Kitchen. I am informd that the wife of Richard Foster is very Sick of a feavour. at Son Pollard*s. Jonathan Ballardis unwell.
