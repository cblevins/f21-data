A Shoury Day. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to meeting. Ephraim here, informs me his Sister is very ill yet. God grant there may be means directed to for her recovery. I felt fatagud and laid my Self Down to Sleep, but was Calld at 3h pm to See the wife of Captain W#m$ Springer who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of a very fine Daughter at 10h evening and Seems Comfortable. I tarried all night. at Captain William Springer*s. Birth 32#nt$. receivd 9/ as a reward.
