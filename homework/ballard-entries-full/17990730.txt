Clear and very warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus to Jonathan as, Hepsy to mr Hinkley*s. mr Densmore Came and Desired me to go and See mr Beadle*s Son who is Sick. mrs Thomas Came and Staid in my house and I walkt there, found y#e$ patient very Sick. I fear its Complaint is of a putred kind, we administred a Clister, prepaired of Comphry, mallows and mellolott. at mr Beadle*s to See his Sick Child.
