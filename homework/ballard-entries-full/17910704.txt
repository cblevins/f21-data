Clear. I have been at home, finish#t$ my Daughter Town*s Stockins & Began my Self a mittin. Dolly finish#t$ weaving thee Lawn Hankerchiefs. mr Hamlin & Burten, their wives & children, & Hannah Rockwood. mrs Livermore wove her own Hankerchief here. mr Ballard at Esqr Coney*s yesterday morn, helped mr Densmore fraiming & raising his house Fraim. at home, had Company. mr Densmore*s house was raisd.Independance observd.
