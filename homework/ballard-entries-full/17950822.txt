Clear. I Dress#t$ the Childs Burn*s with my Salv and went to See Bizer Benjamin*s Child, who is Sick. Came to Son Jonathan*s & Dind, returnd and Dresst my patients Soars and Came home. Calld at Son Pollard*s, Rhoda is unwell. A mr Miles is hurt by a fall. I Calld to See him & at mr Jones*s. Fanny Cox Came here at evening. at ditto & other Neighbor*s. our menfinisht geting Meddow hay. Fanny Cox here.
