Cloudy morn, Clear after 10h m. I went to See mrs Joy afternoon, find her down in her kitchen. Shee Came out of her Chamber yesterday. Shee informs me that Shee has made her Bed this three Days. her infant is finely. mr Ballard has been to the meddow. mary Densmore and Sally, her Cousin, here at evening. at mr Joy*s, [find] her well. mary Densmore herey#s$ evening.
