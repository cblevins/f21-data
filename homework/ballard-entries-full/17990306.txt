Clear and Cold. I have been very unwell all day, had a severe Pain in my left Ear. I applied Some british Oil and a warm Brick, went onto the Bed and got Easier, but was not able to rise to undress, and Lay in my Cloaths all night. I Could wish to grant better attendance to any one who may fall under my Care. Sally went to Singing and did not Come to see me after Shee returnd. at home, was very unwell.
