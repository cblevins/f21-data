Clear. I left my Patienl Cleverly. Came to Son Pollard*s, find my Daughter Seting up and more Comfortable. I Came home, Drest and went to the meting house to See the ordination of mr Daniel Stone. all things were performd with great regutarity, my famely all attended. I went after Dineing to See mr Densmore*s Daughter Polly who I find very Sick. her Complaint is not much abated. Augustus Ballard Sleeps here this night & Son Cyrus allso. at ditto.
