I was at mr Smith*s till evening when I was Calld to Captain Littlefield*s, walkt and was much fatagud. was Calld from there to mrs Smith again, and Shee was Safe Deld at 10h of her first Daughter and 3#d$ Child and is Cleverly. I tarried there all night, had the Cramp. mrs Cleark & Meloy tarried allso. at mr Smith*s & [Littlefield*s]. Birth [4th].recd 15/ [of] mr Smith June 17, '99.
