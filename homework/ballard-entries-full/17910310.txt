Snow & rain fore#n$, Clear af#tn$. I was Calld to See Lidia White who has had fitts this Day, but had left her before I arivd. Shee Complaind of an Opresion at her Stomach and pain in her head. I left her a portion of Senna and manna and returnd home at 11#h$ 20m Eving. my famely all in Bed when I arivd. at mr White*s to See his Daughter Lidia.
