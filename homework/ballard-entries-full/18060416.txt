Clear and pleast morn. mrs Farewel left here before Breakfast to go to mr Bennet*s. I have finisht mending my green Coat and did other mending, began a Stockin for Son Cyrus. mr Ballard been to mr Partridge*s, Says Mary Rockwood is yet alive. he bot me 2 lb Sugar and 20 rows of pins. at home. raind & Snowd afternoon.
