Clear and not very Cold. Son Ephraim and Samuel Trask worked here this afternoon. mr Ballard been Surveying at mr David Thomas*s. mr Siers of Canaan Sleeps here. I have mended hoes for Cyrus and knit a mittne. Jonathan*s wife and Son Ephraim here. Cyrus went to mr Clap*s and brot up my woolin wheal. Hepsy spun 1 Skein of filling y#n$. at home. Daughter Ballard here. Ephraim Came to work on thee house again.
