Clear & warm. I was Calld at 1#h$ morn to See mrs Capin who was in Labour. Shee was Delivd of a fine Daughter at 2. I tarried there till after Dining, went to mr Brooks*s, mrs Coney & Daughter*s Came there. I Came to mr Capin*s again, rec#d$ 6/. Cyrus Came to mr Page*s Landing & Conducted me over y#e$ river. Ephraim heard that our Hon#d$ Mother Ballard is Deceas#t$. at mr Capans. Birth 23#d$.we had News of ye Death of Mother Ballard.
