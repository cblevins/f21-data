Clear & Cold. mrs Caton was Safe Deld at 3#h$ morn of a Daughter and I returnd home at 10h, receivd 6/ as a reward. I Calld to see mrs Jackson, find her very low. mr Straten & Lady, Son Town, his wife & Dolly Came here at evening. mr James Page took Tea with us. Cyrus been [to] mill. at Caton*s. Birth 9#th$.
