Clear. Ephraim had a fitt at day break, has been very sick till after noone. I was Calld at 3h pm to See mrs Felps who was Deld at 6 of a Son, her third Child. I left her & infant Cleverly & returnd home at 9, find Ephraim more Comfortable. Cyrus been to Pitts t#n$ to mill. at mr Felps*s. Birth 38#th$.my Son Ephraim had a fitt this morn.
