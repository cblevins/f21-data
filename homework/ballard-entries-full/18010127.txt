Clear. mr Ballard at Colonel Duttin*s, Ephraim and Betsy to a Sleigh wride, Cyrus to mill. I washt Boards to Cover milk and washt my ketchin. Esqr Davis Came for me to administer Clisters to John. Colman and Page there, mrs Springer allso. I went again at evening. Oliv Fletcher watched. at my Son*s twice. Betsy gone to Sleigh wride.
