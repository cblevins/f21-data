Clear. mr Town Sett out for home. I was at home foren. Cyrus Came up from Pitts tn. Augustus Ballard was here and Dind. I went to meeting afternoon, was Calld to See two of mr Hamlin*s children who are Sick. Son Pollard has been unwell but is better. I was at mr Black*s, his youngest Child is unwell. at meeting, mr Getchel Performd. I was at mr Hamlins& Black*s. Son Ephraim & Ebenz Cole here.
