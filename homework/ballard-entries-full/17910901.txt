Clear & Pleas#t$. Jonathan is gone to Winslow. I was Calld by Esqr Jewet to See his Lady at 1#h$ pm, arivd at 2. Shee was Safe Deld at 3 of a Daughter, her 8#th$ Child; y#e$ number of Sex are Equal. mrs Dearborn, Broads#t$, Shaw, Barry & Lang attendants. I wrode home alone, arivd at 9 evening. the hors pestyrd me, but I got Safe thro. at Esqr Jewet*s. Birth 23#st$.
