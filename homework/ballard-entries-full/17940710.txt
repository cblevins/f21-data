Clear. mr Ballard & Son*s & James Sturges went to hear Coart. mrs Page went from here before noon. Dolly made a Callico goun for Polly Page and Shee went away to go home with her Mother. Son Jonathan*s wife here. Dolly went with her to Son Pollard*s. mrs Benjamin Sent me 6/ for asisting her in Labour with her last Child, which was Born Jany 13#th$ 1794. at home. Son Town & [P ] Baker & a manfrom Penobscott Sleep here this night.
