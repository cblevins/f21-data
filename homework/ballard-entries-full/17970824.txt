Cloudy foren, Clear afternoon. mr Ballard Surveying for mr Crag. Cyrus been to Pitts#tn$. I have been winding quills I wound 19 Skeins. Thankful wove 8#yds$. Helpsy washt & Scoured my Puter. Hoiet Cutt Bushes. I was Calld at 9#h$ evening to See mrs Joy who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 10 of a Daughter, her 3#d$ Child. I left her Cleverly and returnd home at 1h 30m morn. at mr Samuel Joy*s. Birth 41#st$. receivd9/ as a reward.
