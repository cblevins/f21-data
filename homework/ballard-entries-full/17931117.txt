I returnd home at 8 am, receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard & Ephraim attend worship, Dolly & Sally afternoon. Charles & John Cox Supt here. I was Calld to Captain Meloy*s at 11h evening. raind. at ditto & mr Poor*s. Birth 47#th$. A Daugh#tr$ at Captain Meloy*s allso.
