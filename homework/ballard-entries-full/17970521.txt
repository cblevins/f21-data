rainy morn. I was Calld to See the wife of Captain Samuel Hussey, found her Deld of a fourth Son by the help of mrs Cox. I tarried till about Sun rise when I was Conducted home by water. mr Ballard went and redeemd his watch for one Dollar. he and Hoiet & Polly went to meeting foren, Cyrus & Sally aflern. mr Ballard and I went to mr Dingley*s to See mrs Haden who is not So well as Shee Could wish. Son Jonathan here while we were there. at Captain Hussey*s. Birth23#d$ to which I have been Calld.
