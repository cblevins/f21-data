Clear part of y#e$ day and more moderate. I have done part of my house work, Sorted Potatoes, Clear#d$ my Seller of 3 pails ful of dirt, Sett 30 Cabbage Stumps and removd part of the banking from the house. Daughter Ballard & 3 of her children & Sally Farewel took Tea here. Son Pollard Calld, his Famely are well. I have had a pain in my Stomach. at home. Daughter Ballard here.
