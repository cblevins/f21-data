Clear and Beautiful weather. Son Cyrus Came home, took Breakfast. his Father & he went to meeting, it is a day of Thanksgiving. Allen Lambard Came here, informd [me] his Marm is very unwel and wisht me to go and See her. W#m$ Ballard Came for Safron, Says Samuel is very Sick. mr Ballard Dind at Son [Lambard*s]. at Son Lambard*s. my Daughter is very unwel.mr Swanton & wife Supt there & Spent y#e$Evning.
