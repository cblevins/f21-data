Clear and pleast Day, Some rain at evening. my famely all attended worship at the fort. the Ordinance of the Super Administred. mr Ballard and I went to mr Childs at intermition. we Calld at Son Lambard*s after meeting, the Dear little Babe has the Cough yet, as Severe as Ever. at meeting & mr Childs & Son Lambard*s. Son Ephraim Came and Sleeps here.
