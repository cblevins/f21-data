Clear and pleast. mrs Springer was Deld at 7h morn of a Daughter. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd to mr Gorden*s, find her about. from there I returnd home, find my famely all well. mr Ballard is gone from home. Sally Cox is gone to her Dadys. Shee went yesterdy. at [Captain] [Springer*s] and [Gorden*s]. Birth 13[th].
