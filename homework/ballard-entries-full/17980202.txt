Clear morn. mr Ballard went and borrowed Esqr Davis*s Sleigh and Conducted me to See mr Pattin whome I find very Low indeed. I tarried all night; he had a very restless night. [Juda] Smith Sett up with me to watch. mrs Pattin is as well as Can be Expected. at mr Pattin*s, he is [very] low [indeed]. Old mrHinkley Disceast this evening.
