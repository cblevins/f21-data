Clear & very warm. I was Calld at 8h morn to See Captain Ney*s Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 of a very fine Son. mrs Cox, Cleark, Hinkley, Pillsbury & Sherburn were assistants. we tarried till near Sun Sett when we left my Patients Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is returnd. I went to See mrs Densmore, find her Easier. at Captain Ney*s & mr Densmore*s. Birth33#d$. mrs Ney*s 9#th$ Child.
