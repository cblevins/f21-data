Clear. my Sister & I lefe Son Ephraim*s before 10h morn. Calld at mr Carter*s then proseeded to Captain Shubael Pitts*s. Dind & took Tea. we went into Stores for pins but found none. I Sett out for home, walk as far as Rewel Williams*s when I met Barny with a hors and I wrode home. at Son Ephraim*s, mr Carter*s & Captain Shubael Pitts*s.
