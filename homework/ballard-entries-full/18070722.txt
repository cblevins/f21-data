Clear and very warm. I have felt very feeble bu#t$ have done my house work and wed gardin &C. mrs Wiman had 5 pecks ashes. Shee came and got Curents and Tansey. Daughter Ballard, mrs Pratt & Saunders here. Allen & Barny here, brot me 6_ yds Callico and 2/3#d$ Chang for 2 Dols which my Daughter had of me to purchase it with. at home. mrs Parker here for vinegar. I had otherCompy.
