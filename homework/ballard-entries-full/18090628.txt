Clear and warm. I was Calld by mr Burten at 7#h$ morn, his wife in Labour. Shee was [fe] Deld at 6h 30m pm of her 6th Son & 12th Child. I left her Comfortable and returnd home, did my work up which took me till Eleven O Clock evening. i was quite falagued. at mr Burten*s. Birth 7th. William Sleeps here.
