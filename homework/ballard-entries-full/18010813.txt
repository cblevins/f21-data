Clear foren. I Sett out for home after Breakfast, left my Patients Cleverly. Calld at Son Pollard*s, Lambard*s, they are well. his Father there, Son Pollard allso. his oldest Child had an ill turn las#t$ night but is better. I reacht home at 10h#m$. Brother Lambard Dind here and went on for mr Capin*s. it has raind Since 4h pm. at mr Moore*s. mrs Herington washt here yesterday.
