Clear morn. Dolly was up in y#e$ night pas#t$ a number of times. I gatherd my Safron, helped Do house work foren. Silva Moore look Breakfas#t$ & Dend here. Dolly Seems a little better. mr Hamlin Calld me to See his infant, it is very Sick. I went from there to mr Thwing*s & Kimbal*s. was Calld in has#t$ by mr Pitts, his wife being thought near her Dissolution. I tarried there all night, was in bed from 12#h$ to 4. Shee revivd & rested Some. at mr Hamlin*s, Kimbal*s, Thwing & Pitts*s.
