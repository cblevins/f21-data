Clear. mr Ballard has been giting Hay. Ephraim has been unwell yesterday afternoon, not able to Sett up. his Dad#y$ Complains off a pain in his Back. I applid Burdock Leavs, wiltid in Spirrit. I houghd Gardin foren & raiked hay afternoon. Hannah & Dolly went to mr Burten*s, find his Brother wife & mrs Oliv Hamlin there. mrs Livermore here at evening. at home. Ephraim is unwell.
