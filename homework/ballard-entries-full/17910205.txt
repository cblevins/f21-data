Clear & moderate. I went to See Esqr Coney*s Lady, find her a little more Comfortable than Shee has been; Shee is troubled with Biles and Soar Nipples. I Calld and helped git mrs Welch out of Bed, Shee is as well as Could be Expected. I Calld to See mrs Black, Shee had an ill turn yesterday but is Better. Dolly Seems some better this evening. mr Ballard returnd from wiscasset. at Esqr Coney*s, mrs Welchs& mr Black*s.
