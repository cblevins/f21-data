Clear Except a light Shower. I was Calld at 10h morn to See the wife of John Page who was in Labour, and was Deld at 12 of a Son, her first born. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd between 3 & 4 pm. I Calld to See my Daughter Pollard, find her lame. I Calld to See mrs Brigg*s who is recovering. I combd flax after I returnd, or wound quills. at John Page*s. Birth 29th. receivd 6/ as a reward.
