rainy. I was Calld by Captain Molloy about Day, find his wife very unwell. Shee had Some women Calld at 10h#M$, and Some Came in at intermition. Shee was Deld at 1#h$ pm of a Daughter, her Second Child, both Daughter*s. I tarried till near night when I left Mother and Child Cleverly, and returnd. find my famely well. mr Densmore*s and Livrmrs Daughter*s here at evening. mrs Molloy was unwell all Las#t$ night alltho Shee Did not Call me till morn. at ditto and Captain Molloy*s. Birth 20th.receivd 18/ as A reward.
