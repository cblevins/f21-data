Reverend mr Foster is removd from mr Thwing*s to the house where Joseph Williams formerly livd. friend Taber takeing Spruce Boards from this mill on Sherebiah Town*s acount, yesterday Eving. I wrote to my Daughter Hannah by mr Learned & Deliverd him 24/ to Carrie her to purchace things at Bos#tn$. I have been at home, wrote to my Dag#t$Hannah. Ephraim went to School.
