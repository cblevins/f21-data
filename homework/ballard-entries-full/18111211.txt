Clear & pleasant. I have done house wk & knit Some. the remains of Betsy Bodwel were interd this afternoon. Hannah Ballard attended at the house. mr Goodhue performd. Betsy Wiman Calld & took Tea with us. Barny Lambard brot me 2 aprons which his Marm Sent me, al well there. at home. Funeral of Betey Bodwel.
