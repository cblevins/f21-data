Cloudy morn, Clear afternoon. Sister Williams took Breakfas#t$ here. mr Ballard returnd from his Tour of Surveying at Davis Town. he went to attend Coart directly. mrs Farewel Calld here, I went to my Son*s with her. Son Town Sleeps here, informs me Polly is better. the rest of his Famely are well. at Son Jonathan*s. Son Town Sleeps here. mr Ballardreturnd.
