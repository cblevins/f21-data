Clear weather. mrs Black was Safe Delivrd of a fine Daughter at 5#h$ & 57#m$ morn. I returnd at 6#h$ evening. Sherebiah Town and James Stackpol here, informs that Patty Town had been very unwell, but is mending. the rest of y#e$ famely are well. mrs Hodge had a fitt at her Dadys. at mr Pollard*s. receivd 6/ for Servis Done there.
