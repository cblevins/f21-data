Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus to Jonathan*s. Daughter Lambard Sent Samuel for me. I went and Dind there. went to mr Child*s, find the infant Sick with a Soar mouth. Shee as well as Can be Expected. I returnd to My Daughter*s, Drank Tea, mr Ballard allso. I was Calld in hast by mr Pickford to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 9h evening of a fine Son. we took Tea and I returnd to Son Lambard*s at 11h, went to bed and had Comfortable rest. at Son Lambard*s, Childs & mr Pickford*s. Birth34#th$. recd 12/ as a reward.
