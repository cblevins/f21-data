Cloudy and some rain. Ephraim Conducted his grand pah & I to Captain Pitts*s, we found his famely allmost Sick with Colds. Charles Cox*s wife there. we went from there to Judge North*s to an evening Lecture. Reverend mr Tappin performd. the Text Romans XIII C vers 11#th$. we returnd home before 9h evening. at Judge North*s to Lecture. a mr Dean*s here fromNew Sharon.
