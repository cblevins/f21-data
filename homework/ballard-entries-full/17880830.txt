Clear & warm. I was Calld to Shubael Hinkley*s wife in travil at y#e$ 12#th$ hour. Shee was Safe Deliv#d$ of a very fine Daut at 2#h$ pm. I returnd home at 7, Left Mother & Child Cleverly. found mrs Williams & mrs Hamlin here. Lady Foster has been here also. we Rais#d$ the Saw mill floom. Sherebiah Town Sleeps here. I was at Shubael Hinkley*s. Sherebiah Town Came here.
