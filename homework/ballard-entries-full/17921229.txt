Clear & pleasant. mrs Porter was Safe Deld at 1h morn of a Daughter. I left her at 9, as well as Could be Expected, & returnd home without attendance or Company. Calld at mr Page*s & Son Pollard*s, Son Jonathan and his wife & Son there. Patty at her uncles. I los#t$ a Larg hand kercf as I was on my way to washington. at ditto. Birth 40#th$.
