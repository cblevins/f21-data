Clear, Cool morn, warm afternoon. I have washt Some and houghd Cucumbers & Beens N of ye house. Dolly went from here to Son Jonathan*s. Hannah & Shee to Son Pollard*s. Martha & Sohpa here afternoon. Captain Jason Coney infms Son Lambard is well. at home. mr Ballard houghd Beens, put poles to those in the gardin.
