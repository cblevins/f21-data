I am unwell Still. mrs Brown & her Sister Higen*s here. Isaac Hardin had 1 oz. of Burn Salv, price 1/ which is not pd. my Girls Brueing & Scouring y#e$ puter. the Saw mill has gone Some yesterday day. I was Calld To mr Porter*s wife at half after Seven in Eving. Shee Being in Travil and Put her Safe to Bed with a Son at half after 9/O Clok. I tarried with her all night. fee recievd.
