A Clear morn, Cloudy aftern#n$. I Came home from mr Springer*s. Left them Cleverly arivd at home at 12 O Clok. was Calld at 2 PM to mr Woodward*s to See mr Blackman who is unwell. Came home at 6. the girls went to mr Wesson*s. mr Ballard went to ye hook fore#n$, to Reverend mr Foster*s afternoon. Clarisa Barton here.
