Clear and very warm. mrs Cox was Safe Deld at 2h Morn of a fine Daughter, her first Child. I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd home at 10h. I receivd 6/7 for my Service. I left my medesin there thro forGetfullness. Sally went for them. mr Town Came here, his famely are well. at ditto. Birth 37th. receivd 6/7.Sarah Neal went to work at mr Densmore*s afternoon.
