Clear. we washt. Daughter Lambard helped and I washt Some my Self. mr Ballard is gone to Sidney to Survey for mr Richardson. Old mrs Hamlin, Burten & Crag here afternoon. Rusha Capin, Polly Hodge*s, Suky Haywad & Oliv, Rhoda & Hannah Pollard & Patty Livermore & Polly Smith, Son*s Lambard & Ballard been here & mr Soal. Jonathan a had his Shais and Carried mrs Town as far as Son Pollard*s. Shee was not much fatagud. at home, we had Company.
