Clear part of the day. I am very Lambe bu#t$ wrode to Isaac Savage*s hopeing to receiv my Due of him, but did not receiv a Cent. I Calld at Jack Shaw*s, receivd nothing there. I bathed my Lame knee with Vinagar, went to bed but could not rest. got up and lay before fire all night. Jonathan Junr Sleeps [here]. at Isaac Savage*s & Jack Shaw*s.
