Clear and warm. Son and Daughter Lambard Came here foren#n$. he went down to the Settlement to Observ independance, his wife and I went to See Son Jonathan*s wife. I have felt very unwell. I Came home after Sun Sett. Sister Pollard Calld at our door on her way home. Brother Lambard here, took Tea and then Sett out to acompany my Daughter home. may they be prospered on their way and reach their place of destination in Safty, and may my Son, who is Confined from his famely, be returnd in Safety in thine own time. giv him Seutable Contemplations [at] thy Divine disposal and may he from this time devote himself to thy Service. at Son Jonathan*s. mrs Saunders there.
