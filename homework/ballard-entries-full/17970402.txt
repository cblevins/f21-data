Clear. mrs Brigg*s Seems more Comfortable, Son Cyrus Came there to Call me home as Son Town was after me to go and See my Daughter. I returnd home and Sett out for Son Town*s between 9 & 10h morn, we reacht there about 5 pm, find his famely well as usual. at ditto mr Capin*s & Son Town*s. mrs Copeland Deld of a Daughter this evening by Piohon.
