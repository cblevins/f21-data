Son Lambard, his wife and Son Pollard, Jonathan, his wife & children and mrs Getchel Dind here after meeting. we had string Beens and green Pea*s. I feel not so well at evening. Son Pollard Conducted his wife home, their children are not so well as usual. it is Expected they are about haveing the measels. at home. my [children] here to Dine, and 7 Grand children.
