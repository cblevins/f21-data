Clear and Cool. I was at mr Gorden*s, his wife was Deld (at 4#h$ 30#m$ morn) of a Daughter. I left her & infant as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 11#h$ fore#n$. I receivd [3] lb Cottne @ 2/4 pr lb. I Dind and went to See mrs Black, find her better y#n$ my fears. I Calld to See mrs Smith and mrs Capin and at Son Pollard*s. at ditto. Birth 14th. Calld to Thomas Kenny *s at 11h evening.
