Clear morn. I was Calld to See Sally Williams who is very Sick. Dr Colman left her medicin. I was Calld from There to See James Wesson; he remains weak. Came back; tarried all night & watch#t$. Timothy Page*s wife there all day. Patty Seems a little Better. raind in y#e$ Evinn#g$. I watcht with Sally Williams. I heard they were well at Son Town*s.
