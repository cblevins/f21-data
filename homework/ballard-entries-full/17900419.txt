Snowd by Showers all day. I have been at home. my Girls washt, I Began me a pair of Stockins & mended Foster a pair. mr Pollard informd me of y#e$ Death of the Widdow Sherburn*s Daughter & only Child. I allso hear that old mr Smith is very Sick. Reverend Isaac Foster*s 1#st$ Daughter born thisnight.
