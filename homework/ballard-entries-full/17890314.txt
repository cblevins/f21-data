Clear & Cold. the infant Expired at 5#h$ 30M Morn. I put on the grave Cloaths, went to mr Hamlin*s and then returnd. Augustus Ballard, Ephraim Cowan, his wife & Lefe Savage, & mr Soal of winslow Dind here. the Latter informs that my friends at Oxford were all well y#e$ 23 ult. I was at mr Andrews*s, his infant Expird.We receivd News from Oxford.
