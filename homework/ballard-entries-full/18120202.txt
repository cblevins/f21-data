Cloudy, raind at evening. I was Calld by Levy Cowan to go and See his wife in Labour. Shee was Safe deld at 4h am of a Son which Expired at 5. Shee had a fall not Long Since which probably was the Cause. Hannah & Nabby wint to hear mr Tappin this day & evening. at Levy Cowan*s. Birth 3#d$. Death ofthe infant. Death of [Lydae] Springer of Sidney.
