Cloudy, Snowd a little yesterday morn. I left mr Goodin*s at 11h morn, my Patient been up and had her bed made and Seemd Cleverly. Watson returnd from Sidney last night. Cyrus is Breaking flax, he began yesterday. mr Ballard is gone to mill. Allen Lambard Sleeps here, his Dad g#n$ to Son Pollard*s. at mr Goodin*s. mr Ballard receivd 5 Bushels Cornfor Surveying a road in Sidney.
