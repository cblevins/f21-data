Cloudy. I was Calld up at 2h 30m. my Daughter was ill, her women Calld in and Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30m yesterday morn of her 7#th$ Son and 10 Child. Shee Seems as well as we Could Expect and I Came [home] about 10 hours yesterday morn. at Son Jonathan*s. Birth 8th. Shee had an ill turn afternoon. I went and tarried at night.
