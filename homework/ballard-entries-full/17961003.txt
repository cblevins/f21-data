Clear Part of the Day, rain at night. I have Swept my Chambers & lower rooms, & Done other matters. Pally mended me a quillt. I was Calld to See the wife of Anthony Bracket at 9h evening, was fatagud with my hors & the bad roads. Shee was Deld of a Son at 11. I tarried all night, Mother and infant are as well as Can be Expected. at Anthony Bracket*s.
