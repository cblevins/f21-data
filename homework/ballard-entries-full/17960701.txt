Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Dingley this morn, find her very unwell. her Throat is Soar and Shee puk#t$ blood. I Sent her Some honey. I attended Lecter after noon. was to See David Pollard*s Child, it has been very Sick. Daughter Pollard*s infant is very unwell. Daughter Lambard was there. I am informd that Ruth Lee Expird this morn at 10h. at mr Dingley*s and at meeting.Rev#d$ mr Farrer performd. mr Gillet made the firs#t$ prayer. Death mrs Lee.
