Clear. I was Calld by Isaac Hardin at 4 h morn, his wife Delivd of a Daughter at 1 h pm. I went Directly from there to James Savage*s, my hors mrd in a Swamp and I fell off. arivd at Savage*s at 3, find her Safely Deld of a fine Son by his marms assistance. were fresh#t$ and went back to Hardin*s by Colonel Duttin*s farm. I wrode Hardin*s hors home, arivd at 8 very much fatagud. at mr Hardin*s & James Savage*s. Birth 2[1]#th$.Birth James Savage*s Son. Birth 22.
