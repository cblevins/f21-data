Clear morn. I Came home from Hardin*s at 1 OClok, was iminediately Calld to See mrs Wesson, Shee being very unwell. I aply#d$ a Blister, Batht her feet, put on a Back Plaster, returnd home at Evinng. Left her a Little Easyer. left mrs Hardin Comfortable. Shee was helped up & her Bed made. my girls helped mrs Pollard quilt Bed quilt. Lidia Bisbe was here after noone.
