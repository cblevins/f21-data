Cloudy, Snowd at evening. I have been to my Son*s to See Patty, Shee fell out of her Brother*s arms & hur#t$ her face. I have finished futing mr Ballard*s hoes and began to fu#t$ A pair for my Self. Nabby and I pickt up the Chips at the door. mr Ballard to Son Lambard*s. Dr Barton here, his famely well. Eliab Shaw*s wife & mrs Braley Calld here, Sarah Cleark allso. at my Son*s. A Suden Death at Hollowellthis day, a mr [Johnson].
