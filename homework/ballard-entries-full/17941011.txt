Clear, Serene Day. I was Calld to 4h morn to See Nabby Towers who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 11#h$ 30#m$ of a Son which Shee DeClard that Moses*s Palmar was the Father of. I tarried & Dind. Esqr Haywad there. I arivd at home at 3#h$ pm, am fatagud. Dolly is unwell, has a Blister which lames her very much. at mr Lathrop. Birth 40th. receivd my fee Oct.2nd by mr Daw at the hook.
