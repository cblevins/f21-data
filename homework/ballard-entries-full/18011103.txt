Snowd Some [ ]. mr Lambard Dind with me, mrs Cypher allso. Joseph Davis and Joseph Rockwood of OXford here. Son Town Sleeps here. Lucy is no better. I feel feeble my self. Son & Daughter Pollard Calld as they were going home. mrs Black is better. at home. my friends Calld on me. I paid mrs Cypher1/10_ for Sally*s work.
