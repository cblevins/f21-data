Cloudy and Some rain. I have been very unwell but being Calld by mr James Kenny  at 4h pm, I went to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30#m$ of her Second Daughter & 4#th$ Child. I left her and infant very Comfortable and arivd at home at 10#h$ 30#m$ evening. at mr James Kenny *s. Birth 11#th$.receivd 6/ for my Service.
