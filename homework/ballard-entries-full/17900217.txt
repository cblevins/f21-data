Cloudy. Foster & Ephraim are gone to the meddow. mr Bullin & wife & Betsy Brown, Williams Haywad, Elisha Bisbe & his Sister*s Dind here. Gillbreath helping Cyrus. Hamlin & Burten & their wives Drank Tea here. my Daughter*s went to mr Wesson*s. I have been at home till 11#h$ evening. Dr Williams Sleep#s$ here. I was Calld to See Ben#n$ Prescot*s wife at Ben#n$ [Prescot*s].
