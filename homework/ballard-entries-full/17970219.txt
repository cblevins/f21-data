Clear and pleast Day, Cloudy at evening. I was Calld to See mrs Pollard at 4h mor#n$, Shee was Deld at 5 of a Daughter, her forth Child. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd at 7. my Daughter*s and their husbands, Ezra & Ephraim, mrs Eunice & Lucy Town Dind here. Jonathan Ballard allso, the 2 latter Sleep here. at Leut David Pollard*s. Birth 11th. October 6#th$ I recvd 9/ of mrs Pollard as a reward.
