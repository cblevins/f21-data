Clear and very pleas#t$. Son & Daughter Town & Parthenia returnd at evening. I was Calld to Samuel Cumings*s to his wife in Labour. Shee was Delivd of a Daughter at 1#h$ 30m, about 5 m before my arival. his parents with her & no one Else. mrs Brigg*s and hamlin there after her Delivery; tarried thro y#e$ night, my patient Exercised with much pain. at Samuel Cumings. J. Kenny  here for me to go & See his wife.
