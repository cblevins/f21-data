Clear & very warm. I was Calld at 7#h$ 30#m$ by mr Haws to see mrs Daw*s who was in Labour, and was Deld at 9#h$ evening of a fine Son, her fifth Child, all males. I tarried all night, mrs Cox allso. my Patients as well as Could be Expected. mr Daw*s Sett out for Long reach this morn. mrs Ney, Greely, Palmar, Sherbur#n$ & mrs Cox assistants. at mr Daw*s. Birth 24th.
