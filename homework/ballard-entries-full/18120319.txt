Clear and Cold. mrs Norcross was Deld at 5h am of a Son, her first Born. I left her and Child comfortable and arivd at home at 10 morn, receivd two Dollars in Sugar, Tea & Spirit. Son Jonathan*s youngest infant is feeble. Hannah & Sally Came from Clinton. Son & Daughter Lambard here at evening. at ditto. Birth 10th. receivd 12/.
