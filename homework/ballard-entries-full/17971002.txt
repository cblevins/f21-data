Cloudy morn & rain. mrs Densmore was Safe Deld at 4h 30m Morn of her 9th Daughter and twelvth Child, and is Cleverly. I larried with her till the 11 [haur] lefet her and infant as well as Could be Expected. I receivd 6/ as a reward for my Service. mr Ballard is gone to Son Jonathan*s, Cyrus to Pittst#n$, Hoi#t$ Carting Bark for feuell. at mr Densmore*s, 9th Daughter & 12 Child. recd 6/ Cash. Birth 48#th$ for yesterday year, I allsorecd a Beef heart.
