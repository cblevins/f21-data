Clear. we had men giting wood. I was Calld to See mrs Babcock, find find [sic] her very ill. was Calld at evening by mr Perkins to see his wife in Labour. Shee was delivd before 10h evening of her 2nd Child & first Daughter. I tarried all night. at mr [Babcock*s] & Perkins. Birth 2nd. recd9/ as a rewd.
