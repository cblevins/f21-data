Cloudy part of the day. I was Calld at 10h am by Edw#d$ Savage to go and See his wife who was in Labour. I had a fall on my way but not much hurt. found the patiend had Calld 2 Mid wives & Dr Ellis before Shee Saw me. I found her mind was for Dr Coney. he was Calld and as Providence would have Shee Calld on me to assist her. I performd the Case. Shee was Deld at 8#h$ 30m pm of a Son and is as well as can be expected. I Slept at mr Jerry Babcock*s, had an aguish turn in the night. at Edw#d$ Savage*s. Birth 11th.
