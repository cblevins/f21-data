Clear and Cold. mr Linsa Foss*s, mr Millet & wife Calld here. I went to our house and brot a Plan of Livermore. mr Ballard lent it to mr Foss*s. W#m$ Ballard very unwell at night, we gave him medisin for his Cough, he Slep#t$ with Jonathan. I have Seen 77 years this day. may I render prais to the Great author of mercies for all his Goodness to me in my pas#t$ life, and obtain his forgivness for all Sins Committed. at home & to our own house. People from Leadshere. I have Seen 77 years this day.
