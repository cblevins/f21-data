Clear, warm and Spring like. I have worked in my gardin, planted Long Squash by y#e$ hogg pen, Sowd pepper grass, Sett Sage and other roots. mr Ballard mode Bush & dugg gardin. I washt his & Cyrus*s old over Coats, my gound & other things. Son Jonathan had 6 OXen taken by Execution and drove a way. I do feel for but am not able to help hin. Ephraim & W#m$ Sleep here. at home. Son Town left here yesterday morn.mr Ballard is 84 years yesterday day. death mr Hains.
