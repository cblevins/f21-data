Clear & warm. I returnd home foren, left mrs Hodgkins a little revivd. I Calld at mr Densmore*s to See Thomas who is very unwell. find Dolly as well as Shee generally is. I went to mr Livermore*s, find his Lady unwell. mr Dummer & Captain Hussey & Jewet Foster here. mr Ballard & Son*s Sett out to fence the Meddow at 3 h PM. Cyrus Came home. Nabby Hodsk#n$ no better. at ditto & other Neig#rs$. mr Ballard gone to fence his Meddow.
