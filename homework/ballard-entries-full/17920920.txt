Cloudy morn. my Daughter Ballard Sett out for home, went as far as mr Densmore*s, was informd that her Husband had Bea#t$ mr Andrews last evening. Shee Came back here and mr Ballard went to mr Norcross*s, finds it is no#t$ So Bad as is repreSented. Jonathan Came here & went to See him. Sally Sett out again, arivd Safe at her own home. Ephraim went with her & wrode the hors Back. mr Shubael Pitts & Jonathan Dind here. I have been at home. at home.
