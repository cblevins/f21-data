I Crost the river on foot from Colonel Howard*s to mr Burten*s, Came to mr Pollard*s, was Conducted from there home by mr Whiten in a Sleigh. arivd at home at 2h pm. Phillip Bullin here makeing a plan, he tarries all night. I was Calld at 8h evening to See mrs Moore who is in Labour. at Colonel Sewall*s. Cros#t$ the river ony#e$ ice. at mr Moore*s.
