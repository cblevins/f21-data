Clear and Cold. mr Livermore Came to Foy*s & Conducted me home, his wife Came up with him and went to Dr Howard*s. I left Sally better than Shee was but not got to bed. lhe Boys brot my apples from our house, I Cutt & Stued parl of them, they are frozen. at ditto & mr Livermore*s, he and wife Camehome with me.
