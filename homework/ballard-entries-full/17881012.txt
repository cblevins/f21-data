Clear & Pleast. I attended public wors#p$, Reverend mr Foster Discoarst from GeneS#S$ 33#d$ & 4#th$. mr Gille went from here this morn. my Girls went to mr Childs at evening. Ellis Crag & Hannah McKethney Cryed. I was Calld in haste at 11#h$ evening to mr Isaac Cleark*s Lady in travil. Shee was Safe Delivd of a fine Son & a Daughter before 12; all likely to Do well. I receivd 12/ as a reward. at meeting & at mr Isaac Cleark*s.
