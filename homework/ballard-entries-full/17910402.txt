Clear morn. I Took Breakfast at mr Wesson*s and went to mr Hamlin*s & Burten*s. Dind with mrs Hamlin. Lucy Cassety went from Captain Burgess this Day. I Came from Hamlin*s, Calld to see mrs Childs and mrs Pollard, then prosceded homeward. met mr Ballard who Conducted me home tho thro Some difuculty. I find my family as well as usual and that Jonathan has Securd his Boards. rain & high wind in y#e$ night. at mr Wesson*s & other Neig#rs$.
