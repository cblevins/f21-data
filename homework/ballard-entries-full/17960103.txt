I Came home at 11#h$. baked and Cleand my house and Did other matters, was Calld at 7#h$ evening to See the wife of Eliab Shaw who was very ill when I arivd, and was Deld at 9 of a Dead Son. it appeard to have been Dead for Some time, the Skin all mos#t$ Came off. the Mother is Comfortable as Can be Expected. at ditto and at Eliab Shaw*s. Birth 1st for y#e$ year. recd 6/ of Deckman in Snuffon Shaw*s acount.
