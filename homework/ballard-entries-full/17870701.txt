Clear. we had ice an intch thik in our yard South Side of y#e$ house this morn. I attended public worship; a Sermon preacht on y#e$ Death of Lady Howard*s Daughter. Pamela out publisht. mr Porter here. Isaac Leighton Came here to work fors us the ensueing week. this is the Day apointed for Brother Collins Moore*s Joining in wedlok with mrs Betsy Chamberlain of winchester. may their Connections be for their happyness.
