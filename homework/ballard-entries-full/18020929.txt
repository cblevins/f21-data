Clear. I was at mr Gill*s, the Child is viry low. Old Lady Hamlin, mrs Camble & Moore Came there to See me. Old Lady Gill Came there and I left there afler diner. Calld at Son Lambard*s, Thomas is Sick with the measels; from there I Came to mr Burten*s, took Tea with friends who Consisted of mrs Hamlin, Crag, Camble, Moore*s, Carter & 2 mrs Burten*s, & returnd home. Calld at my Son*s, his wife is better. L#e$ faett & W#m$ are Sick. Sister Barton, mr Porter & wife Sleep here. at mr Gill*s & other friends.
