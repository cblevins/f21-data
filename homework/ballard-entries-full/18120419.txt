Cloudy morn, raind afternoon. I have felt very feeble. mrs Stone Came in to See me, Shee brot wood to the doore. Shee informd me that Williams Brooks*s wife fell from the Scaffold of her Father*s Barn yesterday and hurt her So her life is not Expected. Nancy Smith here, Says Shee is not any better. I was Calld by W#m$ Saunders at 4h pm, his wife was Deld at 10 evening of her 3#d$ Daughter & 4th Child and is Comfortable. I laid down & Slept Some. at William Saunders. Birth 14.
