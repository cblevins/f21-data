Clear. mrs Cottle was Safe Deld at 11h morn of her 15th Child & 6th Son. I left her Comfortable and returnd home. I Calld at Son Lambard*s, find my Daughter very unwell. [Daughter] Pollard was with her. mrs Chamberlain Calld me in to See her Child which Seems very low. I have spent from may 24#th$ till this day at mr Cottle*s. I find my house in desent order at my arival and that my famely are all well. at mr Cottle*s. Birth 12th. recd 1 Bus#l$Corn, _#do$ rie, 1 Crown. W#m$ HewenExpired.
