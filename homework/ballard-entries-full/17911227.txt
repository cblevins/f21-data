Clear & very Pleas#t$. mrs Stackpol went home. I went to my Son Town*s, tarried about an hour, returnd to the Esqr*s. Sett out from there at 1#h$ pm, arivd at home at 8 evening. I left my Patients Cleverly, find my famely So. Esqr Lithgow made me a reward of 24/ & a Callico goun patron & 1 y#d$ Cloth Linning. returnd home. was at Town*s, all well there. mrsStrattne there.
