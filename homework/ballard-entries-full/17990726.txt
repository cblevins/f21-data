Clear and very warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus at Jonathan as. I was Calld to See mrs Martin at Hollowell at 10h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 4h 30#m$ pm of her Second Child and Daughter. I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd home at 7. mr Martin gave me 12/ for my Service, he is 33 years old this Day. my assistants were Captain Ney*s Lady and mrs Smith and Molloy. mrs Beeman did not Come in till after the Birth. the Phraim of mr Gillet*s house raisd this afternoon. at mr Martin*s, Hollowell. Birth 29#th$.recd 12/ as a rewd. I gatherd Cucumbers, & Sent to DagtLambds.
