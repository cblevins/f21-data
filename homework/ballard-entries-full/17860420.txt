A Clear day. the Girls washing. mrs Chamberlain & George Bolton*s wife here & Drank Tea. mr Hodge*s work#d$ here. mr Ballard at Dr Coney*s yesterday evening. Daniel went to the hook, Bro#t$ home 6 gal#ns$ of Rhum, 2 lb coffee, 5 lb Sugar & Some Tobacco & 1-1/4 Bush#l$ of Salt from Joseph Williams for me, for assisting his wife in travil with her Last Child. I have Been at home yesterday day.
