Cloudy. mrs Hinkley was Safe Deld of a Daughter, her first Born, at 6h morn. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected after Breakfast. receivd 9/ for my own Service & 1/6 for the hors. I went to bed and had Some Sleep, was Calld about noon to See mrs Byerns. it raind and I had great falague in geting there, find her very unwell. her women were there but She got more Comfortable and they all retird to their own homes. at mr Hinkley*s & mrs Burnss. Birth 50th. receivd 9/ as fee & 1/6 for hors.
