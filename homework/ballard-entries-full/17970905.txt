Clear Except Some lig#t$ Showers. I was Calld at 3#h$ morn to go to See the wife of Andrew Goodin who is in Labour. I arivd there at 4. Shee was Safe Deld at 12 & 40#m$ of a fine Daughter. I left her and infant Cleverly at 4h pm. mr Goodin bestowd 12/ as a reward. I find mr Ballard is gone to Dresden. Hepsy has been washing. EbenZ Hail Esqr Sleeps here yesterday night. at mr Andrew Goodin*s. Birth44th. recd 12/ as a reward.
