I Came home attendid by mr Moore. Receivd my fee & pay for medecin. (arivd at 4/ yesterday morn). mr Ballard at Dr Coney*s makeing Rates yester#d$ & yesterday day. Jonathan found the Loggs & Came home Last Evinn#g$. Sherebiah Town Slept here. Polly Hamlin been here yesterday day, Eliphlt unwell. the mill Lies still yesterday night.
