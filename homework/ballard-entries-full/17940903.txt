Clear & very pleas#t$. Dolly went to Son Pollard*s, Cyrus to Varsalboro. mrs Livermore was here. mr Pitts & mrs Barton Came home with Dolly. Sally Hamlin & Charles Cox took Tea with us. mr Robbins Calld me to See his wife who is not So well as Shee Could wish. Cyrus returnd home. I wrote to Dr ["Barton." - implied], Sent it by mr Crag. he and his wife went on board this afternoon. I gave mrs Robbins Some Derections & relurnd. at mr Robbins. wrote to Dr Barton.
