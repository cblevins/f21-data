Clear. I took Breakfast at mr Hamlin*s. Calld in to See my patient, find her as well as Could be Expected. was Conducted home in a Shais, arivd at 8h 30#m$. Pally Town & I went to Son Jonathan*s to. I saw M. [Purington*s] wounds Dresst. Sally Farewel, mr [&] mrs Eadds*s here for fruit. at ditto & mr Hamlin*s & Son Jonathan*s. JamesP. & his Cousin Sleep here.
