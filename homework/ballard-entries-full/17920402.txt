Clear & pleast. this Town met to Vote for Govr#s$, Sennate#r$, &C, I went to See mrs Livermore, Shee is a little Easier. mr Densmore, his wife, two Daughter*s & my Daughter here. the Ice broke up & the river open allmost acros#t$, against our houses. it parted so that people Crost in boats at y#e$ fort. at mr Livermore*s. we had Company.
