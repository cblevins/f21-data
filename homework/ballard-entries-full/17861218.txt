Clear & pleas#t$. I am free from the Collic pain. we are informd of Jack Howard*s Death. I went to attend funeral, but by reason of Esqr Howard*s being taken very ill, I was Calld in there & tarried with them till about 9 O Clok when Cyrus came there; informd that Ephraim was in a fitt. I returnd home in great hast Led by Cyrus & York. found my Little Son revivd. Left y#e$ Esqr more Comfortable, Betsy also.
