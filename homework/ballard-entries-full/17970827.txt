A very rainy Day till near night. mr Ballard attended worship all day. Cyrus & Hoiet afternoon. Son Ephraim Dind with us, we Dind on roast Lamb. mr Ballard and I went up to Son Pollards,his wife & children are unwell. I Should have tarried all night had I not Expected I might be wanted to attend Some one in greater distress. Shubael Pitts*s wife came in to See my Daughter. at Son Pollard*s and Lambard*s. my Daughter Pollard isunwell. it raind very powerfully in the night.
