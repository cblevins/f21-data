Cloudy, rain at evening. I was Calld this afternoon to See the younges#t$ Child of mr Thwing, who has a Sweling on the left Side of the neck and Seems Some feavourish. I Calld at Son Pollard*s, they are well as usual; at Son Lambard, my Daughter is gone to Sidney. Cyrus Came and Conducted me home on hors back. at mr Thwing*s to See his younges#t$ Child.
