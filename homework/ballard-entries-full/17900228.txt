Clear & pleasant. mrs Babcock Calld mrs Bisbe at 1#h$ pm & was Safe Delivrd at 4 of a Daughter, her fifth Child; the first a Son. I Left her & infant Cleverly at y#e$ 6th hour. arivd at y#e$ 7th. my Daughter*s gone to mr Hamlin*s. Jonathan a is gone to See Luke Barton*s; returnd at evening. informs that he is no better. at ditto. Birth 10th.
