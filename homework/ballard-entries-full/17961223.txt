Clear. I made my Self ready to Come home at 9h morn when the infant apeard to be unwell. I tarried till 4 pm when it Seemd revivd and I left them. the Mother as well as Could be Expected, and Came home. find my famely as well as usual. Phillip Bullin here when I Came home. mr Ballard gone to Esqr Coney*s. at ditto.
