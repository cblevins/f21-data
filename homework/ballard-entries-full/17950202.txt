Snowd. I was Calld from Jonathan*s to See Black Hitty. Shee was Deld of a Son before I arivd, found her Exercisd with pain, administred Something for her relief. returnd home by way of mr Hussey*s, all as well there as usual. I went to See my Daughter Ballard after I Came home, find her & infant Cleverly. Dolly is gone to work at Old mr Pollard*s. Sally Cox gave me a Snuff Box. at my Son*s and Nicholas*s. Birth 7#th$.this man is a Portugues who was Brot here by mrs Hussey from Nantucket.
