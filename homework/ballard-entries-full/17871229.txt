Cloudy & Some Snow. mr Ballard went to the hook. we receivd a Letter from our Friend Hains Learned of the 19#th$ inst, informing us of the Death of my honoured Mother, who Departed this Life after a Short illness y#e$ 4#th$ ins#t$; her Decease was the Palsy. Shee was Aged 72 Years the 27th of July last, Old Stile. he also informs that my Sister Deborah was Safe Delivrd of a Son & that my other friends were in health, and of an acsident by fire which hapend on the Night of y#e$ 5#th$ inst in OXbridg; a house Consumd with four persons, 2 aged & 2 children. News of the Death of My Mother.
