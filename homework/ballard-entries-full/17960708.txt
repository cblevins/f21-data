Clear and warm. I wed gardin in y#e$ morn. went to See Daughter Lambard, Shee is unwell bu#t$ wrode my hors to Son Pollard*s. I went there, was Calld by mr Kitchredg to See his wife who is in Labour. her women were Calld at 8h evening, Shee was Deld at 10 of a Son, her first born. mr Kimbal was there after me. I Could not leave my Patient and he returnd with out me. at Son*s Lambard*s & Pollard*s &mr Kitchredg*s. Birth 35#th$.
