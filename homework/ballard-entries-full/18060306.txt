Clear and Cold. I have washt Some and done other work, brot 7 pails water. feel not So well at evening as I could wish. we hear that mr Wiman & Son Jonathan were Calld to Captain Stone*s yesterday morn abou#t$ day, it was thought he was Expireing. hear afternoon he was alive. at home, wash#t$ Some. mrs Heartwel Stme [1 lb Buller].
