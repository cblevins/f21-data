a very rainy foren, it held up at noon and mr Ballard Sett out for home, and I tarried at my Son*s. Captain Stackpol & wife, mr Fuller and his, there afternoon. my Daughter was taken with fainting and Distress in her limbs. Shee and we thought her to be Expireing, but Shee revivd at evening and gave good Exhortation to her children. mrs Stackpol and I watcht with her. at my Son Town*s. my Daughter had an illturn.
