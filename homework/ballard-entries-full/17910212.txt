Clear. I was Calld by mr Crane to go to See mrs Pond of winthrop, went as far as mr Samuel Cumings*s, was there informd the Lady was safe Delivrd by mrs Whiting, of a Daughter. I returnd home at y#e$ 11 h morn attended by mr Eastman. I went to mr Pollard*s & mr Wesson*s afternoon. Leut Hains Learned Sleeps here. I bot 1#lb$ Tamerins of mr Baxter & a pinte of wine of mr Wesson. at mr Cumings*s, Pollard*s & mr Wesson*s. Leut Learned here.
