Clear. mr Ballard Laying ou#t$ a road Eas#t$ Side the river. I have been much fatagued with the Swine. was Calld at the 7#th$ hour to go and See Nabby Andrews, who was very Sick. I tarried all night, her aunts Pain and Springer, and her Daughter Nabby tarried all night. there were many people Calld to See the Patient. Shee gave them all good Admonition, took her leav of them Expecting to Expire Soon. at mr Andreus#s$. Nabby very Sick indead.
