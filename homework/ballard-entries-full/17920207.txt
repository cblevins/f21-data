Clear & Pleas#t$. I went to mr Densmore*s. Captain Savage*s Lady Came here & Sent for me home. Cyrus Dind here & Conducted mrs Savage to the hook in his Sleigh. my Son*s are gone to y#e$ meddow. Savage Bolton brot a Load of hay, informs me his infant is unwell. John Savage went with one teem. at mr Densmore*s. mrs Savage here, &Cyrus.
