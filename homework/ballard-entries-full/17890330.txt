Clear & pleast. I was Calld at 2#h$ morn to See mrs Arnold at y#e$ hook. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a fine Son, that wd 10 lb 2 oz, at y#e$ 4 h. I returnd 8. Left all Cleverly, receivd 6/ as a reward. mr George Brown informd me that Captain Smith*s wife had 2 children Born Last night, they are dead. I was Calld at mid night to See mrs Hamlin, found her very ill. I wrode the hors which Hannah Hadskin*s had here. at mr Arnold*s. Hannah Hadskin Sleeps here. Snowsys evening. Ephraim is 10 years old. at mr Hamlin*s.
