Clear. I have been at home. Daughter Ballard and Jason Pierce*s wife Dind here. I gatherd String Beens, abou#t$ 3 pints. I was Calld out of Bed at 10h evening to go and See mr Pillsbury*s Child, its Complaint was obstruction of urin. I recommended giving a little Niter which relievd the Patient. at mr Pillsbury*s in ye night.
