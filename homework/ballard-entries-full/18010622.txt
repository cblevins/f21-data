Cloudy foren, Clear afternoon. the wife of mr Robert Fletcher and two of her Daughter*s, Daughter Ballard, 2 of children, her Sister Hitty & Sally Farewel took Tea here. mr Ballard been to See mr Robinson*s house raised.
