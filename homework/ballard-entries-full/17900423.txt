Clear & pleasant. I have been at home. mr Dexter here; informs that Biah Town is wasting fast, that Nathan Haywad is mending & that a woman in their new Plantation Expired in Child bed last Lords Day, & Son Town*s famely are well. mr Shubael Pitts here this night. Elisha Mathews had a Son Drowned at his Logg Landing as they were raffting Loggs. at home. a Death; Mathews*s Son Drownd.
