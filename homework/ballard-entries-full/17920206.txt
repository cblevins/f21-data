Clear & Some windy. I returnd from mr Williams after Breakfast, find mr Ballard is gone to Fairfield*s back of Pitts#tn$, & Ephraim to mill with his teem for our Selva*s & mr Densmore*s & Livermore; got home at 9#h$ evening. mr Ben#n$ Porter & Son & Judy Heuston here. I wrote to Dr Barton. at mr Williams. Judy Heuston Ben#n$Porter here.
