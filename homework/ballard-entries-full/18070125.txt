Clear [&] Cold. Son Cyrus Came home and went to meeting. I have baked bread and Pies, was Calld at 2h pm to go and See the wife of Anthina Bracket who was in Labour. her illness Came on while at the house of worship. Shee was Safe Deliverd at 6h evening of her 2#nd$ Daughter and 7th Child. I left them as well as Could be Expected, arivd at home at 11h 30#m$. at a. Bracket*s. Birth 2nd.
