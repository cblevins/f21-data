Clear. I was home fore#n$. Roasted a Line of veal. I went to mr Williams at one o Clok. Sally is no Better. mrs Williams very poorly; not one of the famely well. I was Calld to mr Wesson*s to See James who Continues very Low. mrs North there. & mrs Densmore, Anne Web Came to mr Williams & watcht. My Girls went to mr Crag*s. Son Town is here. I have not Seen him as my famely were all in bed at my return. Son Town Sleeps here. Captain Porter hadfitts Last night. I was at mr Williams*s.
