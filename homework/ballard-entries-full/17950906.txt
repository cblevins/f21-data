Clear. I left mrs Moore Some more Comfortable, walkt to Colonel Duttin*s farm, wrode mr Moore*s hors 2 miles then wrode with Eunice*s Cottle allmost to mr Dana*s. Calld there, took Tea. her children, the Twins, have got the itch or Some other humir. I walkt home from there, find that mr [sic] Son*s wife and my own Daughter*s have been here, Cyrus allso. mr Gow*s Child and a Child of Daniel Car*s Expird this morn at y#e$ hook. at ditto.
