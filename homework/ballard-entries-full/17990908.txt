Cloudy till 10h morn, Clear and pleasant afterwards. mr Ballard remains very unwell yet. the Dr Calld to See him, gave him Caster oil and 2 Anidines. Son and Daughter Lambard Came to See him, Ephraim allso. mr Livermore and Dingley and their wives have Calld in to See him. at home, mr Ballard very Sick.
