Clear and very warm. mrs Wood was Safe Deld at 6h morn of a fine Son. I left her and infant Comfortable, and reacht home at 10. I went onto the Bed and Slept till afternoon. Son and Daughter Lambard and children, Becky Sewall, mrs Dingley and mrs Eunice Stackpol here after meeting. I receivd 9/ of mrs Wood Sept 12, 1798 for my Service there this day. at mr Wood*s. Birth 22#nt$. mrs Dingleyinforms me that Daughter Town remains much as Shee was.
