Clear and very pleasant. I returnd home between 11 & 12 am, find my Daughter*s have been here. mr Pollard, his wife, Dolly and Catharine Calld at evening. mr Gower brot us 42_ lb pork a 9#d$, 13_ Chese at 9#d$, 14_ Butter a 1#s$ and took up a note which he gave mr Ballard last June 27#th$. at ditto. children Calld here. mr Hatch& wife left here. James Gower here, Paid what he owed us.
