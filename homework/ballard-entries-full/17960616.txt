Clear morn, Some rain afternoon. I returnd from mr Braley*s. I Calld to See the Widdow Jones, Shee is not very well; at mr John Jones*s, Ruth Lee is very unwell. I Calld to See mrs Rice, Shee is unwell. Dind at Son Pollard*s. Calld at Lambard*s. Came home, Skimd my milk and was Calld Directly to See mrs Rice who is in Labour, her women were Calld but Shee had an unnatural travel. at mr Braley*s and other friends.
