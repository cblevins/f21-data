Clear. I was Calld at 9 yesterday morn to See mrs Welch who is in Labour. Shee was Delivd at 12 of a Daughter, Dead Born, and was very ill after Delivery. I tarried with her till about 9 evening when Shee Seemed a little more Comfortable. I was then Calld to Thomas Hinkley*s. at mrs Welch*s. Birth 4#th$. mr Ballardgone to wiscasset.
