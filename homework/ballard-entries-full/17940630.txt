Clear. I was Calld at 9#h$ morn to my Daughter Pollard who was in Labour, found her very ill. her women were Calld (mrs Pollard, Capin, Black, Hamlin & Burten) and Shee was Safe Deliverd at 1#h$ Pm of a Daughter, her firs#t$ Born, and I tarried with her allnight. Shee is Cleverly. mr Ballard went to Survey for Dr Parker. at Son Pollard*s. Birth 28th. Dolly Calld to Seeher Sister.
