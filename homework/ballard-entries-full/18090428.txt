Clear. the ground frose hard yesterday morn. I have finisht Daughter Lambard*s Soap, there is a Larg Barril full & 2 Pails full. Set up a new Leach and put my own Soape to boiling. mrs Farewel Calld here. Daughter Ballard Sent me 9 oz Buller, 1 lb 2 oz pork and Some milk. at [home]. mrs [Farewel] here.
