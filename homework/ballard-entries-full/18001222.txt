Cloudy and Some rain. I have Bake#d$ Bread and done my house work. mr Ballard has been writing. he had a fall in the Barn which hurt his left Shoalder. I bathed it with Sp#t$ turpintine & Spirit. Cyrus Butcherd a veal for Jonathan. we had a quarter, w#d$ 11 lb. he afterwards went to Son Lambard*s, bro#t$ home 3 bushels Corn. at home.
