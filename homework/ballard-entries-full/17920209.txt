Cloudy & Snow. mr Bolton went to Dr*s & Bo#t$ Senan & Seeds; I gave the infant Some. they Calld mrs Savage there & I left the Child a little Easier & returnd home, was over Sett once. felt Exceedingly fatagud, went to bed before dark. was Calld out again at y#e$ 11 h pm & went to Joseph Prescot*s, his wife in Labour. mrs Hinkley & Prescot wrode part of the way with me. at ditto & mr Prescot*s. receivda letter from Sister Waters of Jany 20#th$ 1792. Sent one to Dr Barton.
