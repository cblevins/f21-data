Clear and pleast. mr Ballard and Ephraim went to the meddow for the last of the hay. Cyrus went to hear Coart. Son Jonathan Came and Caried his children from here, they have been with us two weaks. he Conducted Sally Fletcher, allso, to his house to take Care of his wife. Dr Quimby took Tea here, he paid me 2 Crowns and I gave him a receipt as we Could not find the note. at home. Sally went to Son Jonathan a#s$,his children went home. Dr Quimbyhere.
