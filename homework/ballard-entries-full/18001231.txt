Clear part of the day and Snowd Some par#t$ thereof. mr Waid Butchered 3 Swine for us. Captain Tobey and A mr Fish dind here, Son Jonathan allso. Betsy Cowan Came and helped me part of the day, and now this year is Come to a Close and happy is it if we have made a wise improvment of the time. at home. we had 3 Swine Bulchered and purchasd 31 lb rough Tallow of Captain Ney at 10 Cents pr pound.
