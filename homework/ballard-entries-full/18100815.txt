Clear. I was Calld 30#m$ after mid night by mr Martin of Hollowell to go and See his wife who is in Labour. I went, Shee was linguring litt 11h 30#m$ Am when Shee was Safe Deld of her 6 Child & 5th Daughter. I went from her to See her Mother who apears to be near y#e$ Close of life. God grant her Change may be happy. I returnd home at 10h evening. I left my Patients very Comfortable. at mr Martin Hollowell & at Isaac Cleark*s. Birth 13. receivd 12/ as a reward.
