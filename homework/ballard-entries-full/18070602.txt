Clear Part of the day. mr Ballard and Son Cyrus went to Coart. I have leveled the Gardin North of y#e$ rock & west to the Curent Bushes, planted Mellons & Squash. Son Town Sleeps here, his famely are well. Jackson y#e$ Barber Expired yesterday morn. at home. worked in My Gardin. Son Town Sleeps here.Death of Jackson.
