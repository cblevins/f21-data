Clear. mr Ballard drest and Sett out for meeting, went as far as Son Jonathan*s and returnd not being acomodated hors and Saddle to go. Son Cyrus Came here, Spent Some hours and Sett out for Rome again. Son Ephraim Came after meeting, his Famely well. he informd us the Text was PSalm C III#d$ vers#s$ 15 & 16th. at home. Son*s Cyrus & Ephraim here.
