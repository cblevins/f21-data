Clear and warm. mrs Bell was Siesd with a very Severe pain in her head a little before Day. I made use of means which gave her relief, and left her much more Comfortable at 4h pm. I was at mr Carter*s, Kitchredg*s, Wesson*s, Pitts*s, Soal*s and Pattin*s & Dingley*s. at ditto and other Neighbor*s. Esqr Prescot &wife Sleep here.
