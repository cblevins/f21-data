Clear and pleast. mr Ballard went to Son Jonathan*s. I went to Son Lambard*s after houghing my Cucumbers, from there to mr Burten*s. my Daughter with me. Old Lady Hamlin, mrs Crag and Lidia McKethney there to Tea. mr Burten Paid me in Crickery for my assisting his wife in her illness on 30 may las#t$. 1 Boul @ 4/6, 1#do$ 2/6, 1#do$ 1/6, 1 pitcher 3/, and a Cham#r$ Pot 1/6. we Calld at mr Pitts*s. Hepsy been picking wool and mending and Did the house work. at mr Burten*s & Son Lambard*s.
