Clear & very warm. I Came home at 6#h$ morn. Son Town here. I was Calld at 8 to See mrs Ney. Shee was Safe Deld at 1#h$ pm of a Son. I tarrid till near night. there was a Sprinkling of rain. I receivd 6/ as a reward. find mr John Jones, Ezekiel Chace and mrs Rockwood here. mr Jones Sleeps here. at Captain Ney*s. Birth [20#th$]. mr John Jones here.
