Cloudy. I Came from mr Cox by water, left all well as Could be Expected. I Saw a Schooner go up by mr Goodin*s and a boat allso. I find a pair of new Shoes & put them on, they fitt me very well. Selvst#r$ G. Moore Sleep here. we were alarmd by a noise in the night, the Girls & I rose. at ditto. left thm Cleverly.
