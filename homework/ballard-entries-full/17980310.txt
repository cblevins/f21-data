Cloudy morn, Snowd afternoon. Cyrus went up to the Settlement. mr Partridge Came for me to go to his house, but as I was Expecting imediate Calls from home I did not go. his infants Superfluous thumb was taken off this day by Dr Coney. Hepsy finisht our Bed Cover. I have been doing other matters. at home. mr Partridge Came after me butI Did not go.
