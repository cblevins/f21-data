Cloudy. I was Calld at 5h morn to See mrs Benjamin who was in Labour. Shee was Deld between Seven and Eight of a Son. I left them Comfortable and returnd as far as Son Lambard*s at noon, tarried there till near night. Daughter Pollard and Patty Easty there. Ephraim Came home with me. at mr Benjamin*s. Birth 15th. at SonLambarts afternoon.
