Clear, a North wind. I was Calld to See the wife of Dr Colman who was in Labour at 1#h$ 1/2 yesterday morn. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 11#h$ 10#m$ foren. I left her as well as Could be Expected & returnd home at 3#h$ 30#m$ afternoon. I wrode to mr Robbins, find his Lady more Comfortable. I was at Captain Fillebrown*s, all well. at Dr Colman*s. Birth 34#th$.at mr Robbins*s & Captain Fillebrown#s$.Bot 4 oz Snuff @ 9#d$.
