Clear and very warm. I am informd that mrs Peterson was Deld of a Son yester morn, and was very unwell. I Sett ou#t$ and went to mr Kennady*s, mrs Leach went with me to See her. I find her Better y#n$ my fears. I left Some things for her Support for y#e$ present and returnd home Some fatagud. mr Ballard went to winslow, Sally to her Dads. mr Samuel Pierce Dind here. at mrs Peterson*s, Kennady*s & Densmore*s. the hutt which is Erected for mrs Peterson is Dolefull for to be Sickin.
