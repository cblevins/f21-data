Cloudy & Some rain. mr Ballard at Town meeting. Daniel Coney, Esqr Chosen to represent yesterday Town at y#e$ General Coart. we receivd a letter from Brother Jonathan of Ap#l$ y#e$ 21#st$ by mr Joseph White, informing his famely are well. Jonathan Came home, brot 50 Loggs down. W#m$ Haywad Came with him. mr Hail*s children are mending. at home. receivd a Letter from Brother Moore of Rochester of Ap#l$ 21, 1790.
