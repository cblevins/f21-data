Clear morn, raind a little at evening. Ephraim went to Matthew meddow & returnd with his load at 11h morn. I went to Dr Colman*s & Bo#t$ medesin for which I paid 7/6. Calld at mr Childs, Pollard*s, Pitts*s & Son Pollard*s. Jonathan*s wife & children Came home with me. Dolly is very unwell. at Dr Colman*s & other Neighb#s$.
