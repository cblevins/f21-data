Clear. I was Calld to Captain Hussey*s, walkt to the Loading place from there, Crosst the mountains of ice. arivd all most fatagud to Death, found his wife Deld of a Son. I returnd home by fort Wesson, wrode our hors from mr Pollard*s. Daughter Ballard [Cleverly]. at Captain Hussey*s. Birth 8#th$ to which I have been Calld.
