Clear, Cold morn, but warm Day. I was Calld at 10#m$ past midnight to go and See the wife of Isaac Savage who is in Labour. they reside at Phineas Pain*s. Shee was Deld of her 2nd Daughter and 3#d$ Child at 3h morn. I left them as well as Could be Expected and Came to Son Lambard*s at 5h 30m, find my Daughter very ill. tarried with her all day and night. we applyd Feaverfue to her head and poultises to her feet and Shee Seemd Some Easier at night; Slept a little, her Babe is Cleverly. at Isaac Savage*s. Birth 37th. Came from there to Son Lambard*s.
