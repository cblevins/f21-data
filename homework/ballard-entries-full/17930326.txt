Cloudy. I was Calld by Peter Jones to his wife in Labour at 3h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 4 of a Son. I tarried till near night & Left my Patients Cleverly. receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is gone to Survey for Dr Parker. a very rainy night. at Peter Jones*s. Birth 15#th$.
