Cloudy, Snowd Some and was very Cold. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to meeting. I was Calld at 9h 30#m$ evening to mr Kennady*s to See his Daughter Suky, who was in Labour. her Child was Born a few muinets before I arivd. I Performd all that was necessary and we put her to Bed Comfortable. I tarried all night. they Say that William Ellis is the Father of y#e$ Child. at mr Kennady*s. Birth 29#th$. May 14#th$ 99 recd my fee in Spining.
