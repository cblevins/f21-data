Clear. mrs Bisbe was Deliverd of a Daughter at 2#h$ this morn and is Cleverly. I returnd home at 10 am Some fatagud. Polly Bisbe rode the hors Back. my Daughter, Hannah, is much Better. mr Cumings & David Wall here Drying flax. raind this night. mr Bisbe De#t$r to fee & Medicin EightShillings. I was Calld to mr Hains at 4#h$ aft.
