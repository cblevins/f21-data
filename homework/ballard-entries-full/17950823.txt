Clear and warm. I was Calld to See mrs Hardin at [ ]h morn, Shee was Deld of a Son before I arivd, assisted by Dr Hubard. I left her Comfortable, wrode behind mr Samuel Cumings to mr Wesson*s, find her very unwell; went to Son Pollard*s, hir Child is unwell. I Dind at mr Lambard*s, allended meeting aflern & went to mr Burten*s. at mr Hardin*s & at meeting. SamlNorcross & his wife here. Birth 35#th$.
