Cloudy. I Sowd Some redish seed. mrs Child Sent for me to see her. I find her very unwell. I Calld at mr Pollard*s. they are Better at mr Crag*s, find Polly Bisbe very unwell there. returnd at evening; find mrs Brown here. Shee is much more Comfortable than Shee has been. at mr Childs*s Pollard*s & Craggs.Hannah is Cleverly this Day.
