Clear and warm. Sally Pollard and D. Lambard took their leav of me and Expected to Sett out for Clinton before breakfast. Son Cyrus after for Rome. may they all arive at their homes in safty is my ardent wish. Hannah Pollard Calld here, took her liev of me, [Sett] out for home. may they all be blest and prosper through life. at [home]. [Hannah*s] Ballard, Pollard & BetsyWiman [ ].
