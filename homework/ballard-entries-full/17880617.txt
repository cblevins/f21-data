Cloudy. James Wesson Expired at 3#h$ morn. I laid him out ascisted by mrs Loes Harris & Polly Hinkley. mrs North Came in to See them. I returnd home at 10#h$ morn; feel much fatagud, took a knap of Sleep, feel better. mrs Welch here. mr Ballard laying out roade by y#e$ river below mr Pollard*s to Pitts Town.
