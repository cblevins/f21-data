Clear part of the Day, Snowd at evening. I have been Combing flax. Augustus Ballard, his wife & infant here. we were informd of the Death of Captain Samuel Howard. I was Calld at 10h evening by W#m$ Moore to go and See his wife who was in Labour, arivd at near midnight. at W#m$ Moore*s. Ephraim is 20 years oldy#s$ Day.
