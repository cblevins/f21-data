Clear. I have been very Sick. mrs Smith and Stone Came and took Care of me, got my Bed in to the North room. my husband Sent for Dr Coney but they Calld Dr Ellis. he left me medisin. mrs Firbesh Came and Set with me till evening when her Husband Came to accompany her home. Joanna Slept here. [at home, very Sick. Dr Ellis to See me].
