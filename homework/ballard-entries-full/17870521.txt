Clear. the Girls Strikeing harnis. I went to See mrs Williams. mrs Voce & Betsy here. mr Smile Sleeps here. mr Ballard surveying for Reverend mr Foster & been to Dr Coney*s. mrs Harris, Lidia Bisbe, Sally Pierce & others here yesterday Evinng. mr Savage made the irons for our Loome. I paid him 4 Shillings in Cash.
