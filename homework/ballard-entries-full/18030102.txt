Clear morn, Cloudy part y#e$ Day. I was Calld at 5h yesterday morn to go and See mrs Waid, Shee was Deld of a Son at 10. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 3 pm. we went to my Son*s to dine, or rather Sup, after meeting. Nabby Came at 10#h$ evening, then went to Jonathan*s. at mr Waid*s. Birth 1#st$. receivd 4:6.
