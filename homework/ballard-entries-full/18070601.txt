Clear foren, Cloudy and some rain aftn. I have Sett Leutice, removd stones which were dug by the hogs pen, planted Squash at the west Side the house. mr Ballard Planted Potatoes N of y#e$ house. Cyrus to Son*s Lambard & Ephraim*s, and to Esqr Bridg*s. the Democrats were fireing Canon and Ringing the Bell on Sullivan being Governer. they began before Sun rise and kept it up till after 12 O Clock. at home. Democrats Rejoiceing.
