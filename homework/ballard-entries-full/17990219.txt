Clear and Cool at 1h this morn when I was Calld by mr Woodward to go and See the wife of Reuben Branard who was in Labour. I arivd there at 3h m, found they had Calld Old Lady Whiting. there were Some obstructions to remove. when Performd the patient was Safe Deld of her 3#d$ Son at 5h morn. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 10h morn. I receivd 9/ as a reward. I find Eliphalet Cleverly. mr Ballard went to Son Jonathan*s, Cyrus is Dresing flax. at [Reuben Branard*s] [ ]. [Birth 5th]. [recd]9/ [as a rewd].
