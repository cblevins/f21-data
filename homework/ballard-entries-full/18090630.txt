Clear Part of ye day. mr Ballard and I attended Lecture. Reverend mr Stone Discoarst from Acts 26th C 28 vers. Magr Coney*s wife & Rewel Williams*s [ond] y#e$ Covenant and had their children Baptis#ed$. Judge North & Coney presented 2 Each & Brooks & mrs Hannah Cox was admited to full Communion with this Church. at Leclure & at Daughter Lambard*s. her Babe is notwell.
