Snow*s. it is now in the 2#nd$ hour. I have not been to rest for the night. this day is appointed to giv thanks for the mercies received y#e$ pas#t$ year. I feel very ill able to perform my work. Betsy Cowan Came and helped me. Shee, Son Jonathan, his wife and all their children, Lambard & famely, Son Pollard & wife, Cyrus & Ephraim partook with us at Dinner. Esqr Davis & his Son here at evening. Betsy was waited on by James Savage to Asa Pierce*s. at home. my children Dind with us.
