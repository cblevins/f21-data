Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus Surveying. I wed gardin foren. Esqr Coney here. Son Town left here homewd bound. mrs Welch here. mrs Livermore & I went to mr While*s. Old mr Hinkley here. mr Adams & Sprage here who presented an Election Sermon to my Daughter for her Daddy. mr Ballard did not return, Cyrus did. at mr White*s. mr Adams left mr RobbinsElection Sermon.
