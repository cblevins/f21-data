Clear & very warm. mr Ballard went to Pittst#n$ to mill. Son Jonathan a brot his oldest Son here foren. Ephraim went to ye hook for Some Biskitt, 2 Doz#n$ 1/4, Gal#n$ Vinigar 1/6, 1 lb Loaf Sugar 1/10. Betsy Voce Calld here, informs me that mrs Pitts is more Comfortable than Shee was yesterday. Daughter Ballard & children Sleep here. at home. Daughter Ballard & Childrenhere.
