Clear. I was Calld at 2 hours morn to See the wife of mr Thompson, find her in Labour. Shee was Deld of her third Child, a Daughter, at 4#h$. I left her and inf#t$ Cleverly, and Came home foren. I receivd 9/6 as a reward. I went in to See mrs Eadds & mrs Hamlin, find them Cleverly. Son Cyrus Sett ou#t$ for Winslow. at mr [Thompson*s]. Birth 37#th$. receivd9/6. [ ].
