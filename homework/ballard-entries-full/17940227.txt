Clear till evening when it Snowd. I was Calld at 1#h$ pm to See mrs Swanton who Deld at 7 of a Son, her Second Child. I went to See Captain Savage who is unwell. Calld allso to See mr Williams*s Daughter who remains very weak. Came home about 11h evening. I receivd 6/ as a reward. at mr Swanton*s. Birth 8th.
