Clear, wind to y#e$ Northerd. mr Kilridg Conducted his wife to Captain Dean*s Vessel at y#e$ hook Bound to the South Shore. I Came from mr Smith*s after assisting to git his wife up and to Bed again. Calld at mr Gill*s. Saw Son Town, his famely are well as usual. I was at Esqr Haywad*s and Son Pollard*s. my Daughter was here helping Dolly about her Bed quillt. at mr Smith*s. receivd 7/6 as a reward. Polly [sic].
