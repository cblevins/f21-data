Clear part of ye day. I was Calld after 1h Pm to See the wife of John Pierce who was in Labour. Shee was delivd about 2 of a Son, her first Born; her husband absent. Sister Barton & Son Jonathan*s wife there but Shee was Delivd before they arivd. Sister & I got home at dusk. at Captain Smile. Birth 4th. Jany 19 recvd the [last] of my Due.
