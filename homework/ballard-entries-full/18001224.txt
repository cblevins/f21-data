Clear and very pleast. mrs Welch here for things for the Sick Babe. mr Ballard went to mr Asa Williams and got my lether Shoes mended. I have been very unwell yesterday day but have done my house work. Son Jonathan here at evening, Sold a mare to Nathan Tylar, took of him a note for 9 Do#ls$ payable the middle of March; and another, Same Sum, to be paid September y#e$ first in good Shingles. at home.
