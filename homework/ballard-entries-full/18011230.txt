Clear and very Pleast for Decembr. I have been at home, been able to knit Some, finisht futing a pair of hoes for Son Ephraim. mr Ballard*s hand is better. Old Lady Butlar and mrs Farewel Calld to See us. Ephraim been to work at Jonathan*s new house. Cyrus after Corn and to mill. Patty baked. at home. mrs Butlar & Farewel here. Some Snow this night.
