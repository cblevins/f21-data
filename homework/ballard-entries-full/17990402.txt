Cloudy. I have been Combing flax. Sally went to Singing, Jonathan*s Son*s went home. Daughter Lambard & children & Ephraim here. I was Calld to mr Partridge*s to See his children who are very badly Scolt. I tarried and Sett up with the Destresed famely. it Snowd, raind and haild this night. at mr Partridge*s, his children are Scolt.
