Clear part of the day. Daughter Lambard went from here to my Son*s. I went there afternoon to See Hannah who is very Sick with a Pukeing & desentry. Old Lady Mosier Came there, we took Tea and Shee Came home with me; Slept here. the Child is Some Easier. Rhoda Pollard with her aunt. at my Son*s. Daughter Lambard went home.
