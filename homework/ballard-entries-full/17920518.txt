Cloudy & Showery. mrs Foster was Safe Deld of a Daughter & first Child at 5#h$ 30#m$. I was Calld upon to go & See the Lady of Jed#y$ Prescot & I arivd there at 10, find her in Labour. tarried there all night. went to Bed & had rest. mrs Atkins of Sterling there. at Doe*s & mr Prescot*s. Birth 20th.
