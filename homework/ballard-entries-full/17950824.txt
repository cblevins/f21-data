Clear Excep#t$ a Shower. mr Benjamin Calld me to See his Child, it Expird at 4h pm before that I reacht there. mrs Mosier and my Self Put on the grave Cloaths and I Came away. Calld to See mr Burten*s Child, it is mending. I Calld on my Daughter*s and mr Gill as I went. the Latter is unwell. mr Ballard been to mr White*s and to a Parrish Meeting. at mr Benjamin*s, his Child Expird this afternoon.
