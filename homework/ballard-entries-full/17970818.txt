Clear. my patient Seemed very feeble. the force of her illness Came on at noon and Shee was Deld at 1#h$ 30#m$ pm of her first Daughter and 4th Child. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected at 7h evening and returnd home, find mr Ballard very unwill. I applied Burdoc leavs & rhum warm to his neck and Sholders. Son Town Sleeps here. at ditto. Birth 39#th$. Son Town Sleepshere. receivd 12/ in Cash and Butter Jany 19 & 20#th$.1798.
