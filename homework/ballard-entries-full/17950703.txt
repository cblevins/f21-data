Clear and warm. I worked Some in my Gardin and Some on raggs for Coverld. mr James [Webber] and his Sister Sally Dind here. John Kennady & David Densmore worked here, had 9/ in Cash at night. mr Shubael Hinkley*s man worked afternoon. Sally Shows much uneasiness about her wages. magr Stickney here, receivd money of mr Ballard as a Bounty for a wolf. at home. magr Stickney receivd a Bounty for wolfof mr Ballard.
