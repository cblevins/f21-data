Clear & very Cold. mrs Sewall was Safe Deld at 2h 15m morn of a Daughter, her 3#d$ Child, & is as well as Could be Expected. I returnd home after we Dind. receivd 6/ Cash, 1/2#lb$ Tea, 3 Skeins Cambk Thread as a Compensation. Loes Hinkley Calld here. at mr Moses*s Sewall*s. Birth 7#th$.mr Ballard gone to mill. mr Searl*s here; Dind.
