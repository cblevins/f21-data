Clear and Cold. my famely, all Except my Self, attended worship at the meeting house foren. I went afternoon; Daughter Ballard all day. Jonathan, his wife, Ephraim & Patty to Son Lambard*s at evening. mr Gill and wife are gone to winslow yesterday day. the North Parish worship#t$ in the new Coart house. Jotham Sewall performd. at meeting & all my famely. mr Gill is goneto Son Town*s.
