Clear & very Pleasant. I went to mr True*s to See Jenny Coy, find her more Comfortable; then returnd home. mr Hatch & wife & Rufus Ballard here. Son Town Beck here informs his famely are well & that mrs Barton was Delivrd of a Son y#e$ 8th inst. the Reverend mr Isaac Foster removd to Varsalboro yesterday Day. mr Ballard is gone to the hook. at mr Densmore*s & True*s. mr Hatch went fromhere.
