Clear, Pleasant day. mr Waid worked here. I did my washing, Sally Did the house work. Cyrus Conducted me to See mr Dingley and famely. I find him poorly, his wife better. Hannah unwell. mr Ballard Came there for me at 8h evening. mr Gill Came in Soon after him for me to go and See his wife who is in Labour and was Safe Delivered of a Daughter between 10 and 11h evening. Old Lady Brooks, mrs Capin and Daughter Lambard were assistants, they all returnd from there. I tarried all night. at mr Dingley*s and at mr Gill*s. Birth 34#th$.it Snowd at evening. receivd 6/ and _ lb hyson Tea for myService.
