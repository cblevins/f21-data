Clear fore#n$. Sally Cox went to See her Brother Gersham and Sally Hussey joind in wedlock. I have been at home. Dolly is not returnd. mr Ballard been to Colonel Howard*s on business with mr Warin & S. Jewet. mr Kimbal & Hannah Cox were allso married. I am informd. at home.
