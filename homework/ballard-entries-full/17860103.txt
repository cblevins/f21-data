mr Bullin & Rachel Faught Came here at 9/O Clo#k$ going to attend funeral of mrs Faught. mr Densmore Calld me to See his Son which is unwell. it began to snow when I left home. I calld to see mrs North as I came home. Shee is very Sick. was Enformd there of ye Death of Judge Bowman of Pownalboro. it is a Tedious Storm.
