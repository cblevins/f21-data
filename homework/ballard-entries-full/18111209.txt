Clear. mr Firbesh Calld me at 3h am to go to Captain Smith*s to See his wife who w was Deliverd of a Son, her first Born at 5#h$ am. I was Calld from her at 7 by W#m$ Sprage to See his wife who was Deld before 12 of a Daughter, her 3 Child. they are all Comfortable. I reacht home at Sun Sett feel some Fatagued. I receivd 2/1 lb Tea of Sprage in part. March 31#st$ recd 1 Bush#l$ Corn at 4/6. at mr Firbesh*s. birth 12th. at wm Sprage*s. birth13th. Death B. Bodwel.
