Clear, windy and Cold. mr Ballard and Cyrus up to our house. mr Brown Came for Hepsy and Shee Sett out for his home at 2h pm. I have been washing and Scouring yarn, but feel very unfitt for Such Service. mr Stone, from Townsend, Sleeps here, he Slept here the night of oct#r$ 5#th$ 97. at home. Hepsy left me and went to her Father*s.
