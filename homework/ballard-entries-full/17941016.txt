Clear. I have wrote yesterday morn to my Brother Elijah Moore of Douglas. Silva Moore took Breakfast here & Sett out home. mr Ballard been to Esqr Coney*s & mr Burten*s. mr Green of Sandy river Sleeps here. Jonathan Nichols here, receivd Letters to Carry to Oxford for me. James Sturges Came here yesterday. at home. I wrote to Bro#r$Elijah Moore.
