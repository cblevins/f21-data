Clear. mr Ballard & mr Crosby Sett out for Pownalboro at 3#h$ morn. mrs Wesson here fore#n$, mrs Welch afternoon. I Combd flax & Let mr Welch have 2 lb Tow. Shee had 3 lb before. I went to See mr Williams; find him Lower than he has been, and more Distres#t$ for Breath. I was at Neighbor William#ss$.
