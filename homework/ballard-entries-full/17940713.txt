Cloudy morn. mrs Plaisted was Delivd at 10h morn of a Son. I left her before 12 and arivd at home at 1, feel Some fatagud as I have not been undresst Since I left home. mr [sic] famely are all gone to meeting at the meeting house. I killd about 30 flees & Changd my Cloaths, feel refreshed. mr Plaisted bestowd 9/ as a reward for my Service. Dolly Calld to See mr Pitts & Daughter Pollard. mrs Pitts is very low. at ditto. Birth 29th. receivd 9/.
