Clear. mr Hains Learned Came here Directly from the westward. Brot me a Letter from Brother Collins which informs my Friends were then well. mr Ballard went to mr Babcock*s. I was Calld to mrs Howard*s to See his Daughter lbbee who is Siesd with a relaps of the rash. I found mrs Cumings there, So returnd home. Sally Pierce here today. Patty is Better. I was Seisd with a Disentery in the night.
