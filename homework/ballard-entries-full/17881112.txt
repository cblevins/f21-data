Clear. I was at mr Sewall*s. his Lady Calld me up at 4 ys morn & Sent for her women. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son at half after Six Eving & I returnd home about 8. Left Mother & Child Cleverly. find Widdow Williams & Polly Savage here. the wind Blod part of our Barn of yesterdy & Damaged many other Buildings in this Town. Killed some Swine. at mr Sewall*s. Sally Dexter went from here to winthrop.I receivd 6/ of mr Sewall*s.
