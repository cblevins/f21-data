Clear. I have been more Comfortable, have houghd my Squashes, mellons and Cucumbers. Daughter Lambard been with me all day, Cleand Cyrus*s Bedstead of bugs, Swept my Chambers (which Shee found very dirty), done my house work, &C. mrs Farewel, Daughter Lambard, Son Jonathan a and wife took Tea with me. Cyrus has killd a Lamb of ours, I Eat a Small portion there of. at home, have worked Some in my Gardin. Old mrs Marsh Expired yesterday night.
