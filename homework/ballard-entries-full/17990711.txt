Clear. mr Ballard went to Son Jonathan*s. I work#t$ in my Gardin foren. mrs Dingley and her children and Elijah Moore Dind here. I was Calld at 2h pm to See the wife of Church Williams who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 evening of a Son, her first Born. I tarried all night, the patient as well as Could be Expected. at Church Williams. Birth 27. mrs Dingley hereand Dind. I receivd 9/7 for my Service.
