Clear till afternoon. Polly Whealer was removd from here to mr Livermore*s. I went with her. Shee was not much fatagud. mrs Livermore Seemed Sorry Shee was brot there but used her very Kind. this the annevversary of my mariage, 34 years. I went to mr Livermore*s with Polly. John Savagethrew Ephraim Down on y#e$ ice & abused him. Snowd.
