Clear & pleast. mr Bradwill Livermore Calld on me this morn, favrs me with Carrying a Letter to Dr Waters. I went to See mr Densmore*s famely, the infant is very ill; the other children much as they have been. I Came home, Supt & went back & watcht. Eliphalet Robbins Came there for me to go to mr Pollard*s, Betsy Heuston being very Sick, but I Could not go. at mr [Densmore*s].
