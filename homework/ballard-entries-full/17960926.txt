Clear, the wind Northerd. I attended Funeral of mr Waid*s infant. I Calld at mr Densmore*s as I went, at mr Dingley*s as I returnd. mrs Thwing and her Sister Nancy Kiddar, mrs Dingley, Heartford and mrs Nancy Hilton took Tea with me. at the funeral of mrs Waid*s infant. mrs Foster isunwell. mrs Thwing here.
