Cloudy, Snowd at evening. mr Ballard went to winthrop, Carried 25 yds of white woolen Cloath to be Coulered and pres#t$. I was Calld to See mrs Dingley again, find her very ill. I tarrid all night, Sett with her. Shee Seemd Easier before morn, but her throat is very much Sweld inwardly. at mr Dingley*s, Shee is very Sick. mr Heartford& wife allso
