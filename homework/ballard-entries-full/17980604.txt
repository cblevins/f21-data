Cloudy and Some rain. Watson worked for Son Jonathan a. mr Ballard Setled with Samuel & Phillip Norcross and took their obligations for what was Due to him. he went up to mill afterwards, Sett out to run the South line of the plymoth Companies tand. he left home at 1h pm. Nabby washt. I have washt the Store floor. mr Ballard Saw Son Town who informs that his wifeis Some better.
