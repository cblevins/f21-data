Clear & Cool. Captain Hersey Conducted mrs Voce & I home. we left his Lady about house. I find my famely as well as usual. Josiah Ketth informd me that mrs Pierce Expird this morn. mr Ballard is gone to y#e$ hook to a meeting of y#e$ proprietors at unity. Parthenia returnd from mr Foot*s, informs me Fredrick*s infant has had fitts & that Polly Bisbe is much Better. it Snowd be fore night. at ditto. mrs Pierce Expird y#s$morn at 6#h$ 30m.
