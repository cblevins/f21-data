Clear & Cold. I went to See W#m$ Densmore, find him much as he has been of late. I was Calld by mr Greely (from there) to See his Lady who was in Labour & was Safe Del#d$ at 10h 30m evening of a fine Daughter, her 7#th$ Child of which 4 are Daughter*s. I tarried all night, Slept a little. at mr Densmore*s & mr Greely*s. Birth6#th$. receivd 3/ august 16#th$ 1794 ofmr Greely.
