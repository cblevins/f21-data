A foggy morn, Clear & warm afterwds. I was Calld to See Ben#n$ Fitch*s wife at 1#h$ morn. Shee was Deld of a Son (her first Born) at 5#h$ m. I left her Cleverly, her Babe allso, & arivd at home at 10. receivd 6/ as a reward. Dolly is gone to Son Pollard*s. Sally finisht Bleaching y#n$, Cyrus went to Captain Fillebrown bot 1 quart W E Rhum, paid Cash; had 2 lb Shugar, ditto of Butter & 1 lb Coffee which was not paid for. at Ben#n$ Fitche. Birth 35#th$. at home.
