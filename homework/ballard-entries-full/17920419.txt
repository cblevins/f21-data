Clear a Cool air. mr Ballard & Esqr Wesson went to Esqr Coney*s & Engagd to Lay ou#t$ 21 Town Ships, between the rivers PenobScott & Kenebeck. it is Land which belongs to this Commonwelth. I have been at home. Hannah Scoured my Puter & the kitchen. Seth Manley Bro#t$ us Corn & worked here with one yoak oxen. at home. mr Ballard & Es#qr$Wesson at Esqr Coney*s.
