Snowd. I have been at home, not So well as I Could wish. Son Lambard brot Allen and left him here, then went and Conducted Daughter Pollard to his house. Hepsy and I prepareing to make Some Pies. A Stag of Son Jonathan*s hookt a mare So that Shee Expired Soon. at home. Peris Hamlin*s wife Deld by mrs Mosier.
