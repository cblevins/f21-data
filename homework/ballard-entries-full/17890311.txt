Cleard. mrs Savage here. Shee & I Reckond & Setled. I fall in her debt /6. mr Ballard been to Andrew Goodin*s for molases & Colonel Duttin*s for Potatos. Polly Savage here. I was Calld to See mrs Andrews at 5#th$ H pm. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 8 & 30 M evening of a Daugter. I returnd at 12 percisely by our time piece. Left my patients Cleverly. my famely w#s$ in Bed when I returnd. mrs Savage & I Setled. at mr Andrews.
