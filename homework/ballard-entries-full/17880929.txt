Clear. I went to See mr Childs*s Son who is unwell. I went as far as mr Edson*s. Came home at 2 pm. Brother Moore here. I went afterward to old mr Isaac Savage*s. Came home at Eving. I Calld at Dr Colman*s; his Lady made me a presant of a Cap. at mr Childs. Dolly finished weaving the Check,39-2/1 [sic] yds. mr Palmar*s Son at winthrop Expird of a feavor.
