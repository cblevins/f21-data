Clear and not very Cold. I Sett out to go to meeting, went to the road where mr Timothy Foster met me and took me in to his Sleigh and Conductd me to mr Rice*s at Winthrop. his wife was Deld of a Daughter at 1#h$ 30#m$ pm and I returnd home at evening, Conducted by mr Moses*s Wood. am informd that mrs Dickman Expird last evening at 7#h$. at mr Rice*s at Winthr#p$. Birth 8th. receivd 9/ for my Service.
