Clear. Dr Barton & others took Breakfast here. I was Calld to Isaac Hardin*s wife in travil. Calld to mrs Williams to hear from her Husband, he is near Expireing; found mrs Hardin poorly. Shee was Safe Del#d$ of a fine Son at 11#h$ & 1/2 evening. I tarried all night. at mr Hardin*s. Smith & [Cloe] Cleark Published.Spoke with mrs Williams.
