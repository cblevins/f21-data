Clear but very windy. Captain Fillebrown Brown Came & Carried me from mr Norcross*s to his house. I tarried till 7h evening when mr Waid Calld me to See his wife who was in Labour, & was Safe Deld of his first Son & 3#d$ Child at the middle of the night. I tarried there all night, Sett up. I receivd 6/ of mr Waid. at Captain Fillebrown*s & mrWaids. Birth 6th.
