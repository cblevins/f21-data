The Sun rose Clear, Snowd Soon after. I left my patients more Comfortable. Calld to See Sally Hamlin & Hannah Crag; find y#m$ Better. Polly McKethney Seems a little Better. arivd at home by 10 morn, find mr Porter and wife here. Dolly is Better. Hannah*s face is not so badly Scolt as we Expected. Shee Spilt Boiling Licour on her foot this Day and Scolt it Some. Jane Welch Came for me to go and See her Mother. I went. her Complaints are pain in her head and limbs. mr Porter and wife & Hannah went to mr Burten*s. at ditto. Hamlin*s & mr Crag*s &mrs Walch*s. mrs Porter gave me 1/2 lb of wool.
