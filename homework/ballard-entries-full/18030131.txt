Clear. I was Calld at 20m after midnight to go and See my Daughter Lambard who was in Labour. She was Safe deld of her 5#th$ Child & 2#nd$ Daughter at 1#h$ 20#m$. I tarried with her till near the middle of the day when I was Calld by mr Edson to go and See mrs Bodwel who was in Labour. Shee was Safe deld at 6#h$ evening of a Daughter, and I returnd home at 11#h$ 20#m$. I had a Severe turn of Cramp in the night. at Son Lambard*s & mrsBodwe#ls$. Birth Son Lambard*s Daughter. Birth 5th, fee receivd. Birth 6th, recd 6/ as a reward.
