Clear and warm Day. mrs Bailey was Deld of a Son (her Second Child) at 2h morn. I took a nap of Sleep. Sett ou#t$ for home at 5. Calld at Son Jonathan*s, all well there; at Son Lambard*s, they are Cleverly. mrs Cowan Calld here, my little Grand Son*s Sleep with us. at ditto. Birth 39#th$. receivd 9/ as a reward.
