Clear. I have filld quills & Cleand the Store doore, remoovd as much Dirt and old ashes as 12 BuShels, put it about my Cumbers and Mellons which were planted in Chipy Ground. Sally went to Captain Fillebrown Bot me 2 lb Cottne 5/, to mr Medcalf*s bot 1_ yd ribband /9. I was So falagud ys night I did not rest well. at home.
