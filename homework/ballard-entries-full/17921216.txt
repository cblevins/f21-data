Clear & very Cold. I went to my Son*s to See his Child, it is a little more Comfortable. mrs Pitts wrode with me as far as Son Pollard*s. I went in & warmd, they are well there. Cyrus returnd from Sidney. John Chamberlain was marid y#e$ 10 inst, removed his wife to Sidney y#e$ 11#th$. Shee was Deld of a Daughter y#e$ 12#th$ & it Expird before night. at my Son*s, the Child is better.
