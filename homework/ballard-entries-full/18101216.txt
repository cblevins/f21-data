Clear & Cold. mrs D. had her women Calld in ys morn and was Deld of a Daughter, her Second, & 5th Child at 5h 20#m$ and is as Comfortable as Can be Expected. I was Conducted home about noon by her husband. I receivd 5 lb Candles & 1 Dolr. at Abm Devenport*s Birth. Birth 18th. recd 12/ for Service. Funeral infant Livermore*s.
