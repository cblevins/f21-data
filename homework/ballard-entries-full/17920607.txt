A Cloudy, rainy Day. I have been knitting. Ephraim tro#d$ on a board in which was a nale, & lamb#d$ him Self. I wrote to Brother Collins, to Send by mr Burten. I was Calld at 8h evening by EbenZ Church to See his wife who was in Labour. her Child was Born (& not alive), before my arival, but Shee was in Dangerous Circumstance. at EbenZ Church*s. Birth 23#d$.
