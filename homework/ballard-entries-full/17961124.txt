Clear and pleast, but Cold. the ice is Stopt in the river. mr Abram Page broke thro and crost with his Canoe. I went to See mrs Dingley, find her Cleverly. mrs Densmore Came there. I was Calld from there to See Anna Partridge who had Convulsed turns, but is revivd. I Sett up till after 1 O Clock. at ditto and at mr Partriges.
