I Came home from mr Bolton*s Early, had not Set Down before Asa Williams Came for me to go to his wife. I went as far as Crag*s, Crost y#e$ river & went on hors back in great hast. found her Safely Delivrd by Old mrs Ingraham. mr Williams gave me 10#S$. I returnd home at 12 O Clock. Samuel Babcock*s wife & Dorcas Savage here.
