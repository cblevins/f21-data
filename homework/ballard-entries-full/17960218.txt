Clear. I went to Son Lambard*s at noon, mrs Capin, Daughter Pollard and Pally Easty there. we quillted a Coat after I went. Son Town Came there at evening, mr Capin allso. Cyrus Conducted him, his wife & Daughter home. mr Ballard & Cyrus attended funeral of Captain Randal*s Son this after noon. at Son Lambard*s. the remains of mr Randalwere Commited to the Grave.
