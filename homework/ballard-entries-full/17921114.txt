Cloudy & a very Chilly air. I went up as far as Savage Bolton*s, he paid me 8/ at mr Burten*s for attending his wife y#e$ 19th of Jany last. I Bot 2 puter Dishes, wt 5lb @ 2/; 1 Coffee p#t$, 3/6; Six table Spoons, 1/8; total 15/2. I paid 7/2 Cash. mr Bolton answers 8/ of it. at Savage Bolton*s & others.
