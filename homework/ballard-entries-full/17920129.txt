Clear. I Came from mr Shaw*s & arivd at home at 11h morn, receivd 6/ as a reward. Dolly is at home. Hannah & Shee went to mr Waid*s. Joseph Foster here. Cyrus was up. Ephraim went Down as far as Captain Hussey*s with him to take y#e$ hors Back. Jonathan is not here yesterday Day. Moses*s Pollard informs me Eliphalet Robbins is recovering from his late illness. at ditto. my Daughter went to See mrs Waid.
