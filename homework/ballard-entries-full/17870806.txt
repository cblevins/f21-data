I am at mrs Howard*s watching with her Son, went out about Day. Discoverd our saw mill in flames. The men at y#e$ fort went over, found it Consumd together with Some plank & Boards. I tarried till Evinng. Left James Exceeding Dangerously ill. my Daughter Hannah is 18 years old this Day. mrs Williams here when I Came home. Hannah Cool gott mrs North*s web out at the Loome. mr Ballard Complains of a soar Throat yesterday night. he has been to take mr Gardner*s hors home.
