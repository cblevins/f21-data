Clear and very warm. mrs Kenny  here, informs me her Daughter was very ill last night. I gave her Some Bread, meat, Chees, milk, String beens and Soap to Carry to her. Sally Came home at 10h morn, all well at her Dads Excep#t$ mr Kimbal*s infant. Captain Stackpol*s Lady, mrs Greely and mrs Dingley here. I was So falagud that they found me on bed. at home. mrs Stackpol, Greely &Dingley here.
