Cloudy & Some rain. I Sett ou#t$, weak as I was, & wrode home. Find Dolly very unwell. Shee wrode to her Brother Pollard*s & her Sis#tr$ & mrs Pitts Came here to See me. Polly Livermore took Tea here. I Drank 2 Cups of Tea & Eat a Little, which is the firs#t$ food I have taken Since Saterdy noone, Excep#t$ thin gruel. I made a Decoction of feavr fu, peneroial & Cammomile for my Self & Daughter. at ditto. Left Judy & infant Comfortable.
