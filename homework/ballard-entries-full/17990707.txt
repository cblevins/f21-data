Clear and warm with a Comfortable air. mr Ballard and I walkt to meeting. I Came home at noon. Son and Daughter Lambard Came here, took Breakfast with us, his famely all Dind here. Cyrus had his hors and went to mr John Brown*s for Hepsy, they arivd here at [5]h pm. at meeting foren#n$. the ordinance ofy#e$ Supper administred. Hepsy Brown Came here.
