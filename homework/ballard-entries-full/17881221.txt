Clear & Cold. mr Ballard & others went to the house of worship but were not Suferd to Enter. then went to Reverend mr Foster*s, had liberty at mr Thwing for mr Foster to preach in his house; his Text was Geneses 3#d$ Chap 8#th$ vers, mr Ballard has been to mr Livermore*s this Ev#n$. Polly Whealer is very Low & his infant is very Sick also. I have been at home. Widdow Williams Sleeps here. mrCrage was Married. Widdow Williams Sleeps here.
