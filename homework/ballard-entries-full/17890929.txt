Clear fore#n$, Cloudy afternoon. I Came from mr Wesson*s home yesterday morn. Brother Moore here, James Moore also. our Saw mills go Briskly. Cyrus went on Board Dany*s Vesel & Brot me Some wool. he went a Second time & got 14 [lb] of pork for Jonathan a. Dolly finish#t$ weaving the all wool, there is 16 yds. it took 23 Skeins of warp & 25 of filling. I had 6 lb of pick#t$ wool, Cyrus, 8-1/2 lb. Shee was 4 days about it. at mr Wesson*s.
