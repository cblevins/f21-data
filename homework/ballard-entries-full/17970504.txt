Cloudy morn, Clear afternoon. we all attended worship at the fort. the Reverend mr Stone Discoarst from Isaah 66 C 2#nt$ vers. my Daughter Lambard had a faint turn in the time of Service. Shee did not attend the afternoon. I was at Son Pollard*s at intermision. Rhoda Seemd very unwell. I Calld after evening Service, find her better. at Son Lambard*s, Shee is more Comfortable. Hannah Fletcher Dind here, a mr Harford allso. at meeting and Son*s Pollard*s & Lambard*s.
