Clear. I was Calld at 3h m to Son Ephraim*s, his wife in Labour. Son Jonathan*s wife went with me. my Daughter Deld at 7#h$ 30#m$ of her 3#d$ Daughter. her Case was Some Alarming but Shee revivd and Seems Comfortable. Daughter B went to Esqr Titcomb*s and Ephraim*s wife had a fainting fit. Old Lady Hamlin and I attended her [this day ]. [at Son Ephraim*s]. Birth 1st. receivd 18 S as reward.
