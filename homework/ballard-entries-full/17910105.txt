Clear and very pleast, thawd Some. mr Hollowell, y#e$ tinker Dind. Pattey & anne Bullin Dind and Drank Tea. James Cowan & wife took Tea here. Jonathan*s Red Colt was hook#t$ by one of y#e$ Steers, hir Entrales Came out. Jonathan returnd them, Sewed up the rent. Shee not with Standing, Expird before morn. I have been at home. Parthenia gon to Burten*s. at home. Jonathan Lost a Colt. Parthenia gone. Patty*s new Shoes Brot home.
