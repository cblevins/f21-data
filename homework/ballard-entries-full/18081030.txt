Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus to meeting. Son Lambard here, he has been unwel. I went to my Son*s at 2h pm. mr Dickman Expired about 6h evening, his remains were Conducted in a Cart to his house. Son Jonathan & mr Wiman went down with the bereived friends. I took Tea at my Son*s and Came home at 10h evening. at Son Jonathan*s. mr Dickman Expired about 6h evening.
