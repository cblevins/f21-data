Clear. Son Town left here Early, going to Coart. I have Done my work, Sett out at 3h pm to See Daughter Lambard, was met by mr Noles and Disered to go and See his Brother Elisha*s wife who is in Labour. I arivd at 6 and Shee was Deld of a Daughter, her third Child, at 9h evening. I tarried till morn. at Elisha Noles*s. Birth 28#th$. April 27, 1798 receivd 3_ yds Cottne Cloath @3/.
