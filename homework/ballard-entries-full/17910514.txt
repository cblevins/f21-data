rainy morn, Cloudy Day. mrs Moore Calld us up at 2 h morn, Shee was Safe Del#d$ at 2#h$ 30#m$ morn of a fine Daughter, her fifth Child, and is Cleverly. I returnd home accompanied by mr Mortgain, find Dolly at work and much more Comfortable. mr Ballard Sowd flax this Day. the Town mett yesterday & Chose Daniel Coney Esqr to represent this Town. Cyrus Came here. at ditto. Birth 13th. receivd 7/6 of mr James Moore for this Days Performance, Sep#t$ 301793.
