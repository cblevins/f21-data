Phebe Hinkley here at even. my Girls Washt and piecing Bed quillts. they went to mr Burten*s attended by Moses*s Pollard. it raind Soon after they left home. at home. mr Davis left here. I wroteto my friends. my girls went to mr Burten*s. mrs Barton Came here this night.
