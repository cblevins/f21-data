Clear. I am at mr Lyon*s, his wife is much distresed with pain; it Seems a uterine Complaint, and wind. I administred a Clister which gave her great relief. Shee became pirfectly Easy. I went to mr Prescot*s and larried all night, had the Cramp and Slept but little. Peris Hamlin*s wife there. at ditto, mr Swanton*s & Prescot*s. Daughter LambdsBirth day.
