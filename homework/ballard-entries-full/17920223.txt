Clear & very Pleast. mr Ballard is gone to Survey or run lines for Jerry Babcock & others. Jonathan a is gone to winthrop. mr Walker here, Brot me 2 lb Cottne & 1 lb Chocolat for former Services. mr Ballard returnd without Doing y#e$ Business he went for. mrs Black here, Hannah went with her to mr Densmore*s. I went to mr Livermore*s. Shee is not So well as usual. Dolly Came here, Drank Tea. George Brown Sleeps here, has 2 horses. at mr Livermore*s. mrs Black here. Ihad a Severe fitt of y#e$ Cramp yesterday night.
