Snow. mr Bullin & his Daughter Pally here. I was Calld to see the wife of Jedediah Prescot of Winthrop at 1 h pm by Stephen Pullen; he was 2 & 1/2 hours from home till his return with me. the good Lady was Safe Delivrd at 10 of a fine Son. I tarried all night. at mr diah Prescot*s, Winthrop. Birth13th.
