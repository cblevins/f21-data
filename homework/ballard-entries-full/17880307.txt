Clear & pleas#t$. Cofrin works here. Son Town Came here; informs that Sherebiah is a little Better; his famely all well. mrs Barton is Cleverly. Peirly has been to the meddow for mr Waid. Hannah is gone to Joseph Foster*s to get her Shoes made. Colonel Howard & old Lady, mrs Betsy and Polly, Esqr Pette, Esqr Farewel & Lady*s here. mr Town & Samuel Stackpol Sleep here. I have been at home. it Snow*s This evening.
