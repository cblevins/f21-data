Cloudy & misty morn. mr Dingley Came here when we were at Breakf#t$ to hurry mr Straten. they Sett out Directly for to go on board Captain Springer bound for Boston. it has raind Stedily this Day. I have been at home knitting. at home. mr Straten & Lady went from here. I Sent 6/ by her to purchace me Some Cambric.
