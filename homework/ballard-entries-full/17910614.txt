Clear. I was Calld at 2 h morn to See mr Black*s infant who is unwell, it was a little revivd before I arivd. I gave it a Syrrup of mullin which gave it relief. I went from there, Dind at mr Jones*s. Calld at mr Savage*s & mr Crag*s, his little Daughter is not so well as usual. I receivd a letter of may 23 by Esqr Coney from Sister Waters, informing they are well. at mr Black*s & other Neigbrs. receivda letter from Sister Waters of may 23.
