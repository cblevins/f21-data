Clear. I was Calld by John Wall at 6h ys morn to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 8h morn of her 2nd Daughter & 2nd Child. I left her Cleverly and arivd at home at 12 O Clock [percisly]. I got Dinner and did other matters. Daughter*s Ballard & Pollard took Tea, Sally, Martha & Ephraim allso. at mr John Wall*s. Birth 11th. receivd 9/ as a reward.
