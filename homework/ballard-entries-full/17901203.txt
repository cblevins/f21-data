A Cold Snowy Day. I was Calld at y#e$ 6th hour morn to See mrs Williams. Shee was Safe Delivrd of her 4#th$ Daughter at 1 h Pm, and I returnd home at y#e$ 6th evening. found mr Ballard returnd from his tour of Surveying. I began to foot a pair of hoes for mrs Pollard. I receivd 6/ as a reward. at Asa Williams*s. Birth 31#st$.A Snow.
