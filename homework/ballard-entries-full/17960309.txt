Clear and Cold. I was Calld at 3#h$ morn to See the Lady of Captain Ney who was Deld about 5 of a Son, which wd 13_ lb. it Measured round y#e$ Breast (after being Dres#t$ in thin Cloaths) 18_ inches. I left her and infant Cleverly after breakfast. Calld to See mrs Norcross, find her bu#t$ feeble. mr Ballard went to y#e$ hook, Cyrus been to Pitts#tn$. Nabby washt and went to See her Sister, Phillip is gone to his Father*s. I was at mr Morse*s. at Captain Ney*s and mr Norc#ss$. Birth 11th. John Johnson & his wife Sleep here.
