Clear & Cold. I had a Severe pain in my head yesterday morn, but was Calld to See mrs Caton. Shee has taken Some Cold and has a pain in her hip. I feel much better after I returnd. Dolly is very unwell thro the Day, but Seemd revivd in y#e$ evening. mrs Benjamin Spent y#e$ evening here. at mr Chamberlain*s.
