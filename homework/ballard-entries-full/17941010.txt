Cloudy. at mr Sewall*s, they were intimidated & Calld Dr Page who gave my Patient [50] drops ot Laudenum which put her into Such a Stupor her pains (which were regular & promising) in a manner [Scant] till near night when Shee puk#t$ & they returnd & Shee was Delivd at 7h evening of a Son, her firs#t$ Born. I left her Cleverly at 10 & walkt home. I receivd 12/ as a reward. at ditto. Birth 39th. mr Perham Sleepshere. Dolly is unwell.
