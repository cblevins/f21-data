Clear and very warm. I helped about house forn, had a very Dull turn the middle of the Day. Esqr Coney here yesterday morn. I went to See mrs Dingley a little before night. mr Ballard went and took Tea. we went on Board the Schoner. Pally went to Son Pollard*s, finds <STRIKE>her</STRIKE> my Daughter not very will. Daughter Lambard is very Comfortable. at mr Dingley*s. Pally to Son Pollard*s & Lambard*s.
