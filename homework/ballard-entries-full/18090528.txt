Clear part of the day. I was Calld by Son Pollard at 2h 30m yesterday morn to go and See his wife. we reach#t$ there before 5 and my Daughter was Deld at 6 of a fine Daughter, her ninth Child, and is Comfortable. the Babe wd 11 pounds. Sally Cleark & Pruda Snow Came at evening, there all night. at Son Pollard*s. Birth 5th.
