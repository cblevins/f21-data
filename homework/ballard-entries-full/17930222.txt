Clear & Cold. mrs Child is unwell with a Cold, the infant is teething. I left them after Dining. Calld at mr Hamlin*s, Sally is unwell; at mr Pitts, his Lady is not So well as Shee Could wish; at mr Wesson*s, all well there; at Son Pollard*s. Came from there in a Sleigh. mr Ketth attended me. at ditto & other Neighbor*s.
