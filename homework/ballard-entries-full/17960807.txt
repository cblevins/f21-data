Clear. I am at mr Burn*s, his wife is unwell yet. I was Calld from her to See mrs Robbins who feels her Self unwell, but Seems more Comfortable afternoon. I Dind & took Tea and returnd home at evening, find mrs Pikford, her Sister Nabby and mrs Dingley*s Dear little Daughter here. mrs Welch & children Came here to tarrie yesterday night. at mr Burn*s & mr Robbins*s. mrsWelch & children Sleep here.
