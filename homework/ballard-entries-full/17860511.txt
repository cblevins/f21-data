I was Calld at 4/O Clok to Brian Fletcher*s, his wife being unwell, but got better & her women went home. old mrs Fletcher & I went to Roberts afternoon. I was Calld from there in hast to James Moore*s wife, being in Travill. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a Son at 10 Evinn#g$. Calld from there to mrs Norris*s who had took Cold, adminniStred Something which gave relief.
