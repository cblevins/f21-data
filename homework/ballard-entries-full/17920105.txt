Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard & Ephraim giting wood. mr Bullin & Lady here, he Brot us Beef. Jonathan brot home a goos & 25 lb Beef. I went to mr Shepard*s, Bot 3 Chip hatts & a Sheet past bord for which I paid 3/9 Cash. I Calld at mr Livermore*s & took my ring which mr Greenlief mended. Hannah got her piece out of y#e$ Loom, 15-1/2 yds. I went to the Stores at y#e$hook. Snowd in y#e$ night. mr Bullin & Lady here.
