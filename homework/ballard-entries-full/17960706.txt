Clear and warm. I rose and hoed Some before my Door, was Calld by James Hinkley Junr to go to new france. I went. his wife was Deld of a fine Daughter between 12 and 1 O Clok afternoon, and I returnd. then went to Son Lambard*s. Son Jonathan a and wife & Daughter Pollard there. mrs Capin & Sally Webber were here while I was absent. at James Hinkley*s at new france. Birth34#th$. receivd [8/7]. mr Burten*s Vesil was launchd20 m before I came home.
