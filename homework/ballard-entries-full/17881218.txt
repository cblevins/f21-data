Clear & Cold. mr Ballard attended Town meeting. there was a vote pass#t$ that the Reverend mr Foster Should not preach in the meeting house anymore. mr Brooks here to Settle with me about Bakeing & at the rate he was pleasd to alow me for the trouble there was 15/6 Due to me. I have been at home. the girls wash#t$. Polly has been very poorly. at home. 2 years Since Ephraim had y#e$ first fitt. mr Ben#n$ Branard interd this Day.
