Clear. mr Walker Calld me at 2 h morn to Ben#n$ White*s wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at y#e$ 7#th$ of a Daughter, and I returnd home at 12 O Clok. Calld at mr Waker*s, Jackson*s & Devenport*s. I took a nap after I returnd. Cyrus returnd from Gillbreath*s & went to Pitts town, his Daddy is there. at mr Ben#n$ White*s. Birth [26]#th$.
