Clear and very warm. Son & Daughter Lambard, allin & Barnabas Came here Early yesterday morn, took Breakfast. Daughter Ballard and children to Tea, Gidion Braley allso. we are informd of the death of Bulah & Grefin, Persons of Coulor. I had a turn of faintness after my children left here. at home, part of my children here. I had a faint turn at evening. Deaths.
