Clear. I have been Combing flax, finisht what I have of last years groth, had 24 lb Tear. Cyrus and EbenZ finisht husking our Corn. Cyrus went & got 1 Bushl wheat ground. he informd me that Captain Tobey had a Child lately Deceast. mr Child here, wishes me to go and See his wife who is unwell. Brother EbenZ here. at home.
