Clear after Sun rise. we had a fine Shower after 1#h$ yesterday morn. I left mrs Pitts revivd & returnd home at 10#h$ morn. at Shubael Pitts*s, his wife very ill indead. <STRIKE>30 7</STRIKE> mrs Davis & mrs Porter Sleep herethis night. Rachel Cumings, who is Crazy, here y#s$Day. Broke Down Corn, Beens & other misChief. we Confindher & Sally & I led her to mr Hinkley*s who has the Careof her. Shee is an object of pitty, may God restore her reasonif Consistant with his Divine will. mrs Davis, Porter here. RachelCumings here. Crazy.
