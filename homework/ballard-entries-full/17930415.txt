I went to See mrs Densmore, find her & infant Comfortable. mr Ballard with mr Foster, Noah Woodward & Silva Moore Sett out to run lines on mr Pitts*s land in Pownalboro. mrs Barton returnd from her Sister*s, left her more Comfortable than Shee found her. Shee has been there 3 weaks. at mr Densmore*s. mr Ballard went to Survey. mrs Barton Came here.
