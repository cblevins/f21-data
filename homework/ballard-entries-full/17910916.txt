Cleard of a little after Sun rise. I went to mr Densmore*s, Shee & 3 children Came home with me. Shee Cutt out my gown. Dolly had a tooth Extracted by mr Livermore. mr Peter Jones*s Lady here, tarries all night. Parthenia went to Winthrop, Brot Clarisa home with her. at mr Densmore*s, Shee here, mrs Jones & Clarris#a$ allso.
