Clear. mrs Hinkley was Delivd about 1 h morn of a fine Son. I left her Comfortable at 8, and returnd home. Calld to See mrs Welch, find her as well as Could be Expected, her Sister Cypher*s with her. I was Calld immedialely after my return to See W#m$ Densmore find him very low, his thigh Sweld as much as it was when I saw him before. Jonathan & Ephraim gone to the meddow at Thomas Hinkley*s. Birth 5#th$.
