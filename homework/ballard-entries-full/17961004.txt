A rainy Day. I left my patients Cleverly after Breakfas#t$ and Came to Son Jonathan*s; a very heavy Shower while I was there. my Son Conducted me as far as y#e$ meeting house where we met with mr Dingley, who Desird me to go & See his wife who is very unwell. I Calld there, Shee Seems Some better. I arivd at home at Dusk, feel fatagud. at ditto & Son Jonathan*s & mr Dingleys.Birth 47. receivd 9/ as a reward.
