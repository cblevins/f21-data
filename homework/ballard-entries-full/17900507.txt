Clear & very pleasant. I have a pain in my head by reason of my fatagues. mr Cragen here; took Breakfas#t$; informs me Sherebiah Town is very Low. I was Calld to see mrs Belcher yesterday morn; find her a little more Comfortable Except her Breast which is Soar & painfull yet. I was Calld from there at 3 h pm to See mrs Andrews who is unwell; tarried there all night. at Captain Belcher*s & mr Andrews#S$.Began a Stockin for Foster.
