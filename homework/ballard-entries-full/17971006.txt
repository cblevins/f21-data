Clear morn. I was Calld at 3h m to See the wife of Isaac Savage y#e$ 3#d$, Shee was in Labour, and was Deld at 4 of a Daughter, her Second Child. I returnd home at 6h 30m. mr Ballard & Hoiet went to Jonathan*s. Old Lady Coutch Came here and dind, then went on for home. at Isaac Savage*s. Birth 49#th$.
