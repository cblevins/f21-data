Cloudy, raind at night. mr Famely all attind public worship Except my Self. I was unwell and they did not find the hors till it was too late to go. the Ordinance of the Supper was Administred. mrs Cowan had a Bill for prayers. may God grant her all the Comfort that Shee Stands in need of in this life and may her better part be received at the Close of life to the relms of bliss and happiness. at home. it was Communion day. I receivd a letterfm Brother Collins of april 11#th$, it Came to hand yesterday.
