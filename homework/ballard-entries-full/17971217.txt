Clear and Cold. mrs Moore was Safe Deld at 3h morn of a Son, her 8#th$ Child. Shee has been very ill, but I left her more Comfortable at 10 hours morn, reacht home about 2 pm. Old mr Lambard and Barnabas, and my Daughter & Son Ephraim here at evening. the old Gentleman Sleeps here. at ditto. Birth 54. I receivd 9/ as a reward.Hepsy was 18#tn$ years old yesterday.
