Clear. mr Ballard and his men Sett out on his Survey again, Ephraim with them. I went to See mrs Dickman, find her and infant Cleverly. mrs Jones & mrs Crag & mrs Pollard there. I Calld to See Daughter Pollard, Shee remains Comfortable. Polly Densmore is very feeble yet. Polly Livermore here at evening. a mr Greely Sleeps here. put 1 pair Oxin up to hay. at mr Dickman*s, Son Pollard*s & mrDensmores.
