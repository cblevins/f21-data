Clear. Dolly went to mr Dany*s to work, mr Ballard to Survey for Colonel North. I went to take my leave of mr Dany & wife who left yesterday town and are going to New town. I went to [Godlin] Page*s & paid him 1:18:8 which mr Ballard owd him. I was at other Stores. at mr Dany*s & at the Stores. ElishaPreskott paid me 6/ for assisting his wife Oct#r$ 27#th$.
