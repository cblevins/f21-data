Clear & warm. I returnd home at 9h morn, left my Patients Cleverly. I Sowd Beets, Carrots, Parsnips & Planted Beens & Musk mellons; Sowd Cabbage & French Turnips. Dolly Came home at Ev#n$. Ephraim went to mr Wesson*s, Bot me 1 quart W rhum. mr Hamlin Sent me a Stand to Sett a Light on. at Doe*s. receivd 6/6 as a reward.
