Clear. I have been at home. we roasted a Pigg which was Butcherd yesterday. Daughter Lambard Dind with us. mrs Page & Daughter Calld here. mr Ballard Paid my Daughter for all the work Shee had done for him to this day. we lost a Swine this day. at home. Daughter Lambard Dind with me. I let her have [8 lb] Chees for milk.
