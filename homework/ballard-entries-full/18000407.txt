Clear. mr Ballard, Cyrus and Ephraim went to Town meeting. I have been at home, knit some on my Stockin. Daughter Ballard took Tea here. we are informd that Brother Pollard Expired the 5#th$ inst and that his remains are to be interd tomorrow. I allso hear that mr Kimbal is very Sick with a Complaint in his throat. at home. heard of the Death of mr Pollard.
