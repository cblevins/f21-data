Clear. I am very feeble. Betsy Andrews Came here to help me but dare not Stay by reason of Jack Ballard*s being here with the measels. Sally Farewel Came from my Son*s and did part of my work. my Daughter Came home a little before night, her husband and Shee tarried here all night and took Care of their Son. I got Some rest. at home, very unwell. Jack Ballard Sick here.
