Clear & very warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus attended Coart. I went to See mrs Pitts, find her more feeble than Shee was yesterday; at mr Capin*s, mrs Page very bad with her foo#t$. Dolly was up to mr Pitts*s. Daughter Pollard is about y#e$ house. mrs Coutch & I took Tea there & walkt home together. at mr Pitts, Capin*s & Son Pollards.mr Lambard lost his Case and was [Sentancd] to imprisonment & Cost.
