Clear & Cold. I was Calld to See Savage Bolton*s wife in travil at 7h morn. Shee was Safe Deliv#d$ of a very fine Son at 12 O/Clok. I returnd home at 7#H$ Ev#n$. Left Mother & Child Cleverly. mr Ballard been to Brother Moore*s, returnd at 10#h$, informs that Jacob Foot Buried a Daughter this Day. Eliab Shaw*s wife Came to her Father*s yesterday Day, the most of the way on Snow Shoes. her Husband & children Sick. Death. I was at mr Bolton*s.
