Clear part of the day and very Cold. we have baked Apple & Pumkin Pies. mr Ballard been to y#e$ Settlement, was infmd that Judge Dummer had Chartered & Loaded a vesel for the W. indies and it is frozen into the river. Cyrus was informd that mr Moody, Precepter, Buried 2 children yesterday and is very Sick himSelf. Son Ephraim Sleeps here; he this muinit informd that Charles Dingley*s 2nd Child Expired last night. at home. heard of y#e$ Deathof 2 of mr Moody*s children and one of mr Dingley*s.
