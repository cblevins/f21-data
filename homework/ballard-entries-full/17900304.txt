Clear & very Cold & windy. Suse went from here. I feel very unwell all Day; was Calld after Sun Sett to See mrs Bolton. Shee was Safe Delivrd at midnight of a fine Son. I left her Cleverly & returnd home at 4 O Clok. Jonathan is 27 years old yesterday Day. at Savage Bolton*s. Jonathan is 27 years old yesterday Day. Birth 11#th$.
