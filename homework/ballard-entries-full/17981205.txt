Cloudy. mr Waid Choping wood for us. mr Dingley Calld me to See his wife who is very ill with the rash. I went and Spent the day and night with her. Dr Coney was Calld, he approvd of what I had done. he left her Some C#m$ tart, Niter & Senna. at ditto. Dr Coney was Calld. PollyTown is 17 years old yesterday Day.
