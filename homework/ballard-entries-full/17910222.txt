I returnd after we had took Breakfast, leaving my Patients remarkably Comfortable. after Chatting a little while with my friends, took a walk with them as far as mr Pollard*s. Calld at mr Black*s & Savage*s on my return. Fredrick Foot, his wife & Sister here on their way to mr Bullin*s. mr Pitts here. at ditto. mr Thomas gave me 6/ as areward.
