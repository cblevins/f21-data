A Snow Storm. Son Jonathan, his wife and babe Sett out for Clinton at noon whin it Ceas#t$ Storming but it Came on again. Patty Town and Sarah Farewel took Tea here. Son Pollard, his wife, Rhoda & y#e$ babe gone to Clinton, Samuel allso. I have been Cuting Eyes out of Potatoes, have knit Some. at [home]. Company here.
