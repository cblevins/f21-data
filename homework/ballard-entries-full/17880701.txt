mrs Brown Safe Del#d$ at 4 of a fine Son. I returnd at 9#h$ morn; Left them Cleverly; receivd 6/ as a fee. it is her Sixth Child & 3#d$ Son. my Girls Washing. Daniel Savage Jun#r$ Brot me 7 lb Sugar yesterday for attending his wife at her geting to Bed with her Last Child. we had Six men to work to Day. the Girls went to mr Williams*s. Gillbreath went from here yesterday. at mr Ben#n$ Brown*s. it is 19 years ThisDay Since the Death of my Daughter Dorothy. six mento work yesterday d#y$.
