Cloudy. I and all my famely were at Meeting aflern. I was with my Daughter Pollard foren. Shee Seems more Comfortable, was able to Sett at table and Dind. mr Capin had 2 Daughter*s Baptised & Joseph Stackpol 1. Phillip Bullin Came here at evening. Cyrus is here. at meeting & at Son Pollard*s &mr Hamlin.
