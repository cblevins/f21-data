Cloudy & Some Snow. I was Calld at 2h pm to See the wife of Anthony Bracket who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 5h 30#m$ of her 7th Son & 9th Child. her illness was dificult & very Severe but I left Mother & infan#t$ Comfortable and reacht home before 10 evening. at mr Bracket*s. Birth 4th. receivd 1 Dollar.
