Cloudy till afternoon when the Sun Shone. our men takeing Care of the hay. mr Ballard gone to Sidney to survey for mr Getchel. I havee wash#t$ some of my Cloaths. Sister Williams went to my Son*s. Shee and my Daughter Came and took Tea here. mrs Duttin here mending Cloaths for me. Reverend mr Stone Calld here. at home. mr Ballard gone to Sidney.
