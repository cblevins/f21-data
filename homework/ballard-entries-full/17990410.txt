Cloudy and Sun Shine by turns and Cold. I was Calld at 2h m to go and See the wife of mr Eaton, he livs at Major Stickney*s. the Patient ill thro the Day and was Safe Deld at 8h evening of her 2#nd$ Daughter and 3#d$ Child. I tarried all night. I receivd 9/ of mr Eaton as a reward for my Service. Cyrus finisht Dresing of our flax. (this makes 770 Cases. at mr Eaton*s. Birth 14#th$. Burial ofAnna Partridge.
