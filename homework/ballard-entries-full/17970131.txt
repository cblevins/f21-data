Clear Day, Snowd at evening. mr Dingley brot Nancy here. mr Ballard has been to the fort, brot 10 lb Cottne which he had at Coney*s Store @ 2/10 pr lb. I have been doing house work and nursing a Lamb. Cyrus been choping wood at the door. Son*s Pollard & Lambard with their wives are gone to winslow. at home. mr Dingley brot Nancy here. mr Ballardbot 10 lb Cottne at Coney*s store.
