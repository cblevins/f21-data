Clear and Cold for April. I Came home from mr Wesson*s. Cald at mr Pollard*s. Jonathan is gone to Son Town*s to attend funeral of their Son. I went to Bed but, thinking of my Childrens trouble, Could not compose to Sleep. at 11 Oclok Ev#n$, Wood#d$ Allen Cald me up. went by Land to Colonel North*s; very bad walking. we car#d$ a candle to y#e$ top of Burnt hill. between 12 & 1 arivd at Allen but I was much fataugd.
