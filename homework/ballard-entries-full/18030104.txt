Clear and very Cold foren, more moderate afternoon. I was Calld at 4h morn to See the wife of Anthony Bracket. Shee was, after hard Labour, Safe Deld at 4#h$ 30#m$ evening of her 4#th$ Child and first Daugh#tr$ and I Came home at 7. I spent y#e$ evening knitting. Hannah, William & Sally Ballard Sleep here, their dady & marm gone to Son Lambard*s. at mr Bracket*s. Birth 2#nd$. receivd6/.
