rainy. mr Densmore Came here for me to go & See his little Son. thee rain abated & I went about noon. we adminesterd a Clister, the Child revivd. Betty Complains of pain in her head. Lidia had a fitt of the Collic, Anne is poorly with a Cold. I tarried till near night, then walkt home. Cyrus been to the meddow. mr Ballard, he & Ephraim & John Savage Came home. mr Ballard went to mr Pollard*s on a reference. at mr Densmore*s to See his children whoare unwell.
