Clear. mr Wesson Calld here. I went to See mrs Foster. Shee has been not So well. Came to mrs Williams*s; her Child very Sick; it had fitts. mrs Porter there the afternoon. mrs Pollard & Polly Hinkley & mr Samuel Pierce Stay there. he attended me home. I was at Reverend mr Foster*s & the Widdow Williams.Patty had fitts.
