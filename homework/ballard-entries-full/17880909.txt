A Clear day. thee Town mett to hear Rev#d$ mr Foster*s Proposals but did not Axcep#t$ them. Dolly & Parthenia went to See mrs Hamlin. mrs Savage here. Shee has Spun 40 Double Skeins for me Since April 15#th$, and had 2 Bushl of ashess & Some Physic for James, & Dolly wove her 7 yds of Diapor. I let her have 1 Skein of lining warp. the whole is 6/5 X. Town Meeting. I have been at home. mrs Savage here.
