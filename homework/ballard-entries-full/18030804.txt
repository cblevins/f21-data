Clear & very warm. I have done house work, Polly T. assisted me. Shee went to Crane*s, found their Babe in a fitt, Came and informd me. Jonathan*s wife and I went, it Seemd to revive. I let them have 1 lb Candles. mrs Farewel & Bennet, Son and Daughter Ballard, Betsy Pierce, Hannah & W#m$ took Tea. <STRIKE>mr Ballard been to meddow. </STRIKE> at mr Crane*s; had Company here.
