I went to mr Capin*s, find her mending; to mr Abbot*s, his wife is Cleverly; to mr Hamlin*s, his wife is unwell; at mr Dickman*s, receivd 9/ on Ben#n$ Branch*s acoun#t$ for assisting his wife in Child Birth. I Calld at mr Smith*s and took Tea, went and Slep#t$ at Son Pollard*s. I was in great Danger by a fall from the hors but Escap#t$ unhurt thro Gods preserving mercy. at Son Pollard*s and other Neighbor*s. a rainy night. preservd when in Danger by a fall from a hors.
