Clear till near night when it raind. mrs Lashnee*s was Safe Deld at 5h morn of a fine Son which w#d$ 11_ lb. I tarried with her till 6h pm, left her Cleverly as Could be Expected. I receivd [7/4#d$] for my Service. Calld to See mrs Dummer, find her Cleverly. mr Ballard Sett out on his Tour of Surveying. Ephraim went with him to take the hors home. at mr Lashnee*s. Birth 38#th$.mr Ballard left home to Survey 4 Townships.
