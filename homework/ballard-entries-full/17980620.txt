Clear and hott fore noon. I went to mr Bell*s, find her more Comfortable. I was at Lewis Hamlin*s, his wife is Cleverly; at mr Crag*s, Peggy very low. I Calld at mr Heartford*s, his wife Deld of two Dead Babes by Esqr Coney this morn, one of Each Sex. at mr Bell*s and others. Birth. mrs Heartford twins,a Son & Daughter.
