Snowd. mr Burten Came to Bracket*s for me about Day to go & See his wife who is in lab#r$. Shee was Deld at 11h 5#m$ fore#n$ of a Son. I Dind there, was Calld back to mr Bracket*s, his wife in Labour & was Deld at 10h evening of a Son, her firs#t$ born. Shee was very ill. I tarried all night. mr Bracket gave me 6/7 as a reward. mrs Bolton Slep#t$ there, the other women went home. at mr Burten*s & Bracket*s. Births45#th$ and 46th. recd 6/7 of Bracket.
