Clear. mrs Devenport was Deld at 40 muinits after midnight of a Son, her firs#t$ Child. Shee was very ill, her Case Somewhat Singular, but I got through with Safety thro the Divine aid, and left her Cleverly; y#e$ infant allso, and returnd home at 8h morn. I went to See my Daughter Pollard afternoon, find her Cleverly. I tarried with her all night. Shee and infant Slept well. at mr Devenport*s. Birth 25th. fee recd.at Son Pollard*s afternoon. mr Ballard Came home.
