Clear. I Came home, did Some washing, Widdow Savage Came here near night. I was Soon after Calld to go and See mrs Duttin who is in Labour and was safe Deld at 7h Ev of a Son, her Second Child. Shee is as well as Could be Expected as Shee has the measels. I Sett with her till aft mid nigh#t$, mrs Springer afterwards. at mr Andrews*s. Birth 8th. Novr 15#th$ recd 6/7 of mr Andrews.
