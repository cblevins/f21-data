Clear. I was Calld Early this morn to See thee Reverend mr Foster*s Lady who is very unwell; her Breast is Likely to Break. I aplyed a Poltis of Sorril & returnd home. mrs Williams & Polly Cool & mr Savage here after n#n$. Daniel Robbins Left here Bound for the westward. Dolly Slept with mrs Williams. I was at mr Foster*s & Widdow Williams*s. my Girls washt thee floars, Parthenia wrote Lettersto her friends westward.
