Clear and pleast. mr Ballard & Cyrus attended worship, Polly and my Self have Colds and did not. mr Lambard & wife and the Babe here after meeting. mrs Rice Sent for me to See her, Shee being very ill. mr Ballard Conducted me there. I gave her Melleot Tea which brot on perspiration ang gave releaf and I returnd. Calld to See Son Pollard*s children who are unwell. at mr Rice*s. Son & Daughter Lambarthere.
