Clear and pleasant. my Brother EbenZ Moore Dind here, he informs me that mrs Pillsbury Expired this morn. Daughter Lambard Came here with her babe, her husband Came to See her home. Hussey and his uncle Sleep here. Son Ephraim is 18 years old this day. I have been at home, done but little work. at home, Son & Daughter Lambard here. Son Ephraim is 18 years old this Day.
