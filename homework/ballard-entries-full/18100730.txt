Cloudy and Some rain. I have done various kinds of wk. Allen Came here afternoon, very unwel with A pain in his head. I put Burdocke to his feet, bathd his temples with Camphr. he Seems Easier. Ephraim Came & Sleeps with him. mr Ballard been to Son Ephraim*s, brot home 2/1 a hundred of flower which Captain Bastow of Rochester Sent us. at home. Allen here unwel. Son Ephraim gave us 2/1lb Tea.
