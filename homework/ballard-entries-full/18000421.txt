Clear morn. mrs Caton was Deld of her 3#d$ Son and 7#th$ Child at 5h yesterday morn, and I returnd homewd as far as Son Pollard*s at 9h morn; tarried and dind. Cyrus met me there and informd me that Hepsy Brown was Sick. I reacht home before 3h Pm, find her very ill. Son Jonathan*s wife and Lydia Nud were with her. we used means to promote perspiration. Daughter Ballard Sett up with her. at ditto and Son Pollard*s. Birth 13th. all wella#t$ Son Pollard*s & Lambard*s.
