Cloudy. I was at mr Cowan*s, his wife was in Labour all Day. mrs Savage and Swanton went to See the Ship Monlezumaum Launcht. we had 11 children there thro the Day. Shee Calld <STRIKE>the Day </STRIKE> Calld [sic] her women at evening and was Safe Dild at 11h evening of a fine Daughter. I tarried thro the night, my Patients Cleverly. at ditto. Birth 23#d$.
