Clear. I was Calld at 9#h$ morn to See Jotham Smith*s wife who was in Labour & was Safe Delivd of a fine Son at y#e$ 12#th$ h. I returnd home at 3#h$ pm then went to See mr Savage. find him Some more Comfortable. the Girls gone to See mrs Hamlin this Eving. Cyrus been to the hook to Day. at mr Smith*s & Neighbor Savage*s.
