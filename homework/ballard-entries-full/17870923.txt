I fited for going to meeting but was Calld to See mrs McMaster*s who is unwell. mrs Cowan & I administred remedys that Dr Coney prescrib#d$. her physic began to operate. I left her, returnd as far as mrs Williams*s, tarried with her till 10 in Evnng, Shee being Exercisd with much pain, but got easier & I Came home.
