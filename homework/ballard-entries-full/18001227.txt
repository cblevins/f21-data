Cloudy all day and very moderate, raind at night. mr Ballard makeing a plan foren, went to a Parish meeting afternoon. Cyrus went to Son Lambard*s, brot home 2 Bushel of Corn, then went to mill with part of it. Dr Barton Came at evening, Sup#t$ & Sleeps here. I have brot my Beef ou#t$ of the Sellar and repacked it. Boiled the pickel &C, &c. at home. Dr Barton Sleeps here.
