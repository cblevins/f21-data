I attended Lecter. went from meeting to mr Sewall*s to See Captain Sewall*s Foot, it being Scolt. Drank Tea their. James Moore Calld me from there to go to Simeon Cleark*s wife, Shee being in travil. was Safely Del#d$ of a Daughter at 11 / OClock Eving. I tar#d$ all night, mrs Peter Clerk also. I have recievd 6/ & there is 2/ Due.
