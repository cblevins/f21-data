Clear. mrs Duttin went home this foren. I went to my Son*s, find W#m$ & Sally very unwell. I returnd, prepared my Self and went to Lecture; a mr Abbot performd. I Calld at mr Andrews*s as I came home & at my Son*s. the children Seeme Some more Comfortable. my Son Ephraim is not well. at my Son*s, at Lecture & at mr Andrews*s.
