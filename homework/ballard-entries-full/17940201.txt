Clear. I returnd from mr Williams*s, left my Patients as well as Could be Expectd. Calld to See mrs Hersey, Shee is Comforlable. I have receivd 9/ of Captain Hersey as reward for my Servec & 15/ of mr Williams. I Calld at mr Parker*s, his Lady made me a pres#t$ of 1_ yds ribbin, her infant is better. at ditto, Captain Hersey*s &mr Parker*s.
