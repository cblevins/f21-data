Clear & pleasant. mr Ballard is gone to measure the road from this Side mr Crag*s to y#e$ top of y#e$ hill North of here. I have finisht a pair of hoes for my Self. I am informd Captain Read is arivd with a larg quantity of Corn. the river opend as low as mr Livermore*s. mr Jones Crost at Pollard*s Ferry with his hors & 2 Cows. Ephraim went to mr Woodward*s for gardin Seeds. I was at home. finisht my Stockins. the river Opend. Death mrs Sherburn*s Child.
