Clear. I Came home afternoon, found Dolly Better. Polly Taylor & Lucy Porter here. Dr Coney here from mr Brown*s, Says he is very poorly. mr Ballard Been to y#e$ hook, Carrid thee plann to mr Vahan, receivd his pay. Came home yesterday Evinng. Jonathan returnd from Sandy river, Informs that mrs Eaton*s Father Deseast Last w#k$ & was interd on Sunday Last.
