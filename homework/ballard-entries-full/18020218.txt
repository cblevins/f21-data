Cloudy & windy. I was Calld at 4h morn to See Peggy Whealer, Shee was Deld of a Daughter at 9 which Shee declared [ ] Gates was the Father of. I came home at 12 O Clock, tried the lard. A mr Meril of N. Gloster, his wife, Robert Forbes and Fanny Sleep here. his marm made me a pres#t$ of a pair of mitts. at Saunders. Case 3#d$. receivd 9/ ofL. Hamlin. had Compy.
