Cloudy morn, a Shower foren. Clear and very pleast afternoon. I was Calld at 7h morn to See mrs Black who was in Labour. I find her very ill. Shee was Safe Deld between 8 & 9 of a very find Daughter, her 5th Child. I tarried till afler Dineing then left her and infant Cleverly as Could be Expected. mr Black bestowed 12/ as a reward. Thankfull got my web out, 24_ yds. at mr James Black*s. Birth 42#nt$.I recd 12/ as a reward. Thankfull Godfray went to mr Dingleysto Board.
