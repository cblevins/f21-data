Cloudy. I was Calld at 6h morn to See mrs Pillsbury who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 1#h$ pm of a fine Daughter. I went from there to Dr Colman*s Store, bot 2 oz Spermaseta @ /4_ /9. Do Cammomile. y#d$ vial & Salts, & Box Snuf /8. from there to See mrs Child, find her better. I returnd and took Tea, receivd 6/ and then Came home, attended by mr Pitts*s Boy. I left Mother and infant Cleverly. at mr Pillsbury*s & Childs. Birth 50th. receivd6/ as a reward. EbenZ [Cool] Left here.
