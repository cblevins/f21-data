Clear & very pleas#t$. I was Calld between 12 & 1 yesterday morn to See mrs Waid. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a Daughter, which wd 9-1/2 lb, at y#e$ 3#d$ h. I returnd at y#e$ 9#th$. Left all Cleaverly. Calld to see mrs Hodge & mrs Black. was Calld at 10 to See mrs Hamlin, find her very poorly. I tarried all day, Parthenia also. Shee tarries y#e$ night. Clarisa & Taylor Sleep here, Burten also. at mr Waid*s & Hamlin*s.
