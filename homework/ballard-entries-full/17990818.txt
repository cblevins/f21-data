Clear. I felt unwell foren. Hepsy very bad with the Poison. I went to Son Lambard*s afternoon, find her not So well. Shee has a pain in her head. their infant was Carried and Presented in Baptism, his name Calld Thomas. mr Whitwel*s Son was Calld W#m$. I returnd home at evening, my Daughter Some Easier. at Son [Lambard*s], his Babe Baptisd. its name Thomas.
