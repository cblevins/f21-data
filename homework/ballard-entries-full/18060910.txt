Clear and Cool. I have been at home, Polly Town with me till near noon. Two men from Hamdin took Breakfast here. Widdow Savage Dind, Shee & Daughter Pollard took Tea. mr Miller & Philbrook*s paid mr Ballard 4 Dol#s$ & 2#s$ for the busines he did for them. a Gentleman by y#e$ name of Devenport Came for information about land, he gave him a Crown. at home. mrs Savage, Daughter Pollad, mr Miller, Philbrookand A mr Devenport been here this day.
