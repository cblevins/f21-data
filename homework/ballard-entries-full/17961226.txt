Clear and not So Cold. I Came home at noon. I receivd 9/10_ for my Service. I was Calld Directly to See mrs Dingley who has taken Cold and is Exercised with various Complaints. I tarried there this night, made use of remidies which gave Some relief. at ditto & at mr Dingley*s. receivd of mr Thwing9/10_. mr Waid worked here.
