Cloudy morn rain and wind after Sun rise. I was Calld at the first h morn by W#m$ Brooks Esqr to See the wife of John Brooks who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 3/4 hour after 4 of a Daughter, her first Born, mrs Bond & Williams were Calld as assistants, I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd at my home at 11h 30m. I receivd 9/ Cash and a pair Shoes @ 6/-15 as a reward. I put my house in order and Cook#t$ a Dinner. mr Ballard went to the fort and Cyrus to the hook after I Came home. at Esqr Brooks. Birth 6 th. receivd15/ as a reward.
