Clear and pleasant. I was Calld from mrs Moore to Steven Hinkley*s wife at 10h morn, Shee was Deld at 11 of a Son. I part Drest the infant and was Calld to return to mrs Moore, find her more unwell. Shee was Deld at 4h 30#m$ of a Son. the children were the firs#t$ Born of their momys. I returnd home at 8h evening. Brother EbenZ Moore Sleeps here.
