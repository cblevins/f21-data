Clear, a frost this morn. I have been at home, baked and did other matters. Daughter Ballard here & 4 children, informs me my Son is gone down Expecting to go to Jail. I am very Sorry he is So much hampered. we are informd that Captain Cox Expired yesterday very Sudently. may I be prepard for that Chang when ever it may take place. at home, Daughter Ballard here.
