Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus to meeting all day. I attended aftern#n$, and was Calld by mr Young to go and See the wife of John Dun who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 7#h$ 30 evening of a fine Son. I tarried there all night. Shee and Child are Cleverly. mr Dun was married last Thursday, he was 20 years old last July. at Meeting and with mrs Dun. Birth 38th. recd9/ Novr 23.
