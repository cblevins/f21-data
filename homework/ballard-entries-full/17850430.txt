Chilly. I was at home. Brother Moore & mr Haws, mr Forbes & wife & Peggy, Dind here & Captain Hatch, mr Edson, also mrs Savage, Drank Tea. I had three Brooms of mr Smith. Let him have 3:4#d$ in cash & 1 lb of Coffee. Our Saw mill stopt by ye Back water. A Great Cry for provision. no Vesils arived yet. ye ice run this Day.
