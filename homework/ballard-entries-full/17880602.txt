Clear. I went to mr Williams*s. Sally is no Better. I went to See mrs Porter. Receivd my fee of y#e$ Captain in things in The Store 7/. I Came back to Williams. Sally had an ill Turn. we were apprehensive it was Near Expireing. the Neighb#s$ were Called in; it had revivals through the night. I tarried all night, Dolly also, & mr Crag*s till 2#h$ morn. Hannah Cool*s Came there & was very faint & poorly. mrs Williams had fitts. I Watcht at mr Williams*s.
