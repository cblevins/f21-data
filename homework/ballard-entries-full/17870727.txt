Rainy. I was Calld to Joseph Foster*s to See Lidia, Sick with the rash. Came back to mr Pollard*s, went from thence to fort western; attended funeral of Peggy Cool. from that to George Brown*s. Saw his wives hand Drest. returnd home at Ten in evening. Exceedingly fatagud. J.B. here. mrs Sherman Brot to mr Pollard*s by Harford of winslow. mrs Frich here ys night.
