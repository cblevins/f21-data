Clear and warm. Son Jonathan bro#t$ his Daughter here. mr Ballard and I attended Lecture, there were but a small number there. I Calld at Son Pollard*s, find little Hannah in distress by Costivness. we used means which relievd her. we Calld at Son Lambard*s, find them all abou#t$. my Daughter Seems not well. I removd my Rhue and Camomile from where the were in to the Gardin. little Jack Ballard Said if it Should die you will know better nex#t$ time. at Lecture & Calld to See my Child#n$.mr Dingley returnd from Boston.
