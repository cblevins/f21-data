Clear. I returnd home attended by John Chamberlain. Calld at Son Jonathan*s, his wife is very unwell; at Peter Jones*s and Son Lambard*s, they are well. find my famely well on my return. Nabby has made my Soap Since I left home. I found 80 flees on my Cloaths after I Came home, which I brot from mr Caton*s. at ditto and others.
