Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Partridge*s breast, it is very much Sweld but not broke yet. Son Town Came here at evening, his famely are well, his Widdow Daughter*s allso. he informd that Daughter Lambard had a fainting fitt last night. mrs Burten Sent me 10 oz of Sugar & 2 of Tea by mrs Stone. at [mr Partridge*s. Son Town Sleeps here.]
