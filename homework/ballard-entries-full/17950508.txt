Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard at Esqr Coney*s. Daughter Ballard and her children went home. Dolly and Sally washt & went to mr Gill*s. mr Prince Baker here. I was Calld to See Captain Molloy*s infant, it is unwell. we gave it Rhubarb. I tarried all night. at Captain Molloy*s. Daughter Ballard went home,Son Town Slept here.
