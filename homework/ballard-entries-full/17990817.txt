Clear part of the Day. mr Ballard got wood, Cyrus went to Pitts t#n$. I went to the hook, Calld to See mrs Pickford, find her Comfortable. her infant has a Soar mouth. I was at mr Burgess, her Breast Soar; at mr Martin*s, his wife scouring a floor. I was at mr Norcross*s & Hinkley*s, all well there. at mr Pickford*s and other Neighbor*s. yarn Daughter Pollard had Spun for me in 1799. Lining 29_ skeins, Tow 13 Skeins. [sic].
