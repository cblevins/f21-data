Clear. Captain Hersey Calld me at the 5th hour morn to See his Lady who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 6h 30m pm of a fine Son. I had mrs Thomas & mrs Voce as assistants. I tarried all night, my Patients were very Comfortable. mr Voce Supt there. at Captain Hersey*s. Birth 9#th$. I receivd 6/. Tolman a Son Born.
