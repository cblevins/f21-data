Clear & Pleasant. we were informd that mr Pollard had broke his Legg this morn. I am Calld to See mrs Burten who is unwell. Son Town is Coming with Loggs, our men gone to meet him, it is 12 OClok. mrs McKethney, mrs Black, Judy Heuston, Polly Pollard, Neighbor Savage & wife took Tea here. mr Straten, Son Town & Robs#n$, Dexter Dind here. my Daughter & famely well. Biah Declineing, Nathan Haywad mending. at home. mr Pollard Broke his Legg.
