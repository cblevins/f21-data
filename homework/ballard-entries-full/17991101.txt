Clear forn, Cloudy afternoon and Cold. I did my house work. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to our house, Carried the ash Box, a Bed and bedstead, and Beets & F. Turnips & Gardin roots. mr Ballard and I attended Lecture. we were invited to take Tea with Colonel North. mrs Dingley and mrs Pattin here when I was gone. I pikt wool yesterday evening. at Lecture. the text is PSalm 4#th$, 4 vrs. we took Tea at Colonel North*s.
