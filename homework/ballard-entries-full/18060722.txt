Clear. I have worked in my Gardin & Done my house wk, baked tarts. Allen Lambard here, Says his folks are well as usual. Patty Town Came at evening, all well at her Father*s. I was [Calld by] mr Stilson to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld about midnight of a Daught#r$, her firs#t$ Born. I went with Old Lady Hamlin at 2h 30m to Theophelus Hamlin*s, Slept till morn. at mr Stilson*s. Birth 3#d$.Patty Town Came here at evening.
