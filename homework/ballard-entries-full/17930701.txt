Clear & very warm. mr Ballard went to mr Dummer*s for money which mr Pitts Sent him. Dolly & Sally went to the Stores for things. I went to See mr Beadle, find him very poorly. mrs Lang was here while I was gone. Samuel Philbrook hurt in a Saw mill. Nathan Burten*s Child Drowned. at mr Pitts & Son Pollard*s.
