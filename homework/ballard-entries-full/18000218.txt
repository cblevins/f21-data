Clear and very Pleast. mr Ballard, my Self, Cyrus, Jonathan, his wife and 2 oldes#t$ Son*s attended at the meeting house. heard prayers at the funeral of George Warin Esqr. he was interd in the masonic mode, a Number of the Brithren of that Society were present. I Calld and visited mrs Crag foren. at mr Crag*s & at funeral of Esqr Warin.
