Clear and Cold. mr Ballard, my Self, Ephraim, Daughter Ballard & Hannah to meeting. the Ordinance of the Supper administred, there were 7 male & 14 female members Communed. I went to Son Lambard*s, their Babe is very unwell. Dolly Kembal returnd here. at meeting & Son Lambard*s. Dolly K. Came hereat evening.
