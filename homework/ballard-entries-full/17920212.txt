Cloudy morn. I was Calld between midnight & 1h m to See mrs Randal, who was Safe Deld at 1h of a fine Son. Shee Sufferd before my arival but the life of Mother & Child were preservd. I tarried all night, receivd my fee in Pork, 18#lb$, & 4#lb$ Lard for former attendance. I left the Lady & infant as well as Could be Expected. Calld to See mrs Fillebrown who I find Cleverly. a mr Randal*s. Birth 12th.
