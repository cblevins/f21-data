Clear. I have got in matters from my Gardin. Cyrus more unwell. Daughter Ballard, mrs Butlar, Farewel took Tea here. Ephraim Came home very Sick. I got warm watir to Bath his feet & made him Catnip Tea with peneriol. the wife of S. J. Foster Expired at 3h yesterday morn. at home. Ephraim Came home Sick at evening.
