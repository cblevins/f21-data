Cloudy. mr Ballard Sett out at 11#h$ morn to go and assist W#m$ Brooks & Ephraim Town aprise and [Divide] 2000 acrs of tand which lies between this [and] penobscott river to 7 heirs of the late Dr Geneson. Brother & Sister Barton and their Son Luke left here after Dinner to go to house keeping at mr Crag*s Shop. I wish them prosperity. Cyrus and Ephraim have been makeing fence. I have been to Son Jonathan*s with my Sister as Shee wint down. at Son Jonathan*s. Dr Barton, his wife& Luke went from here to house keeping.
