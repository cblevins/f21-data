Clear and warm. Son Lambard Calld me at 2h this morn to See his wife who is very ill and has been thro the night. I Spen#t$ the day with her, Daughter Pollard allso. I felt fatagud and Came home at evening. Cate McLad is with her this night. mr Lambard took a man by the name of Hamelton for thieft and Commited him to Goall. he has taken from Several People about the fort, and Sundry articles. may he Sincirely repen#t$ and God Graciously pardon him. O how I pity his Bosom friend, if he has one. at Son Lambrts, my Daughter very Sick.
