Cloudy. mr Ballard up to our house, Cyrus went afternoon. I went to Hollowell to Call for Some arearages but did not receive anything but promises. mrs Molloy removd from her habitation by the Academy to mr Martin*s. mrs Clap and Daughter took Tea with me. Hepsy Brown returnd to liv with me. David Robbins Sleeps here, he and my husband reaconed. at Hollowell.
