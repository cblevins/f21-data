Clear. I was Calld about mid night to John Shaw*s wife who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 8h yesterday morn of her 2#nd$ Child and Daght. I returnd home about noone. mr Ballard went to Town meeting. Magr Samuel Howard was Chosen to represent yesterday Town in general Coart. at John Shaw*s. Birth 2#nd$. June 16#th$ receivd 7#S$ and 6#d$ of mr Shaw.
