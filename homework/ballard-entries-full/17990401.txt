Snowd Some. I have been with mrs Savage this day. Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30#m$ of a Son, her twelfth Child. I left them as well as Could be Expected about 8 and returnd home. I Calld at Son Pollard*s, find all well Except Rhoda who has had a pain in her Ear. at mr Savage*s. Birth 12th. recd 9/ of Th#s$Hamlin July [8].
