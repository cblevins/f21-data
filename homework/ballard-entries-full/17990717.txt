Clear. mr Ballard to Coart. Daughter Lambard Came to make ma Black Silk Gown. I was Calld at 12 O Clok & 15 m to go and See the wife of Isaac Stone who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 6h pm of her 3#d$ Son. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected after 8, and got Safe home. Daughter Lambard walkt home attended by her husband. Son Town Sleeps here. at Isaac Stone*s. Birth 28th. recd 12/ as a reward.
