Clear morn; Showers afternoon. I was at Widdow Williams*s all Day. we thought the Child was Expireing. the Neighbor*s Calld but it revivd. we administred Clysters prepard with English mallows & Camamile, which operated & relaxed the Bowels. I lift it Easy and returnd home at evening. Hannah been to mrs Wesson*s, Parthenia & Dolly there Last Satterday to help her quillt a Bed quilt. [Guild] Partridge Came here. at ditto. I hear that old mrs Hutchens of winthropDeparted this Life Sudenly this Day.
