Clear till near night when it raind. I went to meeting, it was Communion. we had as full a meeting as I have Seen lately. Ephraim went to Son Lambard s after meeting, Allen went home with him. Samuel Trask took Tea here. I was informd that Old Lady Coutch was disceased. Esqr John Davis & his Bride were at meeting. Hains Learned here at evening. at meeting, Sacrament day. interment of Old mrsCoutch. Esqr Davis & Bride at meeting.
