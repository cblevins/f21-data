Clear and Cool. I have been Doing my house work and Nursing my Cow, her Bag is amazeingly Sweld. Derias went from here. mr Ballard and Ephraim got wood home. Cyrus Cleand wheat and went to the fort. Sarah went to watch with Mary Densmore. Dr Williams Came there. Shee returnd, and Sarah Densmore with her, at 11h evening. I have been Picking wool till then. A womans work is Never Done as y#e$ Song Says, and happy Shee whos Streng#th$ holds out to the End of y#e$ rais. it is now near y#e$ middle of the night and mr Densmore Calls me to his house. I Sett up till Day. at mr Densmore*s. Dr Williams thereto See his Daughter Mary.
