Clear and Cool. I left mrs Guild an hour by Sun, returnd home. mr Ballard is gone to Sidny. I went to mr Andrews*s, found them very Distressed. helped remove Nabby. we put her Bed out and I made her as Clean as I Could and tended her till evening, when I left. mrs Cowan, Ingraham & Cypher to watch.. at mrs Guild*s & Andrews*s and Brownns.Son and Daughter Pollard Sleep here.
