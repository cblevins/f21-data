Election at Boston; very rainy. mr Ballard Came to mr Williams*s for me this morn. I left them very poorly. we had veal for Breakfast; the first we have had yesterday Spring. I took a nap afternoon. Parthenia & Dolly went to See Sally. Cyrus went at evening. Shee remains very Sick indeed. Captain Porter returnd from Bst#n$.Nath#l$ Brown was Drowned.
