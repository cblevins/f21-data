Clear and warm. mr Ballard has been to Son Jonathan*s and to Hollowell. Old Lady Hamlin had a pigg of us. Son Lambard killd and Drest one for to roast. I have baked & Brewd and mended Cloaths for Cyrus, but do not feel So well as I Could wish. Sally has Spun 1 Skein and mended Some Stockins & Nurs#t$ her Child. at home. we killd a Pigg which w#d$ 14lb.
