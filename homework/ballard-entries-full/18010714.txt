Clear till near night when we had a Shower. Sally Selvester left me. I feel very unfit to do without help. mrs Dingley Came to see me afternoon. Shee made me a prest of A q#t$ Spirit and Some hyson Tea. Cate Pitts, Nabby Page, Polly Cowan & Lucy Stone Calld in when the Shower. at home. mrs Dingley here. Sally left me, we paidher all her wages.
