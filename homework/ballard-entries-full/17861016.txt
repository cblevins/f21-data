A Clear Day. Polly went home. mr Davis went from here with mr Bullin. mr Ballard is takeing Valluation. y#e$ Girls went from home at 12 O Clok to take Leav of Polly Hamlin, Shee being Bound for oxford. I wrote a Letter to Deacon Davis, 1 to Brother Collins & 1 to Sister Waters, which I Sent by them for to be Carried by mr Hamlin. I have Bak#t$ for mr Brooks & baked Some mins Pies. Ben#n$ Brown work#t$ here.
