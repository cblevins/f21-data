Sherebiah Town Came here for me to go to mr Dex[ters] up Sebestacook. I Left home at four O/Clock P.M. arivd there a little before Twelve. found mrs Dexter very ill with thee Mumps. fixt her a remedy and Shee mended soon. I tarried there & at Son Town*s till october y#e$ sixth, then came home by water attended by Captain Hail & Sherebiah Town. they brought a bar#l$ of fish from mr Straten*s for us. Daughter Town*s babe been unwell, Some [better].
