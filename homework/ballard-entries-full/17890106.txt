Clear. Dolly & Clarisa washt. mr Ballard went to Collect Rates. I was at Reverend mr Foster*s & mr Wesson*s. Jonathan is unwell. Parthenia is Better, Ephraim also. I Calld at mr Davis*s & left 3/ for mrs Anne Davis, it being all that was Due to her for a Cloak I had of her. at Reverend Foster*s & other Neighb#rs$.
