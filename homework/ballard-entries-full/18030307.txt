Clear. I was Calld up about mid night to go to mr Thompson*s, wrode beyond mr Edson*s when I was met and informd the patient was Delivered. I then returned home attended by mr Edson, arivd at 2h morn, very Cold. I have been mending Cloaths. Lidia washt. a mr Lathrop Came to work for Son Ephraim. Set out to go to mr Thompson*s.
