Clear and warm. I have washt Some wool, my Buttery and other matters. was Calld at 3h pm by mr Orock to See his Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 4 of a fine Daughter, her first Child by her present Husband. I left them as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 6, did my work. at mr Orock*s. Birth 10th. receivd 2 Dols as Areward.
