Clear. mrs Branard was Deld of a fine Son at 6h morn, I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and returnd to Son Jonathan*s at 9h, find him more Comfortable. he Conducted me home afternoon, I was not Long there, when he Came and Carried me to See the wife of David Pollard, who Seems unwell. at mr Branard*s. Birth 8 th. receivd 9/. as Son Jonathan*s and David Pollard*s.
