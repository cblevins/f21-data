Cloudy and rain. Son Lambard Conducted my Daughter Ballard and mrs Gill home and mrs Conrey there to Nurs. I returnd home at 5h pm. w [sic] had heavy thunder and rain afterwards. I left my Child Cleverly. Shee has been out of bed twice and had her bed made. mr Hollowell had Barn burnt by Lightning, [with] 30 tons hay & 3 Cows. at Son Lambard*s. mrs Conrey Came to nurs.
