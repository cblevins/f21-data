Snowd & raind. Daughter Lambard and Mary Butterfield Came here foren, my Son Lambard Came at Eveng and Conducted his wife home in a Sleigh. mr Ballard been to the fort & hook. my Son Jonathan is 34 years old this Day. at home. This is the Aneversary of mySon Jonathan*s Birth. he has Seene 34 years. may he,as inyears,increass in Virtue.
