Cloudy morn, Clear afternoon. mr Ballard has been Surveying for mr J. Jones. Cyrus tending Mill for P. Jones, my Daughter*s & Parthenia to help mrs Hinkley quillt. I have been at home, took Care of my pickeles, gathering Seeds & knitting. at home. my Girls went to mr Hinkley*s. mrs ShawSent me Some Vinegar.
