Cloudy. I was Calld at 4h morn by Gen#l$ Sewall to See his Lady who was in Labour. Shee had her pains gradually till the insewing morn. mrs Livermore went with me, we found the wife of Thomas Sewall there. I went to See mrs Comings, find her in a low State, her Complaint is in her throat. at Gen#l$ Sewall*s & Solom#n$ Cumings.
