Clear & warm. mrs Bisbe, Haywad & my Dagty Hannah went to See mrs Fillebrown yesterday foren, to Son Jonathan*s afternoon. mrs Nancy Cleark Dind here, mr Livermore Supt. mr Ballard finisht weading his Corn. Ephraim attempted going to mill By water, got as far as mr Shepard*s. thee wind agains#t$ him, he left his Canoe & Corn and his Daddy went & brot it home again. I have been at home, worked in my Gardin & did house work; feel much fatagud. at home. had Compa#y$.
