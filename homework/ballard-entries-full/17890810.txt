Clear Except a sprineling of rain about noon. I went with mrs Emerson as far as mr Crag*s, Drank Tea there. was Calld by James Moore to See mrs Shearborn who was very unwell. I tarried all night, gave the Lady Some medisin. Shee Seemd mutch more Comfortable. I left her at 2 h pm & returnd home, find I had a Call to go Elswhere. at mr Crag*s & mr Sherborns. Calld at Reverend mr Foster*s. Levy Moore & wife here.
