Clear & Cold. mrs Hovey was Safe deliverd of a fine Son at 1#h$ morn. I returnd home at half after 1#h$ pm. Left my Patient Cleverly, the Babe was not So well as I could wish. I receivd 2 lb & 10 oz of Butter and 1 lb 15 oz of Cheese in part for my fee. July 22 receivd the remainder of my fee in Butter.
