Cloudy and Some Snow. mrs Williams was Safe Deld at 3h pm of her 6th Daughter and 7#th$ Child. I left her Cleverly and Came to Son Jonathan*s where I tarried all night. he has been to our house with all his children, left Jack and brot the others home. this is y#e$ Aniversary of my mariage, 44 yrs. at mr Asa Williams*s.
