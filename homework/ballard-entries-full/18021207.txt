Clear. I have done my house work. mrs Farewel Calld here. I went to my Son*s, find mrs Gill and Daughter Lambard there; they Came home with me. Ephraim & Allen Came and Slept here. Jason Pierce & wife at my Son*s. Son Cyrus been to Reedfield for Rachel Allen, but did not obtain her. mr Gill, Son*s Lambard & Jonathan here at evening. at ditto. Daughter Lambard & mrs Gill here.
