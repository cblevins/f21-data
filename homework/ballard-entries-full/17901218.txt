Clear and very Cold. Dolly is unwell, took Soap pills, had Several faint turns. I went to See Polly McKethney, find her very low in Strength and Spirrits. Dr Colman let blood in both her feet. I was Calld at y#e$ 8th h evening in hast to See mrs Brown. Shee was Safe Deld at 9 h 30 m of a fine Daughter, her 7th Child. mr Brown was absent. mrs Woodward and my self tarried all night. at mr Crag*s & Brown*s. Birth 32#nt$.Death Polly Taylor.
