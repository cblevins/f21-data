Cloudy. mr Ballard Sett out for Monmoth to Survey. I have been at home mending hoes and doing house work. Dolly went to her marms. I paid her 6/7. Son Jonathan a and all his children, Except Hannah, took Tea. Brother Moore*s Son James and Jack & Le faett Ballard Sleep here. at home. mr Ballard gone to Monmoth. Jethro Gardnerhere.
