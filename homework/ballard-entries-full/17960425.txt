Clear, a frost this morn. mr Ballard went to Varsalboro to Survey. I have been at home. Captain Abram Page*s wife here for Gardin Seeds. Shee brot me Some flower. Cyrus finish#t$ Dresing our flax. we had 62 lb in y#e$ whole. mr Prim paid me 6/4_ in Cash and we had a Chees of mr Burten, w#t$ 10, for his Board [one] weak. at home.
