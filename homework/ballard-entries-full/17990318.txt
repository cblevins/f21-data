Cloudy and Cold, snowd some at Even. I went to See Dorcas Densmore, find her more Comfortable than Shee has been. W#m$ & Anny quite unwell. P. Bullin and mr Straten Dind here. Elipt went out forn, is not returnd. we are informd that mr George Brown has frozen his feet so that part of thim are Come off, a Sad misfortune. at mr Densmore*s.
