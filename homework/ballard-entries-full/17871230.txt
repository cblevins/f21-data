Clear. I attended Divine Servis, went to mr Wesson*s in intermision and was Calld at 7#h$ evening to See Thomas Kenny *s wife in Travil. I Broke through the ice on the Eddy by Captain Sewall*s. went into thee water to my waist, got out without asistance. mr Kenny  Carried me a Shoar in his arms. I mounted his hors, wett as I was rode to mr White*s in hast. find the Pateint very ill. Shee was Safe Delivr#d$ at 10#h$ & 30#m$ of a Daughter and is Cleverly. I fell in to the river.
