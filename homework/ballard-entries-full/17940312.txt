Clear morn, Some rain afternn. I Sett out to go to mr Burten*s, it began to rain before I got as far as Son Pollard*s and I Calld there, tarried a little while & returnd home. Cyrus went for Dolly, brot Her home in mr Hamlin*s Slay. at Son Pollard*s. I am informd that mrsHardin wove 1_ yds this Day, & 3 <STRIKE>y</STRIKE> next day.
