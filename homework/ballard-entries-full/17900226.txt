Clear & very pleasant. Hannah was unwell this forenn. I attended a Lecter at the meeting house af#tn$. Rev#d$ mr Eaton & Parson Emerson performd. I Calld at mr Pollard*s; was informd that Sherebiah Town was Declineing very fast; he thinks he Shall not Continue Long in this world. I allso hear that Luke Barton was thought to be Expireing this morn. I went to Lecter & to mr Hamlin*s.
