Clear & Pleasant. mr Ballard Surveyd land that mr Pollard Sold to Esqr Williams, made a Plann & wrote a Deed at evening. mr Daughter*s went to help Thomas Hinkley*s wife quillt a Bed quillt. Ephraim and Ezra went to Pitts town. I have been at home. we got our Beets in. mrs Livermore here at evening. W. Haywad Sleeps here. Dolly tarried till Late. at home, my Girls went to quillting. Dr Colman removd to the hook.
