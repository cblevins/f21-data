Clear and Cold. I was Calld at 3h yesterday morn to See mrs Gill again. Shee was Safe Deld at 5 of a Son (her first Child by mr Gill), and is very Comfortable. mr Gill bestowd 12/ as a reward for my Service, and I returnd home. we brot Harnon Barton with us. Calld at mr Pollard*s and Lambard*s, they are all well. I finisht my washing and Did my other work. mrs Dingley Sent home a Shirt which Shee made for mr Ballard. at ditto. Birth 2#nt$. receivd12/ and 3 lb Sugar.
