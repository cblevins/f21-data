Cloudy & Some rain. Martha Purington Expired at 3h yesterday morn. I have been very unwel but have had to Bake and do my other work. Daniel Savage, his wife, mrs Swanton & Williams & mrs Webber here for Cherrys & Curents. they gave me 2#s$ and 3#d$. mrs Savage brot me [3-2/1]#lb$ Salmon. at home. People here for fruit.
