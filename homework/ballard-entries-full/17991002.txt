Clear. I dug part of my Beets. mr Ballard & Cyrus up at the farm. I was Calld at 4h pm to go and See mrs Kimbal at the fort, Shee was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a fine Daughter at 11h evening. I left her at 11h and 10m and went to See the wife of John Jackson in Sidney, found her Some unwill. I had no Sleep this night. at mr Kimbal*s. Birth 45#th$. recd asa rewd 23#d$ inst 9/.
