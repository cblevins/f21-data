rainy. I was Calld at 1h 35#m$ morn to go and See mrs Gow who was in Labour. I walkt in the rain and reacht mr Gow*s at 1h 50#m$, found the Patient Deld of a Daughter by the help of mrs Tucker and a little more Done by Dr Page. I got her into Bed and dress#t$ the infant, and tarried and attended them till 7 then returnd home. found my famely at Breakfas#t$. I receivd 6/ of mr Gow. Samuel Trask Came here for me to go and See Daughter Lambard. I went and Came home at evening. at mr Gow*s. Birth 30th. recd 6/.
