Clear. I went to my Son*s to see his Son Jonathan who hurt him Self yesterday at his uncle Lambard*s. we are aprehensive he has a Briech. we Bathd and bandaged him and I Came home. I am informd that mrs Whitwel was Deliv#d$ las#t$ night, her infan#t$ Expired Soon and Shee Expired this morning at 4 O Clock. Daughter Ballard took Coffee here, her Son W#m$ Sleeps here. may God Support the bereeved in their affliction and be better to them than what he has taken from them. at home. hear of the Death of mrs Whitwel &infant. mr Livermore here this afternoon.
