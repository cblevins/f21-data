Clear and remarkble pleasant. I returnd home Early this morn, was Calld in at mr Kimbal*s to See a Sick Child, it has the rash and Canker. I took Breakfast at Son Pollard*s, they are well. Calld at mr Lambard*s, all well there; at mr Densmore*s, Polly is more Comfortable than when I Saw her last, tho feeble yet. at ditto and other Neighbor*s.
