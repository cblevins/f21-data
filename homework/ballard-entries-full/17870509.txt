Clear morn. I was calld at 9 O Clok yesterday morn to See mrs Hersey, Shee Being in Travil, and was Safe Deliverd of a fine Son at 1 PM. I returnd home at 5, find the two mr Learneds here. I receivd my fee on six & 8 pence of mr Hersey. mr Thomas Sewall made me a Presant of a Calve Skin. haild & raind about one yesterday aftern#n$.
