Clear. mr Ballard had his flax Sowd. I was Calld to Samuel Cumings to See his wife. Shee was Deld at 2h pm of a Daughter, her 3#d$ Child. I Calld to See mrs Child, find her Seting up. I arivd at home about Dusk, walkt from mr Child*s. at Samuel Cumings#s$.Birth 25#th$. Dolly at mr Dany*s. receivd6/ of mr Cumings July 13.
