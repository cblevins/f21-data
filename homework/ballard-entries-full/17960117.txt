Clear day, Snowd at night. mr Densmore, his wife and Daughter Mary, Son & Daughter Lambard, Ephraim & Phillip Dind here on roast Chickins. I have not been to Meeting. I was Calld at 6h evening to mr Peter Cleark*s to See the wife of W#m$ Moore who was in Labour. mr Eadds Came there for me. Shee would not Dismis me. I had no Sleep. at mr Peter Cleark*s. Death Jim#y$Pages#s$ infan#t$.
