Williams McMaster*s Expired at 3 O Clock yesterday morn. mrs Pattin & I laid out the Child. poor Mother, how distress[d] her Case. near the hour of Labour and three children more very Sick. I Sett out for home. Calld at mrs Hd#s$, I find her Son very Low. at mr Williams*s, Shee very ill indeed. now at home it is nine O Clok morn. I feel as if I must take Some rest. I find mr Ballard is going to Pitts ton on Business. Dolly is begining to weave thee hand kerChiefs. Ephraim & I went to see mrs Williams at Evinn#g$, find her Some Better.
