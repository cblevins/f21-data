Clear. I mended mr Ballard*s Coat foren, went to meeting afternoon. I Saw a Girl take a fan ou#t$ of mr James Page*s Sea#t$ and pu#t$ it in her pocke#t$. I informd mr Page who went to the Seat Shee was in and Shee Deliverd it up after denying Shee had Seen it. I Did not know her name but am informd it is Ellis. at meeting. Reverend mr Stone informd us he proposesa visit to his friends.
