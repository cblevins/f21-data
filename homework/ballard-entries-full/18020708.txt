Clear. I have done my house work & houghd in my gardin. was Calld at 3h pm to go and See my Daughter Lambard who I find very feeble. Dr Coney has given her an Emetic. I find Sister Barton with her and Betsy Kumbal with her as help to do her work. I larried all night. Son Ephraim Slept there this night. at Son Lambard*s, his wife is Sick. Sis#t$Barton there.
