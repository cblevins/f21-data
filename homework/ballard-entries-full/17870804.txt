Clear morn. I pulld flax till noon. a very Severe Shower of hail with thunder and Litning began at half after one, Continud near 1 hour. I hear it broke 130 pains of Glass in fort western. Colonel Howard made me a pres#t$ of 1 Gal w Rhum & 2 lb Sugar on acount of my atendance of his famely in Sickness. Peter Kenny  has wounded his Legg & Bled Excesivily.
