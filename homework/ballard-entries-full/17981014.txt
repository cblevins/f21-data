Clear and pleasant. Cyrus Came up and went to Meeting. I went, was at mr Hamlin*s at intermition. I Calld at Son Pollard*s, his younges#t$ Child is unwell with a Soar mouth. I Sen#t$ Watson with Some honney and Safron for it. I went to mr Dingley*s, his wife is not very well. I Calld at mr Livermore*s, all well there. at Meeting and at mr Dingley*s. it is21 years this Day Since I and my famely landed on kenebe#k$[Shore].
