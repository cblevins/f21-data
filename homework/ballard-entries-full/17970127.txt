Clear and moderate. Polly & Patty Town went to Son Pollard*s. I went to See Nancy Dingley who is unwell with the Cough. Came home and went to See mrs Burten, find her Cleverly. I bot at mr Carter*s Two Shawls @ 4/6 Each which I gave to Son Town*s Daughter*s. I Calld at my Daughter*s. allso, /9 worth of ging#r$ Bread. at mr Dingley*s and at the fort Settlement. we hada Lamb come this Day.
