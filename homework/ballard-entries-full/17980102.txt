at ditto. mr Partridge Came there for me. we Sett out from there, it is a tedious Snow Storm. we advanced 1 mile and broke the Sleigh. mr Partridge went back to mr Leanard*s where we were kindly used and refresht by food, and helped by him & teem as far as William Ward*s where, joind with his OXen and helped me as far Echabod Pitts*s; they left us there and mr Pitts Came on with us. I wrode his hors, we find the Patient Deld of a Daughter and as well as Could be Expected. at ditto & mr Patrag#s$.
