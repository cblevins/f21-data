Clear. I rose and did my mornings work and Mended mr Ballard*s Every day Coat. Son Jonathan brot his two Son*s here and Conducted me to See his wife who is unwell, he went and brot mrs Andrews, Savage, Saunders & Daughter Lambard. his wife was Safe Deld at 6h evening of her third Son & 4#th$ Child. I tarried all night, mrs Savage & my Daughter allso. at Son Jonathan*s. Birth 10th.
