Clear for#n$, Cloudy af#t$. I worked in the gardin all day Diging grass roots, rakeing them of, planting Beens. Ephraim Sett Cabbage Stumps, Beets, Carrots & parsnips & Turnips. Dolly wrode to mr Jans*s. Cyrus Came here from mr Robinson*s at Varsalbor#o$ where he has been to tend mill Since Wedensday last. at home. worked in my Gardin.
