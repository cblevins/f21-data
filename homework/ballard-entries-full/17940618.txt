Clear morn. Dolly went to mr Burten*s to work. I went to See mrs Smith, Shee has taken Cold & is unwell. her Sister Came there yesterday day. Son Jonathan Calld me to See his children, they are Exercised with a Disentary. I was at Son Pollard*s. my Daughter is washing. at mr Smith*s, Burten*s, Pollard*s & Son Jonathan a#s$.James#s$ [Brother] Sleeps here.
