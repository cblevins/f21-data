Clear, wind South. I have been unwel all day. Selvanus Hatch & wife, Old mrs Butterfield & Betsy Andrews here. I was Calld at evening to See the wife of Samuel Babcock 3#d$, found her put to bed with her 1#st$ Daughter & 5th Child by her prest husband. I tarried all night. at Samuel Babcock*s. Birth 6th. Son Jonathan is 49 yearsold yesterday day.
