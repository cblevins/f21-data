Clear. mr Waid helped us get wood. I was Calld at 8h evening to See the wife of mr Jobe Springer, who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 10h of a Daughter, her Eighth Child and 5#th$ Daughter. I left her Comfortable and returnd home about midnight. at Captain Jobe Springer*s. Birth 10th. Son Jonathan a 33 years old yesterday Day.
