Clear Part of the Day; some Showers. I went to See mrs Williams; her Child is very unwell. mrs Pollard there, mr Vahan also. Polly Bisbe tarried with her this night. I was Calld to Leut John Shaw*s Lady who is in travil; left home at 9#h$ 30#m$. Shee was Safe Deld of a Daughter about 11#h$ Ev#n$. I returned home at two. at mrs William#ss$. Dolly finisht StrikeingHarnis to a Forty.
