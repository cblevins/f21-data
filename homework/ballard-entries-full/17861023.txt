A Clear Day. I made a Syrrup for mrs Wesson and for mrs Porter, & Some oil a mulge & went to See mrs Porter. mrs Coy & Daughter were here. Asa Mason here to have his foot Drest. mr Ballard & others worked on y#e$ rode by y#e$ Little Brooks South of this. mr Stephens, a Turner, Sleeps here. Brog#t$ me a morter.
