I was Calld from there at 3h pm to See the wife of Daniel Fosters#s$. who was in Labour, and was Deld of a fine Daughter, her third Child, at 8h pm. I left them Cleverly and returnd to Son Lambard*s at 11#h$ evening. find my Daughter has had her tooth Extracted by Dr Colman and Shee is Easy. I tarried there this night, Slept with my Daughter. at Son Lambard*s. my Daughter unwell, wentfrom there to Daniel Foster*s. Birth 29#th$. receivd6/ as reward. augst 24 recd 3/.
