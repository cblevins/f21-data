Cloudy. mrs Beck had her women Calld after 1h morn, was Deld at 2 of a fine Daughter. I tarried till 9 and returnd home. receivd an order on mr Nason for 9/. Brother Ebenz Moore Came here, Dind. he Sleeps here, has been to Portland. at ditto. Birth 3#d$. receivd an orderon mr Nason for 9/, had it answerd [25] inst.
