Clear and Cold. the Public thanksgiving. my Girls went to mr Hamlin*s. Jonathan Sitt out for Long reach, with a rafft of Boards yesterday evening. mr Wall with him with Boards for Captain Davis. I am informd that Samuel Foster of Winthrop had the misfortin to Break one of his Leggs last Satturday and is tho#t$ to be in Danger of loosing his Life. at home.
