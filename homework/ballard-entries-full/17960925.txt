Clear. Cyrus, Ebenz, & Patty attinded public worship foren. I went my Self afternoon. Dr Coney Calld to See me this morn, he informd me that mr Waid*s infant Daughter Expired yesterday morn. its Death was Causd by the hooping Cough which brot on fitts. I Calld at Son Lambard*s after meeting, Cyrus allso. we took Tea with them. mr Savage informd me that mr Ballard was <STRIKE>unbe</STRIKE> unwell when he arivd at the uper Settlement. God grant the restoration of health to him if yet wanting and return him & his assistants in Safety to their respective homes. at meeting & at Son Lambard*s. Death of mr Waidsinfant AE 31 Days.
