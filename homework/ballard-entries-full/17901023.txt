Clear & very pleas#t$. I Left mrs Snell & infant Cleverly; receivd 6/ as reward. Calld at Captain Hussey*s, he made me a present of a waistcoat for my little Son. I Calld at Colonel Sewall*s & Colonel Howard*s. mrs Polly is better. got home at noone, finish#t$ a pair of mitts for mr Ballard. at ditto. returnd home.
