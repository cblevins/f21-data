a rainy Day. I was So unwel lhat I Sett up but little, but was Calld about 1h pm to go and See Sarah Waid who was in Labour. Shee was Deld about 4 of a Daughter which Shee Declarad John Alby to be the Father of. I was Conducted home Just at night, had my work to do. Ezra Town and Jonathan B. Junr Sleep here. at mrs Waid*s. Birth [8]th. receivd my fee &3/ of what mrs Waid owd me.
