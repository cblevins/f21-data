Cloudy and Clear. I was Calld at 4#h$ 30 [morn] to go to Joseph Brown*s wife, arivd there at 6. Shee was Safe Deld at 8#h$ 15#m$ of her 6th Daughter. I tarried with her till 3#h$ pm, arivd at home at 5. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected. Shee had a Laborious illness but Blessed be God, it terminated in Safety. may Shee and I ascribe the prais to the the [sic] Grea#t$ Parent of the universe. at Joseph Brown*s, Hollowell. Birth 9th.recd 6/.
