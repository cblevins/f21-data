I was Calld to thee hook to mrs Norris, Shee being in travil. Put her Safe to Bed with a Son at 1/O Clock afternoon. Returnd home at four. mr Cowan & Ephraim here Setling w#t$ mr Ballard. Due to them on Ballance 3/8. I Receivd 6/ of mr Norris, 2 remains Due. mrs Chamberlain Sent my Cloath, 31-1/2 yards. Polly Savage Brot home 7 Skeins & 5 knot#s$ of Tow yarn.
