Clear and pleast. I rose at 4h yesterday morn. mr Ballard Sett ou#t$ to Survey for Judge Robbins. I have been Seting my house in Some degree of order. Son Ephraim made me a present of a Chest. W. Cypher brot it from y#e$ Shop and I filld it with lining. was Calld about 2h pm to go and See Son Jonathan as wife. Shee had her women Calld at 9 and was Safe Deld at 11 of her 7th Child and 3#d$ Daughter & is Cleverly. at Son Jonathan*s. Birth 10th. I Came homea#t$ 2 and got Some Sleep.
