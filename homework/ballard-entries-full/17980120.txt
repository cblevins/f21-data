Clear. mr Gill & Lambard, with their wives and Babes, Came here and joind Company with Son Town & his wife and went to pittst#n$ on a visit to Brother Town*s. mr Gill paid me 6/ Cash. mr Ballard gone to y#e$ fort. I returnd from mr Swanton*s at 2h 30m yesterday morn. the Widdow Savage & mrs Rachel there; they are unwell. [at ditto at 2h morn.]
