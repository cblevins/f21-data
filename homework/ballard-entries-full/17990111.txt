Clear and very Pleasant. mr Ballard got 1 load of wood and went to hear Coart, Cyrus went allso. Noah Watson here at evening, receivd 22#Dol$. mr Ballard paid Sally Fletcher all that we owd her. I have Brewd and baked wheat and Brown Bread, and Done my other house work. at home. we paid to Watson 22#Dol$, toSally Fletcher 3#Dols$ & 1:1_. Shee has recd 2/before Shee [ ] worked for us.
