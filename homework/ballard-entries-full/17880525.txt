Clear till 1/O Clok PM, then Thunder & rain. our famely went to meeting Except Ephraim & my Self. I went to See mr Williams famely, they are very unwell. mrs Pollard & mrs Porter there. I went to Bed at 10, was Calld at 11#h$ Evin#n$ to mr Moses*s White*s wife in Travil. I walkt over the String piece of y#e$ Bridg by mr Hovey*s. Ephraim Cowan publisht to Hannah Savage.at Williams*s.
