Snowd. I was Calld at 2h morn to See mrs Heartford who was in Labour, her marm was there. mrs Haywad & Bell were Calld and Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Daughter at 6h, I returnd home at 2 PM in a Sleigh. mrs Heartford had two children Born y#e$ 20th of June last. at mr Heartford*s. Birth 17th. I receivd 12/ asa rewd.
