Clear and warm. I planted winter Squash & Beens and houghd about my Current Bushes and put my Cloath in Lee. I remoovd the wood, which was Cutt, into the door yard in the evening. it is now 11h evening, my famely have been in bed 2 hours. Jabez Cowan*s wife, her Daughter Polly & Sarah Cowan here, they took to Spin 2 lb Cottne and 7 lb Tow. at home. Jabez Cowan*s wife here.
