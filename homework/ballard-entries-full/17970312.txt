Clear and Pleasan#t$. I was with mrs White who was Deld at 3h morn of a fine Son, I left her as well as could be Expected, and returnd home at 7. I reicivd 9/ as a reward. the men went to meeting, I went to bed and Slept a fine nap. Son Town Came here at evening, Sleeps here, his famely well. at Bmn White*s.
