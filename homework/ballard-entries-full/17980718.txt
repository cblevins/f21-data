Cloudy, raind at evening. my Daughter Pollard and mrs Dingley here, helped me do my work; washt my Kitchen &C. mrs Livermore and her Daughter Heartford Came in to See mrs Town. Shee Seems more Comfortable than Shee was yesterday. Son Town Came here afternoon, his famely are well at home. at home. my Daughter Town is a little moreComfortable. I had Company.
