Clear and very warm. we killd a Lamb the quarters waid 36 lb. mrs Cypher*s Came here and brot home Some yarn. Shee and John Blunt dind here. I was Calld to See the wife of mr Eadds, who was in labour,and was Safe Deld of a fine Son between the hours of 4 & 5. I returnd home at evening. at mr Eadds. Birth 33#d$. receivd 9/ as a reward, on y#e$ 31 inst.
