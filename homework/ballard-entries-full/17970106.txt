Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Burten at 1h 20m morn, Shee Seemd to get better. we went to bed at 3. I spent the forenoon with her and went to visit mrs Thwing. was Calld from there at 2h PM by reason of Polly Burten*s being Burnt in a most Shocking maner by her Cloaths takeing fire. Shee being in the Chamber with her Brother Billy, we used Every measure for her rilief but all in vain, Shee became a victim to Death. at mr Burten*s & mr Thwing*s. Polly Burten wasBurn#t$.
