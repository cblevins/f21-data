Clear, a frost yesterday morn. the Patient was Safe Deld at 1h yesterday morn of A Son, her 5th Child and 3#d$ Son. Shee is Cleverly, y#e$ Babe was atact with distrese about 2h after the Birth. we thought it was Dead but it revivd. mr Beals went to Read field and brot a Nurs. I tarried till he returnd and Came home on hors back, had my house to Sett in order. I have not Slept Sence I left home. at mr Beals*s. Birth 2#nt$.receivd 6/ in par#t$ 26#th$. recd 3/ by my husband which he receivd of mr Beals.
