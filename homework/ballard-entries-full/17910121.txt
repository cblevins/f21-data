Clear and very Cold. mr Ballard went from home bound for Sandy river. I was Calld to See W#m$ Densmore, he was Siesd with the Collic, got Ease before I arivd. I returnd home & went Directly to mr Bisbe*s, tarried & watcht. Polly*s feaver very high the most of y#e$ night. W#m$ Howard Came there at evening. the wind Shifted to y#e$ S & blew very hard. mrs Bisbe is very anxious about Polly. at mr Bisbe*s. mrs Rockwood went home.
