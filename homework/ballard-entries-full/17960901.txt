Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard Still prepareing for his Tour. he brot Two Birch Cannoes to our Shore. Patty Easty went after Dineing to Son Pollard*s, Shee informs me that both his children are very unwell. Ephraim brot his dady a Box for Chocolat. Ebenz has been Spreading flax. at home. Son Lambard is 24 years old this day.
