A Severe Snow Storm. I was Calld to See mrs Robinson who was in Labour. Shee Continued ill till 6h evening when Shee was Safe Deld of her 3#d$ Child and 2#nd$ Daughter. I tarried all night, Sett up with the patient. Shee is Comfortable as Could be Expected. I finisht Jack and Le faett#s$ Stockins. at mr Robinson. Birth 8#th$. recd 9/.
