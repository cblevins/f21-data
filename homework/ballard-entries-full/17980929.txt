Clear. my Son Jonathan a & I left Son Town*s and reacht home before Sun Sett. it has been very Cold yesterday day. I left my Dear Daughter a little more Comfortable than Shee was when I went there. Watson got in the last of our flax yesterday evening. Jane returnd here this Morn. at Son Town*s. my Daughter Seems a little revivd. mrssHynes watcht with her last night.
