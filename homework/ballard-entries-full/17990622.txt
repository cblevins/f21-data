Clear. mr Ballard went to mr Moody*s and Bot 7#yds$ Callico, 1#yd$ Cottne, 2 lb Sugar; then he Conducted Daughter Lambard here to make my Gown, thin went up Streat. was informd that Betsy welch was found in the river. he then went to Captain Williams, was Sumoned on the Jury. mrs Dingley here, mrs Livermore and her Daughter Heartfod Calld here. Rufus Ballard and Son Lambard took Tea with us. at home. mrs Land made me a goun. Betsy Welch was found and Buried.
