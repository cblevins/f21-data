Snowd. I was Calld by mr Whealer at day Break to go and see his wife in Labour. Shee was Safe deld of a Daughter, her first Born, at 10h m. I tarried and Dined, then returnd as far as Son Lambard*s, tarried and Sup#t$ there and Samuel Conducted me home at 7h evening. at mr Whealer*s and Son Lambard*s. Birth 50th. recd9/.
