Clear and Pleast. mrs Porter is as Comfortable but weak. I left her at 11h morn. mr Chambers accompanied me home. I arivd at 8h evening, find my famely much better than I left them. Dolly has wrode out this Day. Son and Daughter Pollard and mr Gill, and his wife returnd from winslow las#t$ Sater Day. all well there. at ditto. returnd home. mr Ballardis Surveying at Pittston.
