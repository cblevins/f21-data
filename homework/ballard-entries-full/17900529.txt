Cloudy, began to rain jus#t$ at night. Parthenia went to mr Foot*s. Hannah is at mr Hamlin*s. I have been at home, am not so well as I Could wish. am informd mrs Bisbe is very Sick & Samuel Babcock*s wife. they have y#e$ measels. at Captain Belcher*s & mr Brooks*s. at home. wrote to Brother & Sister Barton.
