Clear. I was Calld from mrs Williams*s at 2#h$ 30#m$ to mr Fuller*s Lady in Travil. Shee was Safe Deld at 1#h$ pm of a Son. I returnd home at 4 & find mrs Polly White here. Shee Slept here last night. mr Fuller paid me 6/ as a fee, gave me 5 Bowls. Extraidinary for medisin. Patty Williams*s fitts on her. at ditto. Polly White left here. Parthenia is 17years old this Day.
