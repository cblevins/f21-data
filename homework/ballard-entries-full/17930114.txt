Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to Town meeting. I baked & Brewd & was Calld to See mrs Thwing at 6h evening. Shee was Deld at 10 off her first Born, a Son. I was waited for by mr Brown to attend mrs Sewall <STRIKE>who</STRIKE> we arivd there at 11 30#m$. Shee was Deld off a Son at 12. I tarried there thro the night. my Patient is Cleverly. receivd 7/4 march 18 1794. at mr Thwing*s & Sewall*s. Birth 1#st$. Birth 2#nd$. receivd 6/ of mr Thwing.
