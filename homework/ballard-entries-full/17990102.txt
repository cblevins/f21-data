Clear and Cold. mrs Pitts was Deld at 45m afler noon of her 2#nd$ Daughter. I left her and infant Cleverly at Sun Sett and went home with Son Jonathan, his wife has had an ill turn and is Some more Comfortable. I tarried there all night. it is Cold. at mr Shubael Pitts*s. Birth 1st. receivd 9/ asa reward.
