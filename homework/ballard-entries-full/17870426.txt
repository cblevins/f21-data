I was Calld at 2 O Clok to Nathan Taylor*s wife in Travil, found her Dilivr#d$ of a Daughter. Dr Williams operator. very rainy. I tarried till morn then Sett out on foot, walkt to the Bridg Below mr Kennady*s when Tylar Came up with a hors. I rode home, arivd at 7 much fatagud. Jonathan & Samuel Dicky had a quarrel; what a Shame it is. Captain Nichols & his Son Jonathan here this morn going to CobessiContee.
