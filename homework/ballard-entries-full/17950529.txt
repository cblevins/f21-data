Cloudy, raind afternoon. I was Clearing my Door yd fore#n$. Asa Mason gave [me] an order on mr Baxter for what he owd me. he Dind here. Captain Nichols & Son Alaxander here directly from Oxford, my friends are well. they bro#t$ a Letter from Josiah Kinsbury. I wrote to Brother*s Collins Moore, Barton & Waters. at home. wrote to my friends at the wes#t$. Dolly Sett a w#b$ of Diaper to work.
