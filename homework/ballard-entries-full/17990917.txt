Clear. I was Calld to See the wife of Moses*s Palmar at 1h 30m this morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 4 of a Daughter, her Second Child. I left her and infan#t$ at 7, Cleverly, and returnd home. find mr Ballard is gone to Son Jonathan*s. I harvisted more Beens. mr James Page Came to See him. I was Calld to See the wife of Moses*s Partridge who is ill. at moses Palmar*s. Birth 41. recd 9/. at MosesPartridges. mr Ballard to Jonathan*s.
