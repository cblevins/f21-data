foggy morn. Captain Hersey Sent for me to See his Babe, it being Sick. I left it Some Senna & manna & returnd home at 11h fore#n$, was Calld imediately to Captain Fillebrown*s wife who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 10#h$ 30#m$. I tarried & took Care of her. mr Town left here, mrs Barton & my Daughter Dolly gone with him. at Captain Hersey*s & Captain F Bns. Birth 36#th$. Dolly is gone to winslow.
