Clear. mr Capin & Daughter Lambard and Jabez*s Cowan dind here. my Daughter & I walkt Down to the Common afternoon to See Companies who met there for revew. Son Lambard took Tea with us. Thankfull & Hepsy went to the perade, returnd to Tea. I went to See the melitia piraded. Cyrus and DagtLambt went with me.
