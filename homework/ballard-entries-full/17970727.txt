Clear and warm Day, rain at evening. mrs Dingley and Daughter Lambard, mr Lambard allso, took Tea with me. I Came home from mr Roby*s this morn, he went to mr Hall*s and Conductd Polly Butterfield home with him. our men all at Son Jonathan*s to work. <STRIKE>mrs</STRIKE> at mr Roby*s. mrs Dingley and Daughter Lambard here.
