Cloudy and Some Snow. John, the Son of J. V. Davis, Expired this morn at 1#h$ 10m. mrs Saunders, Oliv Fletcher and my Self put on the Grave Cloaths, finisht the performance at 3. Dr Colman Conducted me home at 6. I went on the bed and Slept. mr Ballard went to Colonel Duttin*s for hay. Bill Cypher went with the Team. at my Son*s. John Expired at 1h 10m yesterday morn. &AElig; 2 years, 7 months & 13 days. he was a promising Child, how oft are our Expectations Cut off.
