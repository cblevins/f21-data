A Clear morn. mr Brown went in hast after Dr Colman for his Eldist Son being sick. I went from home at 12 O Clok to See him, find him very ill. Returnd home at Sun Set, found Sherebiah, Betsy & Polly Town and James Stackpol here. I was informd that mr Hathaway who was Drowned Last week was taken up & is to be interd this day. Ephraim Cowan Came here yesterday Evinng to work for mr Ballard one month.
