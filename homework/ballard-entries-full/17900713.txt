Clear & hott. we Came to thee Coart house; Saw Melona receive the punishment which the Coart inflicted at 8 hours morn, then returnd to mr Hatch*s. Paid our reckoning & Sett out for home. I wrode mr Pollard*s hors; we Dind at mag#r$ Smith*s. Calld at mr Bullin*s & mr Jackson*s; arivd home near Sun Sett. find Alice Ballard here. Gillbreath helping Cyrus. at D#o$ & returnd home.
