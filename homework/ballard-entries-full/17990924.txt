Cloudy and Some rain. I left my Patients Cleverly at 6h morn. Came home by water, find my famely and friends takeing Breakfast. they left here soon after. mr Ballard went to the hook and to Son Lambard*s, Conducted Allen home. Cyrus to work on the road. at ditto. mr Porter & wife & mrs#s$Foster left here after Breakfas#t$.
