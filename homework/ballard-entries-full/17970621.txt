Clear. mr Ballard went to Son Jonathan*s to work. mrs Dingley & her children Dind here. my Daughter is not So ill as Shee was yesterday, but we Calld Dr Coney to See her. he recomminded Some medicin, left her Some pills. P. Bullin is here this night. Thankfull has Put a piece in the Loom & Sett it to work. at home. Bullin Sleeps here.
