Clear & pleas#t$. I Came from mr Prescot*s after Breakfas#t$. mett mr Ballard on his way to Unity to Survey y#e$ river. I arivd at home at noon. mr Prescot Dind here. he let me have 5 pecks of Rie for my <STRIKE>service</STRIKE> asisting his Lady. mr Livermore & Jessy Bullin took Tea here. I have been to see Neighbor Savage*s folks; find them poorly with a Cold. mrs Patty left here. I am informd that mrs Mason Expird the 21#st$ inst & her remains were Carried to Varsalboro for interment this Day. at mr Prescot*s & Savage*s.
