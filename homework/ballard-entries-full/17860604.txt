A very warm Day. I was Calld at 8 yesterday morn to Brian Fletcher*s. his wife in Travil & was Safe Delivd of a Daughter Betwen ten & 11. I returnd home at 5 afternoon. mr Lee here fore#n$. Ben#n$ Edwards, mr Wels & David Learned Dind here. I receivd 6/ of mr Fletcher. Beriah Ingraham*s wife was Delivrd of a Daughter 30#th$ of may, Extracted by old mrs Fletcher.
