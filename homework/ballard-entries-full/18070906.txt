Clear. mr Ballard, my Self and Son Cyrus attended Bublic worship, it was Communion day. Reverend mr Stone Discoarst from Galations III C 1st vers, O [foolish] &C. I went to Son Lambard*s find his youngest Child Sick, tarried there afternoon. Dr Coney Came there after meeting and opened a Soar on the Childs face. I Came to Son Jonathan*s, Sent for Polly Pitts to go there but Shee did not go. at meeting room. at Son Lambard*s afternoon, their BabeSick. Dr Coney opend a Soar on its face.
