Clear & very pleas#t$. mr Ballard went up to mr Jones*s, informs me that their Son Stephen is Scolt Badly. I went to See mrs Livermore, find her very unwell. her face is Sweld & Shee is in much pain. I applyd a Cateplas#m$ made of hysop and wheat Bran, & Shee Seems Easier. the Ice removd Above here & the river is open this afternoon. at Doc#s$. the rivir Opend.
