Clear and very warm. mr Ballard to Son Jonathan as, Cyrus to winthrop. Hepsy went to mr Hinkley*s to carry filling y#n$. mr Edward Savage*s Widdow Dind with me. Shee has been to Rousekilld. I went to mr Dingley*s, mrs Livermore there. Cyrus informd me that mr Ben#n$ Porter of Goshan has buried his Son Ben#n$, and that his wife bore him a Son a few weaks Since. we had a fine mess of cucumbers. at mr Dingley*s. Widdow Savage here. Cyrus brot newsof a Death & Birth at mr Porter*s.
