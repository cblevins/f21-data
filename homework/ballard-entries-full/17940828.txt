Clear. my Daughter Pollard Came here foren, mrs Davis afternoon. Shee went to Colonel North*s. I was Calld in hast at 8h evening to See mrs Pitts who had an altaration & thought near Expireing. I watcht, mrs Livermore allso. mrs Pitts revivd. at Shubael Pitts*s mrs Livermore &I watcht. Daughter Pollard and mrs [Davis] here yesterday Day.
