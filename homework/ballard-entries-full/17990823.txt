Clear and warm. mr Ballard and Cyrus to hauling Stone. Hepsy washt. I have been So unwell as not to Sett up but little. the wives of Samuel & Phillip Norcross Came to visit me. mrs Livermore Calld. mr Ballard informs me Daughter Lambard is better but had a fright by her little Daughter runing away, but they found her Safe. at home, had Compy.
