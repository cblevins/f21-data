Cloudy & some rain. mrs Howard was Safe Deld at 2h am of her 2#nd$ Daughter and 5th Child. I returnd home at 10h 30m am, receivd 1 Dolr. left Mother and infant Cleverly. performd a Considerable work. I Sent by Ephraim Bond, got 2 qts N E Spirit and 3_ lb Sugar. Daughter Ballard in here afternoon. at ditto. Birth 14th. receivd 6#s$,28 recd 3 & a gift of 10 Candles.
