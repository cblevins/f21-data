Clear and warm. our young folks went to Pitts#tn$ to Church, mr Ballard and my Self to the for#t$. the Ordinanc of the Super administred. a young Gentle man, who was a Stranger, Performd. he Deld Two Exelint Discourses. I went to mr Hamlin*s at intermition. Calld at mr Eadds*s & Son Pollard*s afternoon. Son & Daughter Lambard and their little Son here after meeting. at meeting.
