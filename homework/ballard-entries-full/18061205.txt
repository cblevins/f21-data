Clear and pleast. Sally Cleark Came to finish mr Ballard*s Cloaths. Shee & Jonathan Sleep here. I was Calld at 10h evening by mr Nason to go and See his wife who is in Labour. he Calld her women after midnight. mrs Smith & 2 children & Son Ephraim Supt here. at mr Nason*s at night. Warin Stone receivd mrs Farewel*s Cow that we had last year.
