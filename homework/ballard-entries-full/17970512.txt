Clear and warm. I removd parsley and Burnit roots and planted 30 hills of Cucumbers and made a visit to mrs Joy afternoon. mrs Dingley there. I went to See Sary Neal about weaving for me, Shee Came here at evening and brot home 16 Skeins Lining y#n$ and took 5 lb flax to Spin. at mr Joy*s & Dingley*s.
