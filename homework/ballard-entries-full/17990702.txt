Clear and warm. mr Ballard went and finisht his Seller Drain. Sally Andrews and I washt the west room, the Stairs and Entry. I partly Cleand the Store. Sally & Oliv Fletcher here, the former took her Deeds and threatend to Sue us in 1 weak from this time if we Did not pai her what was her due. Shee looks thin fased to what Shee Did when Shee left us. at home.
