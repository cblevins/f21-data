Cloudy Day, raind at night. mr Ballard and the young men went to meeting at the fort, the Girls to the hook. I tarried at home. mr George Brown & Phillip Bullin Dind with us. I have been at home, Did not feel So well as usual. I had a very Severe fitt of y#e$ Cramp last night. mr Ballard & y#e$ Girls rose to my assistance. at home. George Brown & P Bullin Dind here.
