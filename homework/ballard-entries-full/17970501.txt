Cloudy foren, Clear part of y#e$ afternoon, raind at evening. I was Calld at 5h morn to See the wife of mr Phelps, Shee was in Labour and was Deld at 10 of a fine Daughter. I Calld in to See mrs Guild, find her and famely well. I was at mr Lords. I returnd home afternoon, Sowd Lettis, peper, S grass, radish & Savory Seeds. at mr Phelps*s. Birth 22#nt$.Septr [11] receivd 9/ of mr Phelps.
