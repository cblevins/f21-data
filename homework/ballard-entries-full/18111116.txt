Clear foren, raind at night. Son Ephraim Calld here at evening, gave us a quarter Lamb which wd 10 lb. he informd us he was told that Ezra Town was killd by the fall of a Tree last thursday. if So, may God grant us all to take a Seutable notis of his Dealings with us, may he be A Husband to the weddo & Father to y#e$ children. at home.
