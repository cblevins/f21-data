Cloudy & Dark. mr Ballard went & put up the publishments of Ben#n$ Prescot & Polly Hinkley & Mcalister & Elisabath Carver. Parthenia is unwell. I gave her Some pills. Shee took one in a spoon full of Beer. the wind mett it & Shee was so Strangled Shee turnd Black in her face & almost lost her Breath. I was at home. Parthenia unwell. I wrote to Hannah & Dr Barton, Send by Esqr Coney.
