Clear morn; Showers afternoon. mr Learned & mr Ballard went to Brother Moore*s. I went to y#e$ hook, was at Colonel Duttin*s. his Lady is Low but on y#e$ recovery. I was at mrs Hollowell*s, mr Densmore*s, Reverend mr Foster, Joseph Foster*s, mr Wesson*s & Joseph Williams*s. my Da#ts$ wash#t$ & wove some. Betsy Chever Been here, mrs Savage also. Jonathan Came home. I was at the hook & Joseph Williams*s.
