Clear. BiZer Benjamin & a mr Rice worked here geting wood. Son Jonathan & Joseph Foster junr Dind here. a mr Coulridg Breakfasted. Becky & Patty left here at 11h 30#m$ morn homewd bound. I have been at home, puting my house in order. I have had 47 yards made into Cloath & two Ragg Coverleds Since the Girls Came to work for me. Some part was Spun before. at home. Becky Robbins & Patty Moore went fromhere to their own homes. Son Ephraim Sleeps here.
