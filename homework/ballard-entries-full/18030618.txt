Cloudy. I have put my Candles into the Sellar. I have 11 lb of those made in the winter past and 45 Dozn made this weak. I have allso Sown parsley, pink radishs, peppers. Zepporah Nud Dind here. Lydia went to Hollowell and Came home unwell. I made her Some Cammomile tea. Hannah Pollard Sleeps here. at home, washt the north room and did various otherServises. Lydia Came home Sick. I made her herb Tea.
