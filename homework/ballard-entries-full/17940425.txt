Cloudy. I Came from mrs Burgess*s at 9h morn. mr Ballard has been to y#e$ hook. Captain Bouland of unity & Reuben Fairfield Dind here. Dolly went to mr Bisbe*s. Sally & I worked Some in the Gardin. Jason Livermore Junr took Curren#t$ Sprouts of our Land with out Leav. I Calld him back & he gave y#m$ back. we Sett them & others out in y#e$ Gardin. at ditto.
