Clear and pleas#t$. I was Calld when at Breakfast to See the Lady of Captain Guild*s who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 10h 30m of a fine Daughter, her Second Child, both feemales. I Calld to See mrs Abbot. find her and famely Cleverly. mr Dany Conducted me home. Son Cyrus is gone to his Brother Jonathan*s. he is Set out for Portland. Ephraim took Breakfast with us. at Captain Guild*s. Birth7 th. receivd 9/ as a rew#d$.
