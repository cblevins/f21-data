a rainy Day. I Combd 7 lb of flax for my Self & 4 for Cyrus. mr Ballard went to Captain Cox*s. Hannah is at mr Hamlin*s. Polly Savage here, Drank Tea. mr Savage returnd Jonathan*s hors which he rode to Green. I am informd there was a man Drowned in Jones*s Eddy, who Came paseng#r$ from Boston with Captain Howard. I have been at home. receivd 1/2 Bushel of rie of Captain Hersey as reward for asisting his Lady.
