Clear and pleast. I have done hs wk and knit. Barny Lambard here, mr Ballard to y#e$ Settlement. brot home 2 lb Sugar, _ do Tea, 1 qt Spirit. I was Calld at 6h pm to Son Jonathan*s, his wife being in Labour. we Calld mrs Bennet & Smith and Shee was Deld at 10h 30m of a Daughter that wd 9_ lb. I tarried all night. Son Jonathan has been in Jail, Came home about 9h evening. at Son Jonathan*s. Birth 1st.
