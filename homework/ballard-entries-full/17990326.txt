Clear and very pleast. mr Ballard and my Self went to Son Lamb#ds$, he and Son town were here this morn. mr Town informs me his Daughter Dolly is very unwell, may it Pleas the great Authur of all Good to restore her to health again. I Calld at mr Densmore*s, find his wife Sick, his Daughter is Easier. at Son Lambard*s & mr Densmore*s.
