Clear & Pleast Day, Cool evening. mr Ballard & Ephraim got wood to mr Selvester*s & for our Selva*s. I have been at home. mr Childs here, invited Hannah to go to help his Lady quillt. mrs Densmore here at evening, mr Waid allso. Jonathan Staid from home last night, returnd this evening. I Doubled yarn for Buskins & Some worsted, 24 knots, which mrs Wicksom Comd & spun. at home.
