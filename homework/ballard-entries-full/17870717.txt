Clear. mr Faught here, Says Gidion Barton is very well & Sister went from there this morn. mr Porter & Pamela Left here for her her [sic] Father*s on a visit. I came home about Day light & went to bed. Ephraim is Better, went to School. I went to mr Savage*s afternoon, Shee is very Comfortable. Gillbreath Sleeps here. mr [G Cowan] here on business with mr Ballard.
