Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld at 2h morn to See the wife of mr Phillip Norcross, found her very ill. Shee was Safe Deld at 3#h$ of her Second Son and 7#th$ Child. I receivd 9/ as a reward and returnd home, went to bed and Slept. mr Dingley Calld here. Ebenz went to Pittst#n$ afternoon. Cyrus went to mr Brooks*s, bo#t$ 2 Stone potts for to put Butter in; they cos#t$ 3/6 each - 7/6. at [Phillip Norcross*s]. Birth 44th. receivd 9/ as a [reward].
