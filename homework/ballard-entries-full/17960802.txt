Clear. I helped Do the house work foren, went to Son Lambard*s afternoon. her Dear little infant has got the thrush. I went to mr Baxter*s and Voce*s. Calld to See Daughter Pollard, Shee & children are well. I returnd home, went to mr Beeman*s, find her not So well as Could be wisht. mr Capin has been writing for mr Ballard on plans. at Son Lambard*s and others.
