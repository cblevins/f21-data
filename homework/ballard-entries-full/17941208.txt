Cloudy & Some rain. I left mrs Williams Comfortable as we Could Expect and Came home. Calld at mr Crag*s, all well there; at Son Pollard*s, Doore. they and Son Jonathan*s famely are Cleverly. mr Ballard gone up to fort, Cyrus to hook, Ephraim to meddow. mrs Baxter been here to Day. at mr Williams & Crag*s.
