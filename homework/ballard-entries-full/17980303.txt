I was Calld by W#m$ White at 4h 20m morn to go to See the wife of Timothy Page. we took Captain Cox*s Lady in at his house. the pateint Deld of a fine Daughter which w#d$ 11 2/2 lb at 5h 50m. I was Conducted home by Leut Jessy Bullin where we arivd at 4h 30m pm. I left my Patients Cleverly. mr Page Sent mr Ballard 2/2 bushl wheat in part of what is due for Taxes. at Timothy Page*s.
