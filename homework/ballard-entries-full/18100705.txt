Cloudy morn and Some rain. mr Rowel Calld on me at 6h 30#m$ to to and See his wife in Labour. I found the Child born when I arivd. I finisht the performance. it is hir 3#d$ Daughter and 4th Child. I returnd to Son Jonathan*s, Dind there; mr Ballard allso. I came home, removd Beet Plants and did other matters. at mr Rowel. Birth 11th. recd 9/ as a reward.
