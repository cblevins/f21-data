Clear and warm. I was Calld to See mrs Hamlin at 4h pm, find her very ill. Shee was Safe Deld about 5 of a Daughter, her 5th Child. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd home at evening. I receivd 12/ as a riward. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to work at Son Jonathan*s, his little Son*s Sleep here, Jack [unwell]. at mr Thelp#s$ [Hamlin*s]. Birth 36th.receivd 12/ as a [reward].
