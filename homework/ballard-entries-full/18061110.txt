Clear and very windy. I have Cut Pumkin to drie. I was Calld at 7#h$ 45#m$ to go and See the wife of Asa Mason. Samuel Hovey attended me. the Patient Delivd of her 4th Daughter & 8th Child at 9 and is Cleverly. I tarried till morning and returnd home, his Son Came with me. at Asa Mason*s. Birth 10th. fee receivd by Spining.
