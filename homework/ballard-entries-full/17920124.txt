Clear & very Cold. I tarried at the Captain till evening then Came home. I went to mr Dummer*s & Bot 2#yds$ Sarsnet, 1/2 lb Snuff, 4#yd$ Lawn, a mustard & Sallt; all Cost 8/4#d$, paid y#e$ Cash. I finisht mr Ballard Buskins & was Calld to mr Sewall*s at 11#h$ 30m. at ditto & mr Dummer*s, mr Ballard & Jonathan to meddow.
