Clear. mrs Sew Calld us up at 1h ys morn. mrs Pollard & Voce Calld at 6h morn. mrs Sewall was Diliv#d$ of a Daughter before they got inn & is Cleverly. mr Ballard Calld there & Dind. I Came from there at 2h pm. Calld at Son Pollard*s & Spent ye rest of y#e$ afternoon. arivd at home at 7, feel fatagud. Dolly tarries at Son Pollard*s yesterday night. at ditto. Birth 15th. recd 8/3. herfifth Child.
