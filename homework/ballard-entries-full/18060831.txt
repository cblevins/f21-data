Clear. I was Calld at 0h 30#m$ yesterday morn by Luce*s Hamlin to go and See his wife who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 3h 45#m$ of a Daughter, her 3#d$ Daughter & 5th Child. I left her & infant as well as Could be Expected and reacht home at 7. prepared Breakfast and did my house work and went to meeting, it was Communion. a Stranger to me Performd foren from Luke 14 C 20#th$ V, af#n$ from Isaiah 33 Chap 15 & 16th verses. Polly Town Came here at Eving. at Luce*s Hamlin*s and to Meeting. Birth 4#th$.receivd 12#s$ as reward. I was at Son Ephraim*s at <STRIKE>Evng</STRIKE> intermition, Saw Jonathan & wife there,lhey were at meeting afternoon.
