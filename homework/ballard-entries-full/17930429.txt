Clear, a Coal morn, fine Day. I was Calld between twelve & one yesterday morn to See mrs Hinkley who was in Labour & was Deld of a fine Son at 11#h$. I left her & infant Cleverly & returnd home, find mr Ballard Sett out on a tour of Surveying yesterday morn. my girls have washt & are Scouring house &C. at mr Hinkley*s. Birth 22#nt$, Sixteen of which were Son*s. recd 6/.
