Snowd. Son Lambard had 2 Swine Buschered. I helped her Some packing her Glass & Croccry. W#m$ is unwell but plays abou#t$ Some. my Son had a load of his goods Carried up to Varsalboro where he is going to remove tomorrow, had 2 loads Carried yesterd#y$. I wrode home in a Sleigh yesterday afternoon. they gave me 5 lb pork & 51 herring and Some Crackers & pickeles. at Son Lambard*s. mr Ballard found our Cow with a Calf this morning.
