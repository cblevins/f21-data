Clear. I was Calld to mrs Church y#e$ 5#th$ h Morn. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 2#h$ 30 PM. I left Mother & Daughter Cleverly & returnd at 4. Echabod Pitts Came there for me but I Could not Leave my Patient. Williams Haywad here, bro#t$ Letters from Daughter Town which informs Shee is able to walk & has been to their field yesterd#y$. I was at mr Church*s. Echabod Pitts*s wife Delivrd of a Son yesterday evening by mrs Marsh.
