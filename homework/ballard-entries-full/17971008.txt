Clear foren, raind at night. my famely, Except Cyrus and I, attended worship at the hook. I Did not feel So well as to go, but made a Chees. we had String & Shell Beens for diner. Hepsy informd me that Nabby Andrews is very Sick at Shubael Hinkley*s. Hannah Savage here for advice respecting her Brother*s infant. Shee Says it pases blody Stools.
