[ ] rainy forn, Cleard up at 1h pm. we went to Pitts#tn$ and attended the interment of the remains of our Brother, Thomas Town. Son*s Town and Jonathan a Dind here and went with us. mr Gill and wife went on before us. the Corps were Born to the Church where Prayers were red, than Carried into the Church yard and interd. we Calld at mr Moore*s and took Tea, arivd at home at Sun Sett. Son Town Sleeps here. at the Funeral of Brother Town.
