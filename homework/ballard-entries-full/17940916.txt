Clear & warm. I tarried with mrs Fillebrown till 3#h$ PM when her Marm Came, & I Came home. left my Patent as well as Could be Expected. receivd 12/7 as a reward. the Captain is Confind by Sickness, but we hope he is on the recovery. I left mrs Wesson, Duttin, Conrey, & mrs Loes Harris with them. I wrode home with Nathan Wesson. at <STRIKE>Ditoes</STRIKE> Captain Fillebns,receivd 12/7.
