Clear. I have been at home, finisht futing a pair of hoes for Son Ephraim. mr Ballard has had a Collic turn. Cyrus been to the fort. Son & Daughter Lambard Sett out for Long reach to visit his Connections. a mr Heartford from Monmoth Sleeps here.
