I was Calld at 3 yesterday morn to Daniel Savage*s wife in travil. Shee was Safe Deliverd at Two afternoon of a Son. I Left her & Child Cleverly & arivd at home about 6/, then went to See mrs Hodge*s, Shee being not So well as I could wish. Calld to see Betsy Chever who is not well. returnd home at 9. mr Crag here. Polly & Thop#h$ here at Evinn#g$.
