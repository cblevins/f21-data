Clear & warm. I was Calld to Son Jonathan*s between 3 & 4 morn to See James who was in a fitt. we administ#d$ a Clister which gave Some relief. Dr Ellis was Calld, approvd of whad we had done. he left Senna & manna & drops. I left him at dusk much revived. mrs Baudin there. at ditto. James has had a fitt. mrs Wiman isvery low. Jonathan Junr Sleeps here.
