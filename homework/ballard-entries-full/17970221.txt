Clear. my Daughter Lambard Came here with her Babe. Ezra Town & Son Ephraim took Breakfas#t$, I gave Each of them 6/ to purchace them Bibles with. Dolly Spun me yarn for Candle wicks. mr T Page and P Bullin Dind here. I was Calld to See the wife of Samuel Davis who was in Labour and was Deld of a Son at 7h 30m evening. I tarried all night. at Samuel Davis*s. Birth 12th.receivd 6/7 as a reward. Daughter Lambard here.
