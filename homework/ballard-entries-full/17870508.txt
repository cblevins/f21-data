I returnd at 11 O Clok morn. Left mrs Edson So well that Shee was quilling yarn for her Sister to weave. mr Learned & Sally White Dind here. mr Wellman Sent me a piece of Salmon. Hannah & Dolly, Ephraim & Patty attend School. Pamela went to mr Crag*s. our men turnd off Boards to Davis. Town meeting yesterday. Dr Coney Chosen to Represent this Town in Ge#n$ Coart. mr Car Delegate for Convention at Falmoth or Portland.
