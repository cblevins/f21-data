Clear & Cold. Dr Whicher preacht in the meeting house. mr Ballard attended fore noone, was So Lame he was oblidged to Ly on the Bed afternoon. Clarisa Barton here. Dolly & Shee attended worship afternoon. I had pork & Veal & Green*s for Dinner. Esqr Howard Dind here. I went to See Neighbor Savage, he is mending. I was at Neighbor Savage*s. Polly Wale Camehere to work for me the weak insuing. a frost yesterday morn.
