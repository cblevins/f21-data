Cold. I was at home yesterday Day. mr Hollowell mendid Puter here for which he Received 3 peks of Corn. Old mr Springer & wife here. Elisabath Cosen also. Cyrus Bo#t$ a Case & 12 bottles of mr Crag, pr#s$ 14#s$. George Bolton has our hors, is gone to weding of Daniel Savage junr & Betsy*s Pierce. Jubal Hinkley & widdo Robison are to be joind in Wedlok yesterday Day.
