Clear and moderate. I was Calld at 6h morn from mr Burten*s to mr Kimbal*s to See his Lady who was in labour and was Deld at 4h 30#m $ evening of a Son. I returnd to mr Burten*s at 7, was Calld back at 10, by reason of the infants bleeding, made use of means which preventd it. tarried there thro the night. it snowd. at mr Kimbal*s. Birth 2 nt. receivd 9/as a reward.
