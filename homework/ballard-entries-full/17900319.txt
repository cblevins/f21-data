Clear & pleasant tho Somthing Cool. The Ladies went from here. my Son Jonathan & the Girls went to Winthrop. mr Burten & his Lady allso. mrs Hamlin & her Little Daughter Rode here in the Sleigh with mr Burten. Samuel Pierce Dresing flax here. Foster & Ephraim gone to mr Andrew Goodin*s for Hay. at home. my Girls went to Winthrop.
