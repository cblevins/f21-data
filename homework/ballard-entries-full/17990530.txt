Clear part of the Day. mr Ballard to the Meddow, finisht mending the fence. I was Calld 7#h$ pm to See mrs Burten who was in Labour, and was Deld at 10 evening of a fine Daugt#r$. I tarried thro the night. mr Fenny was there at 8, wisht me to go and See his wife who was ill allso, but Could not leave mrs Burten to Speak with hime, he went after mrs Mosier, Shee was Absent. at mr Burten*s. Birth 22#nt$. recd 12/out of his Store.
