Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard & Cyrus to Coart. mrs Farewel, Daughter Ballard and Son Town took Tea here. Son Town Sleeps here, his Famely all well Except Lucy, who is declineing fas#t$. may God be with her and aford her Comfortable hopes of his love for immortal part, may he Send a guardan Angel to guid her better part to bliss. Son*s Jonathan & Ephraim been to Sidney. Jason Pierce Came to my Son*s. at home. Son Town Sleeps here. Lucy is no better.
