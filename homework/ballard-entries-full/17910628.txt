Clear & pleasant. I wrote to Sister Waters & went to mr Wesson*s. Commited my Letters to him for Conveyance. Hannah Receivd a Letter from Ezra Davis of Boston of 17#th$ inst, wrote him by mr Wesson with hoome I left 6/ to procure wine for Dolly. mrs Livermore here yesterday afternoon. mr Adams & his Brother Left here after Breakfast. at mr Wesson*s, Sent Letters by him tomy friends. Jonathan Sleeps here.
