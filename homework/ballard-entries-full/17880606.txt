Clear & Hott. I Came home before Sun rize. Sally is much as She was. Theophelus Hamlin & James Burten Sett out for oxford. I rote to my Brother Collins Moore. I went to mr Williams af#tnn$. we thought Sally was Expireing. Shee had a Severe feaver fitt. Parthenia & Dolly watcht. I tarried all night.
