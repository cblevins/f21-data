Clear part of the day. I was Calld from Son Ephraim*s to mr Piper*s Early yesterday morn, his wife in Labour. She was Deld of a Son, her 5th Child at 12 O Clock. we took dinner & I left them, went to Daughter Lambard*s; tarri all night. Henry had a very Sick night. I did not undres but Slept Some. Sophia Gill Slept there. my Daughter been to See mrs Mosier, finds her very Sick. at Son Ephraim*s. mr Piper*s & Daughter Lambard*s. Birth 3#d$. receivd 9#s$ at Babcock24 inst.
