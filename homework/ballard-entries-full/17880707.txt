Cloudy morn. we raisd the Saw Mill Fraim. mr Marshal & Thomas Moore were hurt; The Businiss other ways Done with Safety. there were a vast Concorse of men & children & not many Disguisd with Licquor. the Young Folks had a Dance here at evening. Disperst at midnight. Our Saw Mill was Raisd. Robert Denisonswife Dild of a Daughter by mrs Fletcher.
