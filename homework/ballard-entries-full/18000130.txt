Clear and a very Cold morn. I had fire put into our Seller. Son Pollard and Lambard, with their wives and Babes, and Rhoda Came here. we had meet and beens for Diner. Son Jonathan and his wife Joind us at Tea. Rhoda went home with them and their Daughter Hannah tarries there this night. at home. Son*s Ballard, Pollard, Lambard & wiveshere.
