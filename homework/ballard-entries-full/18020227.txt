Cloudy & some Snow. we made my Sausages. I was Calld to See Nathan Tylar*s wife, Shee was Deld at 8h evening of her 6th Son & 10#th$ Child. I tarrie all night. Hannah Bolton Expired at 3#h$ pm. Daughter Pollard was gone to put on her grave Cloaths. at mr Tylar*s. Birth 4#th$. Death of Hannah Bolton.
