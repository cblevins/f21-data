Clear & warm. I returnd home. mrs Dingley Came here. I Cut herbs to Dry, houghd french turnips, then went to See my Daughter Lambard, find her very Comfortable. Daughter Pollard & children & mrs Carter there to Tea. mrs Carter bestowd 18/3_ for my assisting her in travil June 5#th$. at ditto and Son Lambard*s. Shubael Pitts& Sally Cox were Joind in Marriage yesterday Day.
