Cloudy part off y#e$ Day. Hannah washt. I went to mr Livermore*s, his Lady & I went & Spent afternoon at mr Densmore*s, mrs Edson there. Dolly Came home, tarries yesterday night. mr Ballard been to y#e$ hook, Bo#t$ 2/1 Gal#n$ N E Rhum & 1 gal#n$ molases. Dolly went to mr Wesson*s, Bot her Self a pair Shoes. I gave her the money. at mr Densmore*s. S.J. Foster & Samuel Jackson paid me for asisting their wives.
