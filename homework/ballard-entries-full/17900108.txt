Snowd this morn. Cleard of before noon. mr Ballard went to the meddow with Foster & Ephraim, from there to mr Foot*s & Brot home 5 pecks of wheat which Jobe Springer left there for my asisting his wife in Travil Nov#r$ 5, 1786. I have been at home; finisht makeing an apron for my Self & Sewd on my Cotten Shirts. Son Town Came here; informs that his Dear Babe fell on the hand iron & Burnt its face. Sherebiah is a little more Comfortable. I have been at home. Elijah Davis returnd f#m$Boston. Son Town Came here. Luke Barton Cutt his knee.
