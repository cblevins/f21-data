Cloudy. I was Calld by Major Stickney at 4h morn to See his Lady in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 2h pm of her 4th Child, a Son. her women tarried till near night when we left her & infant Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as reward. find mr Ballard is better & gone to y#e$ hook. at mgr Stickney*s. Birth 43#d$.mr [Ballard] is more [Comfortable. we ].
