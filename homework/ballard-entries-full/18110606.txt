Clear morn, raind & Thundered before night. I was Calld Early yesterday morn to mr Hodge*s to see his Daughter Fanny who was in Labour & was Safe Delivered of a Daughter, her first born, which Shee in the Severety of her illness Dectared Winslow Burten was the Falher of. I tarried till 4#h$ pm then left them Comfortable. Came to Captain Pitts*s, went in at mr Thwing*s, Came back to y#e$ Captain*s & tarried all night. at Ezra Hodge*s. Birth 7th. recvd 6/, 12-2/1 lbTea.
