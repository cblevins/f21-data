Clear the most of y#e$ Day. we were greatly alarmd this morn with fears that my little Son was Drownd Crossing the mill Pond. he was Sent to mr Savage*s but mistook his Directions & went to mr Childs*s. mr Jones was here this Day. his man Richardson Sett out with his rafft, got hicht on a rock & returnd, tarries for y#e$ next tide. Dolly and I made Ephraim a pair of Trousers. mrs Polly McKethney & Judy Heuston here, Drank Tea with me. at home. mr Jones here. two men from Sandy riverSleep here. we were alarmed about Ephraim.
