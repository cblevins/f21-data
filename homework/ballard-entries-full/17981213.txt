Cloudy and Cold, Snowd at night. mr Ballard went to See the Sick at mr Livermore*s & finds them better; at mr Dingley*s, they are all better but Hannah who is very unwell. I have done my house work, Cut my Sewett and washt the kitchen floor. Sally washt and went to her Father*s, Cyrus Conducted her in a Sleigh. at home.
