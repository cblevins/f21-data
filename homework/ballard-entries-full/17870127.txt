Cloudy and Some Snow. I was at mrs Savage*s al Day. the Child a little revivd. old mrs Savage Came to watch. mr Ballard Been to mr Pollard*s to here tryal of a Case between the Reverend mr Foster & the Sewall*s, it is ajournd till monday next at 8/O Clok. Pamela & Hannah gone to see the Sick Child.
