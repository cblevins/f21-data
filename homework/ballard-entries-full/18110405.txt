Clear and Cool. my Daughter is as well as Can be Expected. mrs Gill, her Son*s, Luke & Harnon left Son Lambard*s, went to a farm belonging to mr Rice. I wish them prosperity. Hannah Getchel and Sally Ballard Came there at 11h evening. they drove a Carriage from the kenebeck bridg after 2h pm. at [ditto]. H. [Getchel] Came in the night.
