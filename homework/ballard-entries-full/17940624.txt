Clear. mrs Pitts Came here, had a feavour fitt. Cyrus & Dolly returnd. at well at Son Town*s. I was Calld to mr Hodge*s, his wife in Labour & was Safe Deld at 11#h$ evening of a Daughter, her 7#th$ Child, & is Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as a reward. I tarried till Daylight. at mr Hodge*s. Birth 25#th$. mrs Hodge*s is 29 years old yesterday Day.
