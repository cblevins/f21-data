Clear foren, Showers afternoon. I was Calld at 2h ys morn to See Joshua Heath*s Lady who was in Labour, Shee was Safe Deld at 3 of her 3#d$ Son and 5th Child. Shee & infant are Cleverly. I was Conduted hom w#d$ as far as Captain Smith*s by mr Allby. I Calld in to See mrs Pierce, find her Breas#t$ is not well yet. I sorted Beens for Planting and did my other wk. at mr Heath*s. Birth 9th. receivd 6/ June 2nd 3/#S$.
