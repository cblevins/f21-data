Clear & very pleasant. mrs Sherburn was Safe Delivrd at 3 h & 30 M morn of a Daughter. I took a nap of Sleep which was the first since Satterday night. receivd 3/ in Bread & Snuff which, with the flower I had when there before, rewards me for my asisting her. returnd home at y#e$ 11#th$ hour. at ditto. mr Pond let me have 4 lb Butter.
