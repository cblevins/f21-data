Cloudy. mr Thomas Stickney Calld us from his Brother*s to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of her 3#d$ Son at 6h pm and is Cleverly. mrs Devenport and the mrs White*s were Conducted home. I tarried all night. mrs Conrey is Nurs to this Patient. at Leut Thomas. Stickney*s. Birth 16th. recd 9/ asa rewd.
