Clear and warm. Son Lambard Calld in to See me. mr Ballard Sett out to go to Esqr Lock*s at 11h 3 [ ] foren. I have pulld my String Pea vines and gatherd [pees] and Sheled, gathered Mustard Seed, Shelld Some that was dried. I went to Son Jonathan*s, took Tea there. Linda, Hannah, Ephraim and Samuel Pierce Sleep here. at Son Jonathan*s. Son Lambard Calld in to See me.
