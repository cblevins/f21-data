Clear and warm. I houghd in my Gardin foren, went to quillt for old Lady Hamlin afternoon. my Daughter Lambard went with me. Old Lady Lamb, mrs Crag and Burten, the two mrs Hamlin*s, mrs Duttin and two mrs Partridge*s made our Company. a number of her Grand children were there; her Son*s there for Tea. at Old mr Hamlin*s. mr Ballard been to [Jonathan*s] fora Cart and got wood.
