A light Shower in the morn. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Jonathan as, Cyrus Came home at noon to bring Some Lamb. I wrode to mr Lambard*s with him. mr Daughter has been washing. Esqr Haywad*s Lady and mrs Pitts Came and took Tea there, mr Ballard and Cyrus allso. Ephraim is not very well. Polly Hodge*s went to visit Lucy Page. at Son Lambard*s. mrs Haywad &Pitts there to Tea.
