Cloudy morn, Clear afternoon. mr Ballard is gone to Survey for mr Wesson & Esqr Howard. I have gatherd Safron, radish pods & parsnip Seed, Done my house work & made Some Pills for my Daughter Dolly. Sally weaving. Ephraim wounded his foot. Dolly Came home, informs me that Hannah & her Babe are not So well as Could be wisht. at home, heard mrs Pitts was not so well.
