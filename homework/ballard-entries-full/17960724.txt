Clear morn, a heavy thunder and rain afternoon. I was Calld from Son Lambard*s at 10h m to AnSel Ney*s wife who was Deld of a Son, her firs#t$ Born, at 2h pm. heavy thunder while her illness was on her. I left Mother and infant Cleverly, Came home, put Clean Linning on and went to Son Lambard*s. find her without any help Excep#t$ Daughter Pollard there with both her children, and Jonathan as lit Daughter which was brot to take the milk from Dolly*s Breast. at ditto and at Ansil Neys.Birth 37#th$. I receivd 9/3_ of mr Ney.
