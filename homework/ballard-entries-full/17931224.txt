Clear. I mrs Childs a little Easier. I was Calld to John Babcock*s to See his wife at 1h pm. Shee was Safe Deld of a Daughter at 9. I tarried all night, mr [sic] patient as well as Could be Expected; the Negro Dr*s there. at ditto & Babcock*s. Birth 53#d$.mrs Childs had Dr Coney with her this night.
