Clear. I Sett out for home Soon after Day, walkt as far as mr James Savage*s then Rode out as far as mr Isaac Savage*s, his Son*s brot me Down by water. I Searcht my Cloaths immediately after I arivd, found a vas#t$ number of Flees on them. I Calld at mr Pitts*s, Bolton*s & Savage*s. mrs Dorcas Pollard here for Parthenia. at ditto.
