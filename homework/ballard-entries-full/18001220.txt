Cloudy and moderate foren, raind afternoon and evening. mr Ballard went to Survey land for mr Frances*s of Hollowell. the land is near Alpheus Lion*s. I have been Corking the west Side of y#e$ North room. Son Lambard took Breakfast with me. Ephraim has finisht a door to the Buttery and hung it and put a hatch on y#e$ W door. at home.
