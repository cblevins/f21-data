I put mrs Richards Safe to Bed with a fine Daughter at about 4 yesterday Morn. Left there a little after break of Day. Called to See mrs Branard who is unwell & Woodward Allen Wife who is very Low indeed. Calld at Samuel Comings; rode his hors & Saddle as far as Foster*s, Walkd from There. was at Crag*s & Williams. Dolly at mr Crag*s. I Sent 21 Skeins of Tow yn to mrs Chamberlain. Ben#n$ Brown & wife here. I was in bed much fatagud.
