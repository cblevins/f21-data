Clear. I am very unwell with a pain in my Neck & Shoulder. Jacob Chandler here, informd his wife has fitts & he wisht for me to go & See her. the young people Left here, went to Captain Daniel Smith*s. mr Pollard Calld me to his Daughter, Chandler, at 5#h$ 30M evening. we arivd at 7#h$ 30#m$. She was Deld at y#e$ 9th hour of a Daughter, Still Born, & Seems more Comfortable. I Sett up & tended her with her Marm. Shee Seemd revivd in y#e$ morn. at mr Chandler*s.
