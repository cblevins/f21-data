Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to work at Son Jonathan*s. Thankfull washt and went to mr Thwing*s. a man by the name of Edson Dind here, who is on his way to Sandage and Expects to See my Brother Jonathan. Cyrus informs me that Old mr Pierce Expird this Day before noon. may we all attend to the Call, be ye allso ready. at home.
