Cloudy morn. I was Calld at 6#h$ 15#m$ morn to See the wife of Peter Cleark who was in Labour and was Safe Deld of her firs#t$ Daughter & 3#d$ Child at 11h morn. I returnd home at 2h pm. I wrote to Brother Collins and Sister Barton and Cyrus Carried them to Shubael Pitts*s for Conveyanse. at Peter Cleark*s. [Dgt] Birth 8#th$.I wrote Brother Collins & Sister Barton.
