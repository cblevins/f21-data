Clear & very pleast. I was Calld at five O Clok morn to See mrs Read who was Safe Deld of a Daughter at 9. I returnd home at noone, left my Patients Cleverly; receivd 6/ as a reward. Son Jonathan, his wife & children all Supt with us. Pally & Jack Sleep here. mr Thomas waited on Dolly to mr Pollard*s. at mr Read*s. Birth 46th.
