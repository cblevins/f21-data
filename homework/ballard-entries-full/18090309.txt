Clear & windy. Son Town & his children left here after Breakfast to go to Son Ephraim*s. Judge Coney and Captain Seth Williams Calld in to See us. Sally, M and Lucy L here, brot me 3 lb _ of drie fish. I have baked Some Pumpkin Pies and knit, Finisht footing mr Ballard*s Stockins. at home. Judge Coney & Captain Williamshere. Le faett & Samuel Getchel Sleep here.
