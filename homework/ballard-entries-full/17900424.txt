Cloudy morn. Sun Shine before noon. mr Ballard Gone to Settle with Captain Hersey. Hannah Putt Soup on to Boil this morn. I am informd that a mrs Crag of winthrop Departed this life the weak past. mr Andrews gave me a Lemmon yesterday Day & mrs Hamlin gave me a piece of Veal. at home. news of a Death. there was a Drowned Lad taken up at y#e$ Chopps in this river.
