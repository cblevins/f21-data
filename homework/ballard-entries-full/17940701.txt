A light Shower. mr Pollard bro#t$ mrs Voce there. my Daughter is Cleverly. we got her up & made her Bed. I tarried till near night when mrs Porter Came there & I Came home. Cyrus went to winslow. mrs Porter, Foster & Pitts all Sleep here. mrs Pitts Came here yester day & Dolly went to Old mr Pollard*s. at Son Pollard*s. Came home, mrs Pitts& her Sister*s here.
