Clear & very warm Day. mrs Foster was Safe Deld of a Son, (her second Child) at 4#h$ & 50m morn. I left her at 9 as well as Could be Expected. Calld to See mr James Page who is very low by a fall he receivd last Satterday. I find mrs Pitts greatly revivd, how wonderfull is Gods goodness. I Came to Son Pollard*s & Slep#t$, which refresht me, being very much fatagud. Came home at evening. Dolly is Gone to Jon#as$. at Richard Fostres & Pitts and SonPollards. Birth 31st.
