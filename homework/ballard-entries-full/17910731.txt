Clear & warm morn. mr Ballard, Jonathan and Hannah attended public worship and I wrote to Brother Collins, Elijah Barton & Sister Waters to Send by mr Burten. I Sent him 7/11-2/1 by Hannah to purchace things in Boston for me. mr Pollard and his wife here. I was Calld at Sun Sett to Samuel Babcock*s wife, went by water. I was Seis#d$ after I gott there with thee Collic. Did not Sleep any this night. at Samuel Babcock*s. I wrote to my [friends] at Oxford.I had the Collic yesterday night.
