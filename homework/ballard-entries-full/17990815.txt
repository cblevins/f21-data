Clear. Cyrus went to Jonathan*s, Samuel Came for me to go and See Daughter Lambard. Shee was Concerned about her infant. I tarried there till near night when Amos Partridge Came for me to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 9h evening of a Son, her 3#d$ Child. Shee and infant as well as Could be Expected. I tarried all night. mr Ballard returnd from New Sharon about noon. at Son Lambard*s and mr Partridge*s. Birth 36th. recd12/ Novr 19th as a reward.
