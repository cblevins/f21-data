Clear and Pleast. Son Pollard brot his wife and children and Daughter Lambard & babe here, they Dind with us. we went to Son Jonathan*s afternoon. mr Lambard, Son Ephraim, mr Gill, his wife, mr Trask and his wife, Son Cyrus all took Tea there. I felt very unwell, had a Severe pain in my head. I was at Son Jonathan*s. my Daughter*s were here to Dine.
