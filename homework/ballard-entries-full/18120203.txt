Clear part of the day. mr James Gore of New Sharon brot us Corn, rie & flower in part of what he owed us. he paid 5 Doll#s$ in money. I have been at home, my head is Soar. H. N. and Sally Sleep here. it began to rain at 10h evening. mrs Davis & Rhoda Pollard Calld here, took Tea; left here near Sun Sett. at home. Rhoda Pollard & mrs Davis here.
