Clear. mrs Cowan*s illness Came on about Two yesterday morn & Shee was Safe Delivd at 3#h$ 30m of a fine Daughter, with 5 pains after my inquiry in to her Case. I tarried, took breakfast and Sett out homewd. Calld at Colonel Sewall*s, his Child is much better. I arivd at home at 10h morn. I receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is gone to pitts#tn$. Fanny Cox & Hannah Goodin Came here. at ditto. Birth 24#th$.
