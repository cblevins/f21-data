Snowd. Sally washt, I did house work foren, was very unwell afternoon. was Calld at 4h 40m to go and See the wife of Moses*s French who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 8h 30m evening of her 4#th$ Daughter. I tarried all night. our women were mrs Tilar, two mrs Moore*s, mrs Wellman & Coy. mr William Moore Conducted me to the Patient. at Moses*s French*s. Birth 15th.
