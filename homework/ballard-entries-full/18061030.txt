Cloudy the most of y#e$ Day. I have Sorted apples & Cut some and done my house wk. Son Lambard Came and Conducted the girls home. W#m$ Stone*s wife brot 14 Skeins filling yarn which Shee spun for me. I had paid her 2#s$ before. Shee had _ bus#l$ apples yesterday day. at home. mrs Slone brot my yarn home.
