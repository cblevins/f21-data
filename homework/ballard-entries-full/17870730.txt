Clear & pleas#t$. Hannah Came to mrs [Foster*s]. I returnd, Calld to See mr Foster; find him mending. Left Lidia better also. Dolly is unwell. Colonel North is returnd. Dr Colman Enforms that Betsy Stackpol was Drouned 28 inst in y#e$ river Sebastacuk. May God Sanctify y#e$ instance to us all. I am informd that mr McMaster*s Son Daniel is very Sick with the rash. mr Williams has Bled again & is very poorly. mr Ballard & Dolly unwell. I Batht their feet & gave them Some herb Tea. I feel much fatagud my Self. Ephraim Cowan & Ezekiel Leighton here yesterday night.
