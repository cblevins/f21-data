Clear & very warm. I Came to Son Pollard*s, took Breakfast. Ephraim Brot y#e$ hors & I Came home, met mr Ballard, Selva Moore & James Sturges on their way to Surveying. may they be preservd in Safety & return. I left mrs Pitts more Comfortable, am informd that Shee has been Carried into mrs Pitts Since I left her. Sally Cox is gone to watch yesterday night. at mr Pitts*s. mr Ballard Sett out fora Tour of Surveying, Ephraim to bring the hors home.
