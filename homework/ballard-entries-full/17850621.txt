I was Calld at two O Clok in morn to go to Bunker Farewel*s; got there at Sun rise. I put mrs Farewel*s to bed at 2 in y#e$ afternoon; returnd home at Eleven Even#g$. had a fit of ye cramp & fainting also. Dolly got up & brought ye Lavender which revivd me much. mr Farewel Gave me 7#s$:4#d$ for my performance.
