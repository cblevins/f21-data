Clear & Cool. I was Calld to mr Rowel*s, his wife in Labour. I reacht there at 11#h$ am. the Patient Deld of a Daughter, her first born at 1#h$ pm. I left her & infant as well as Could be Expected & returnd home about 4. I baked after I Came home, recd 1 Do#lr$. at mr Rowel*s. Birth 6th. recd 6/ in part, July9th paid 6/ in full.
