Ephraim was taken with an Other fitt about 2h this morn. we Calld mr Livermore again, thinking he was Expireing. he Came to his Sinces in Some Degree & Slep#t$ quietly before Day. mr Livermore tarried till morn. I laid down on ye bed with my Child & Slept a little. we were So fatagud we [did] not attend worship. at home. mr Ballard at meeting. Son& Daughter Pollard [here]. Ephraim is Better.
