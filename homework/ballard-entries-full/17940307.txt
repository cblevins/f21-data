Cloudy, Snowd Some. mrs Smith was Safe Deld at 6h pm of a fine Son & is as well as Could be Expected; her illness was very Severe. mr Smith Conducted me home at 9#h$ evening. he gave me 18/7 as a reward for my Service. mrs Cleark, Felps & Robinson were assistants at the Birth. at ditto. Birth 9#th$. a first Born.Bror Moore Sett out for Oxford. I Sent a Letter to Sister Waters.
