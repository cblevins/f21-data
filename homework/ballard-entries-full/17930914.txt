Clear. I Did house work till near night & worked in my Gardin. Sally & I made Some Pies. I was Siesd with a Severe Pain in my Back which lasted an hour when I Seemd freed there from. mr Buzel Calld me to go & See his Sister Judy who was in Lab#r$. I arivd at his house about Dusk. I felt the Collick before I reacht there, was Severely Exercised with it thro the night. at mr Buzel*s, New France.
