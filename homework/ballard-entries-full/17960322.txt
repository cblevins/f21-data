Cloudy. Nabby went to the hook with her Cousin. I washt my kitchen, was Calld at 9#h$ 30#m$ evening to See the wife of James Kenny  who was in Labour, and was Deld of a Son at 11#h$ 30#m$ evening. I left my patients Cleverly and returnd home at 3h morn. mr Kenny  gave me 6/7 for my Service. at James Kenny *s. Birth 14#th$.receivd 6/7.
