Cloudy and Snowd all Day. mrs Blanchard had her women Calld and was Deld at 11h evening of a Daughter, her first born. Shee was very ill. I was Calld to See Joseph Brown*s wife immediately after I put mrs Blanchard to bed. wrode on hors back with out any Saddle accompanied by mr Atkins and Brown*s Son. at ditto & Joseph Brown*s. Birth 49#th$. I receivd 8/1 for my Service at mr Blanchard*s.
