Snowd and raind Some. I Came from mr Burten*s, left James mending. Came to mr Pillsbury*s find her very ill, in a kind of Deliriam. her raising had ceast and her mouth very dry, they informd me Shee had been much So thro the night Past, it is my opinion the use of the Bark was in Some measure the Cause. at ditto & ditto. mrs Pillsbury is verySick indeed.
