Clear. I Came home and was Calld by mr Crag to See his wife who was in labour, & was Deld of her first Child, a Son, at 10h evening and is as Cleverly as Could be Expected. I was Calld from there to See the wife of Reuben Branard who was Delivd of her first Son & 4th Child before my arival. at Captain Hussey*s, Coney*s &Branards. Birth#s$ 2nt and 3d. Asa Cumings#s$remains interd this afternoon.
