Clear. I have worked very hard till after Six pm whin I was Calld in hast to go to See the wife of Abiah Herington who was in Labour, and was Deld at 7h 30#m$ by the help of mrs Smith. I found her and her infant Daughter Cleverly on my arival. I tarried all night, there were 22 in number Slept under that roof yesterday night. at mr Braley*s. Birth 30th.
