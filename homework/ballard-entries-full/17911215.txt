Clear & Pleast. mrs Selvester was Safe Deld of a Son which wd 8-1/2 lb at 3#h$ morn. I left her Cleaverly at 11. Calld at Captain Fillebrown*s & Jenny Web*s. walk#t$ part of ye way home, wrode the other. receivd 2 lb Coffee, 1#yd$ ribbond & a [Cap boader] as Extraodenary for waiting on her. I went to mr Densmore*s, was informd that mr Ezekiel Page*s youngest Child Expird last night. at ditto. Birth [35]th. fee receivd.mr [sic].
