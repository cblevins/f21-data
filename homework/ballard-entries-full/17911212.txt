Snowd. I left mr Jones*s at noon. Calld at mr Wesson*s, Dind there, find her weak. Shee has not walkt her room any Since Shee was put to bed. her infant is at mr Hewen*s. I Calld at mr Densmore*s, their children are giting better. Esqr Lithgow here, requested of me to go & See his Lady next weak. we had a very rainy night. at ditto & mr Wesson*s & Densmore*s.
