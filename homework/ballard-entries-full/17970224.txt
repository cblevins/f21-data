Clear. mr Ballard went to Dresden. Son Jonathan Came for me to go and See his infant who is not So well as they Could wish. I Calld at mr Dingley*s to See their infant, it has the Cough but is not so ill as it has been. Daughter Ballard is as well as Could be Expectd. Jack went home with us, Le faett & Hannah are with their aunt Hitty. at Son Jonathan*s. at mr Dingley*s.
