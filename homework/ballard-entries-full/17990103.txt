Cloudy, Snowd Some. I Came from my Son*s with mr Ballard, L faett with us; left my Daughter better. we Calld at mr Hamlin*s Store, bot a pair Specktacles, price 4/6; _ yd Cambric 3/9; 3 yds quallity /6; thread /4_; Biskit /6; plates 3/6. we calld to see mrs Pitts, find her Cleverly. we Dind at Son Lambard*s on roast Turkey, his Brother Truman and wife there. Son Ephraim Sleeps here. at Son Jonathan*s & others.
