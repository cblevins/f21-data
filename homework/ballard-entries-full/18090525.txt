Clear. I was Calld by mr Caton at 2h 30m yesterday morn to go and See his wife. I arivd there at Sun rise, found her delivard of a Son. her 11th Child, but not Safe. I performd what was necessary and left her as Comfortable as Could be Expected. arivd home before ten and Sent herbs and other matters. at James Calon*s. Birth 4th. fell from the hors onmy way home. receivd 9#s$ of William Babcock.
