Clear. mr Town & I left my house at 8#h$ this morn, we were put to Some dificulty at the ferry, but Got Safe over at 11 h, and arivd at mr Town*s at 6#h$ evening; found them well. mrs Stackpol & Straten there quillting a Coat for mrs Barton, we got it out. I was fatagud, but went to bed & had rest. I went to mr Town*s.
