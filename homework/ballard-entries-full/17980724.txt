Some heavy Showers this afternoon. I have done a larg wash and am fatagud. I brewed, allso. Daughter Lambard Came before I had finisht, She assisted me. mr Ballard been to Jonathan*s. a mr Sturges Calld here, informd me that Son & Daughter Town pas#t$ by his Dadys yesterday a little Past noon. at home. Daughter Lambard & children herethis afternoon.
