Clear. mrs Howard & mrs Dexter to see my Daughter. Shee had an ague & feaver fitt & is reduced very Low, her Babe Exercised with much pain. I feel some fatagud, I am informd that mr Piper & one of his children were Drownd, his wife & another Escaped with Life. at ditto. Piper & Child Drownd. Seth Wilmswife Deld of a Daughter.
