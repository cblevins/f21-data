Cleard of at evening. mr Pollard Came to his Son*s and Conducted me to his house, I went from there to meeting foren, was Calld at intermition to mrs Burten who was Delivd at 3h pm of a Fine Son and is Cleverly. I tarried all night. at ditto, meeting & mr Burten*s. Birth 9th. I recd 24/ as a reward for Servics Done.
