Clear. I went to Dr Colman*s at 1#h$ pm, his Child Expird at 4. I put on the grave Cloaths and tarried till 7. Colonel North and Lady there. I found mrs Williams & mrs Harris here at my return. I Sett up till very late to finish Hannah*s Stockins. Death of Dr Colman*s infant. I wasthere. Dolly finisht her web, 44-1/2 yds. Beniah Ingirham hada Son born.
