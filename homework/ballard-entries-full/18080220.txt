Cloudy and Cold. I have Brewd, knit, finisht a paer of mits for my husband and knit on his Stockin. this is the anneversary of my Birth, 73 years have I Seen, pas#t$ thro many Sceins. may it pleas y#e$ Great Parent of y#e$ universe to Enable me to liv devoted to his Service the little time I have to Sojourn in this world. Allen Lamb brot us 23 lb Beef. at home, have Seen 73 years yesterday Day. Allen, [Barny] & Ephraim B. Dind here. Funeral mr Edward Springer.
