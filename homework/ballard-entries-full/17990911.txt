Sister Williams went to her Son Augustus*s and returnd here before noon, then took her leav of us and Sett out for home. Jonathan Came here. my Son Cyrus is 43 years old this Day. mr Livermore and Densmore Sett up with mr Ballard and I undres#t$ & went to bed at 11#h$, Slep#t$ till 3h morn then rose by reason of the Cramp, and mr Livermore went home. at home.
