Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus to meeting. I have Cookt y#e$ head & harslet of y#e$ Veal. Son Jonathan, his wife and 3 children partook with us, Luke Barton allso. he Came for & had a Loin of Veal for Son Ephraim. Reverend mr Stone Discoarst foren from Proverbs 16 C 3 V, afternoon from Daniel 3#d$ C 12 vers. at home. Son Jonathan & wife Dind here. I receivda Letter of march 7 from Brother Collins Moore.
