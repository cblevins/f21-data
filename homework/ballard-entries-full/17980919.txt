Cloudy morn, raind afternoon. Son Lambard*s Daughter had fitts this morn, he Came for me. I went and found the babe a little revivd. mrs Haywad there. I administred a Clister which gave great relief. I tarried all night. Son Lambard & Pollard and his wife went to Son Town*s. Sister Williams went from here to Son Pollard*s and tarries there while they are gone. at Son Lambard*s, their Babe had a fittthis morn.
