Clear. mrs Sewall was Safe Deld at 3h yesterday morn of a fine Daughter, her 7#th$ Child. I left her Cleverly and returnd home at 9#h$ 30m. I receivd 6/7 as a reward. mrs Livermore tarries with her till he went to magr Fillebrown*s for her nurs. mr Ballard been to Son Jonathan*s, all well there. I have had a very bad head aick this afternoon & evening. at Gen#l$ Sewall*s. Birth 8th. receivd 6/7 as a reward.
