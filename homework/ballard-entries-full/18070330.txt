Clear. I left mr Braley*s Early this Morn, Came to Son Jonathan*s. he Sent for his Father, we took Breakfast there. Son Lambard & [Gidion] Barton allso. I Came home and did Some washing. George Heartwel had a fitt and I was Calld there. [Loiza] Gilbe is unwel allso, and Parker*s Child. at mr Braley*s, Son Jonathan*s & Heartwel*s; have doneSome washing.
