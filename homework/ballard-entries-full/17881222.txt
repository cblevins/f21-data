Clear & Cold. mr Ballard & the other Gentlemen who are the meeting house Committee mett at Colonel North*s to Settle that Business. I went to mr Livermore*s. Polly is Decaying; his infant is unwell. Widdow Williams Came there at evening. my Girls washt & wash#t$ the room. mr Page Sup#t$ here. mr Ballard wrote a note for Eliphalet Robbins against Daniel of 10 Dollars. I went to mr Livermore*s.
