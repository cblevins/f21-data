Clear. I am more Comfortable. mr Isaac Savage Second#s$ & wife here on a visit. mr John Abbot Calld me at 6h pm to See his wife who is unwell. I was there this night. Shee and I Slept Some. mr Town took Breakfast here. Dolly went to Son Pollard*s. mrs Cashin here foren. at mr Abbot*s. mr Savage and wife here,Son Town allso.
