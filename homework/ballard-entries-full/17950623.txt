Clear till near night, then we had a Shower. mr Ballard & Ephraim weading Corn between mr Livermore & here. I have worked in my Gardin the most of the day, feel fatagud. Sally Came here this morn. Shee Brewd yesterday afternoon. mr Ballard brot 1 Gall#n$ molases & Pork home. at home. Sally Cox Came from Son Lambartsy#s$ morn.
