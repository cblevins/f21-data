Cloudy, Snowd Some. I feel Some more Comfortable, went to washing; mr Ballard to mr Shepard*s. my Son Jonathan Came for me and Cyrus went & Brot our hors back. we went in hast, find my Daughter ill. her women were Calld & Shee Safe Deld at 8#h$ evening of a Son, and is very Comfortable. at my Son*s. Birth 7#th$.
