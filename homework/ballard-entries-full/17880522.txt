Cloudy & Some rain. I did house work fore n#n$. was Calld to See mrs Sewall at the hook. Shee is Exercised with a Swelling in her Left Legg & Some pain. my Da#ts$ washt. old mr Coutch Departed This Life at 2#h$ pm. I Calld at mr Williams*s & they are all unwell. Sally no Better. Death old mr Coutch. I was at ye hook & mr Williams*s.
