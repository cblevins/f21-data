Clear. mr Ballard and the young men went to meeting. my Daughter Town rode on a hors to the road this morn & in a Shais afternoon, as far as mr Hinkley*s; her husband with her. Dr Odihon here to See her. mr Gill & wife, Widdow Savage, Son Jonathan and wife, my Daughter tarries, Son & Daughter Lambard. at home. Son Town Came. my Daughter has been not Soill as on friday. Hannah Moore [Calld].
