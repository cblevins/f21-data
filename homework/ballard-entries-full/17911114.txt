Cloudy & moderate. mr Dany Calld me Early this morn to See his younges#t$ Child who is Burnt on his face. I went & made an ointment & Dress#t$ it. I went to See mrs Selvester, find her Cleverly. I Calld at [mr] Palmar*s, Bo#t$ a pail, paid 1/6 off John Babcock, Thomas Hinkley & Ben#n$ White. I receivd of each 6/ as a reward for assisting their wives in Child [Birth]. I was at mr Densmore*s, his children unwell. at mr Dany*s & other Neighbor*s. I Spent evening at mr Densmore*s.
