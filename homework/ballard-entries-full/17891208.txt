Clear & pleasant. I returnd home, feel Some fatagud. find my famely much as they have been. mrs Palmar let me have 2 lb Chocolate & 2 lb of Butter. I receivd Butter of Esqr Petingail*s Lady for pills Shee had of me. mr Savage watk out this day. Esqr Lovjoy Calld here. Captain Porter, mr Burten & their wives & mr Ben#n$ Porter Drank Tea here; the Latter Sleeps here. Jonathan very unwell Since he returnd from Winthrop. at Esqr [Petingail*s] & mr Savages.David Pette*s wife Departed this life y#e$ 4#th$inst in Child Bed. Shee left 12 children to moarnthe Loss.
