Clear and very warm. I went to See Son Jonathan*s wife who is unwel, has y#e$ ague in her face & other Complaints. Dr Howard to See her, Extracted a tooth for her. mrs Pierce there, informd me that Warin Stone & Sally Church were to be Joind in wedlock this day. mr Ballard Cut fart of his Stalks and got some wood. Allen Lambard here. at Son Jonathan*s, his wife unwel.
