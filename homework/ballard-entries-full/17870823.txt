I Sett out to visit Joseph Foster*s children, met Ephraim Cowan by Brooks*s Barn. Calld me to See his Daughter*s Polly & Nabby who are Sick with the rash, find them very ill; gave directions. was then Calld to mrs Shaw who has been ill some time. Put her Safe to Bed with a Daughter at 10 O Clok yesterday Evinn#g$. Shee is finely.
