Clear and hoter than it has been this Sumer. mr Ballard been to Town meeting and to Gen#l$ Sewall*s. Nabby Washt and went to mr Densmore*s. Son and Daughter Town Came here at 7h evening, my Daughter is feeble but not more So than I Expected to See her. may it please God to restore her to health, or prepare her for a happy futurity. mr Ballard informs me that Peggy Crag Expired this afternoon at the 6th hour. may we all attend to the Call and be ready. Watson Came home at 10#h$ 15#m$. I have been at home, Sewd Some and houghd Cucumbers. at home. Son Town brot his wife here.
