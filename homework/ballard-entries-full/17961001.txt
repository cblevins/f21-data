Clear. I went after Breakfast to Son Lambard*s, they were Seting out for to go to mr Capin*s. I assisted them to Dress & See them Depart, then went to Son Pollard*s, they are well Except little Hannah. Shee is better than Shee was. I Came home near night. Cyrus & Ebenz housd our flax. Cyrus then went to the hook, bot at Fillebrown*s & Stratns _ g#ln$ Spirit & 1 lb Coffee, paid Cash 6/. Son Ephraim Came to Sleep here. at Son*s Pollard*s & Lambard*s, the latter went toVarsalboro.
