Clear morn, Cloudy Day. I was Calld at 2h 15#m$ yesterday morn from mr Partridge*s to See the wife of Samuel Damerin who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of a Daughter, her first born Child at 10#h$. I left Mother & infant Cleverly & arivd at home at 1h 30 munits. at mr Damerin*s. Birth 51st. receivd 9/ as a reward.at mrs Byerns*s.
