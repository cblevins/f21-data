Clear. mr Ballard came to Son Pollard*s and I Came home, left my Daughter Low bu#t$ as well as could be Expected. there was a great discharg from her Breast this morn. we Calld at Son Lambard*s, they are well as usual. am informd that mr Allen is interd this afternoon. we Calld at Son Jonathan*s, his Son wilm is Sick. the Reverend mr Joseph Bowman, who was the Pastor we Satt under at OXford, Came to see us this afternoon. at Son*s Pollard*s & Lambard*s.
