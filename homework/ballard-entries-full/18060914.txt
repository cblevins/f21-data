Clear. I was Calld at 2h yesterday morn to go and See a mrs Perkins who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son, her first born, at 4h morn, and I returnd home at about 7h morn. left my patienls Cleverly. Cyrus Came here and went to meeting. mr Pratt performd from Ezekiel 7 Chap y#e$ last Claws of y#e$ 10 vers, pride is Buded. Son Jonathan & wife & 3 children, Jason Pierce & wife & 1 Child here to Tea. Cyrus Sleeps here, H. Pol#d$, H. & Sally Ballard allso. at mr Perkins*s. Birth 5th. recvd 9#s$/ as a rewd.
