Cloudy. mrs Sewall was Safe Deld at 10#m$ morn of her 8th Child and 5th Daughter. Shee was viry ill a little while, but is Cleverly now. Shee is pu#t$ to bed. I took Care of her till Day light, had a Shor#t$ nap of Sleep. I left her and infant at 10 O Clock as well as Could be Expected. recd 12/ as a rewd. I Calld at mr Gill*s, his inf#t$ is unwell; was at mr Miller*s, Kimbal*s, Pitts and Son Lamb#ds$. mrs Baxter there. Sally Taylor is mending. at ditto. Birth 48th. recd as rewd 12/.
