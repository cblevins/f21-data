Clear. mr Ballard Surveying for Esqr Lyon. mr Floid*s remains were intered. I have been doing house work and Some in my Gardin till 3 O Clock afternoon, when I went to Son Jonathan*s. his Daughter is much as Shee was. S. Jewet Foster & his wife there. Son Lambard is 28 years old yesterday day. at my Son*s. the Child is much as She has been. the remains of mr Floid were interd this day.
