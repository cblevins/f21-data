Clear & very hott fore#nn$, a Shower & heavy Thunder afternoon. Rachel Foot here & mrs Welch. Jonathan returnd from Coart. Wil#m $ Howard of Winslow Sleeps here. I have been at home. Spent part of the Day Seeking The Red Cow. Cyrus & True found her with a Calf & Drove y#m$ home. I hear Norcross is Comin and that his Brother fell over Bord [&] was Drowned on his Pasage. at home. hear from Coart the Criminals Condemned& Reverend mr Foster has Lost his Case with Walker.
