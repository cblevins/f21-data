Clear & pleasant. Son Jonathan*s wife Came up here, brot James & Elis#a$ with her. mr Partridge Came in to See her. I have had a pain in my Stomach till near night. mrs Ballard had 4 gees killd, pickt & gutted. Shee left here at 10h evening. at [home]. Jonathan*s wife here. Jonathan & [Forgeson][Sleep] here.
