Cloudy and Some rain. I was informd this morn that my Daughter Lambard was unwell. I went to See her, find her more Comfortable than Shee was las#t$ night. Calld at mr Gill*s to See her Son who is unwell. Came to Son Pollard*s, tarried all night. mr Ballard Calld there, he Came home from Surveying this Day. at my Childns and mr Gill*s. Son Town Slep#t$here, his folks all well as Can be Expected.
