Cloudy and Some rain till noon. mrs Page was Deld about 5h morn of a Son, her first Born. I lef#t$ her and infant Cleverly and arivd at home at noon, acompanied by Old mr Page. Son Jonathan here. Old Lady Hamlin, mrs Burten, mrs Dingley, Ruth Rockwood & two of her Brother*s took Tea with me. at [mr Page*s. receivd 12/. Birth 46th].
