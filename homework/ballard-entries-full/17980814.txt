I Sett out from Son Town*s for home, left my Daughter Some revived. may the Great God Support her thro all he Sees fitt to lay upon her in yesterday tife and Croun her with glory here after. may he be a very present help to her ConSort and children and all Connections!!! I Calld at mrs Capin*s and Rufus Ballard*s and mr Ben#n$ Brown*s on my ret#n$. at Son Town*s. Cyrus went to Son Town*s.
