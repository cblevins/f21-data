Clear. Nabby is So well that Shee washt. I have mended Cloaths for mr Ballard, he went to Son Jonathan*s. Ephraim here this morn for Some Sause and Eggs. Cyrus & Ebenz ploughing North part of y#e$ field & Door yard. mrs McMaster, her Daughter Bell and mrs Hardford Calld here. I was unwell at evening, but was Calld at 11 O Clok to Peter Cleark*s Junr, his wife ill. I was at Peter Cleark*s.
