A SE wind & rain this fore#n$, Clear at evening. I have been at home. Cleand the Seller & pickt a pound of wool which I had of mr Snell. mr Ballard been to the fort & hook, is gone to y#e$ fort again to meet the Select Gentlemen, requested an order for what is Due to him from this Town. they did not grant it but paid him forty Shillings in part of his acount. at home.
