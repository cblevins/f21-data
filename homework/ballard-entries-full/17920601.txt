Clear & Cooler. I went to mr Crag*s, helped Dress the wounded. Samuel Johnson was Carried on a Bier Born by 4 men from mr Crag*s to mr Childs where was Dresst. I took Care of him thro the Day. Dr Colman informd me his wife & children went on Board a vessel bound for Nubury. I went in at mr Hamlin*s, & Burten*s. mrs Wicksom was there. at mr Crag*s & Childs & Hamlin*s.
