Clear but a very high wind. I was, I was [sic] Calld at 6#h$ morn to go to Phillip Norcross*s wife in Labour, men Shoveld thro Drifts & we arivd at 7. I left mrs Bolton & inf#t$ Cleverly. mrs Norcross Deld at 1h 30m pm. of her 5#th$ Child & first Son. I tarried all night, Slept a little. my Patients are Cleverly. mr Norcross was not at home. at Doe*s & mr Norcros#s$.Birth 5th.
