Cloudy, a Chilly air. Esqr Coney here. I paid him for his attendance & medesin for my Daughter Dolly. his Demand was 6/. mrs Porter left here after we Dind. my Daughter*s & Sally Densmore went to help mrs Norcross quillt a Bed quillt. Pally went to her uncles to take care at his little Son. Ephraim went to See her Safe there. I have been at home. at home. mrs Porter went home, my Dagtsto quillting, Patty to Jonathan*s.
