Clear. I was Calld at 2#h$ morn to go & See Midian Cowan*s wife who is unwell. I See her in Safe Circumstance and returnd home, have felt allmos#t$ Sick. Lydia to work at my Son*s. mrs Farewel took Tea here, Shee informd me that Theophelus Hamlin*s wife was Deld last night. at midion Cowan*s. receivd 3/9 for my trouble.
