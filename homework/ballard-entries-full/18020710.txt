Clear part of the day. I have had the pain in my Stomach very Severe but did my house work, Excep#t$ Churning which Sally Tole did for me. I was so ill after Shee went home that for Some time it Seemd I must Expire Soon, but the author of all mercies was grant me relief for which I disire to prais his holy name. at home, have been very ill all day.
