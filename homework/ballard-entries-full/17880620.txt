Cloudy morn. I was Calld to Daniel Savage Jun#rs$ wife who is in travil at 10#h$ morn. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son at 9#h$ evening. I Left her Cleverly. arivd at home at 11#h$ 45#m$. mr Ballard gone to Varsalboro. Clarisa Barton been here and mrs Loes Hinkley, Polly White from Falmoth & Susanna Cleark. I was at Daniel Savage*s.
