Clear. Patty Town has Been very Sick, her throat has Canker in it. mr Ballard & the Girls went to the funeral of Captain Sewall*s Child. Maj#r$ Duttin and his Lady Came here. Gillbreath, Leighton & Son Sleeps here. I am informd that Pamela Barton is publish#t$ to mr Porter of winthrop, a widower. mrs Read was here this Day. I hear that Thomas Sewall*s Son is very Sick.
