Cloudy foren and a Shower; Clear part aftn, raind at Ev#n$. Son & Daughter Lambard & Ephraim Sett out to go to mr Capin*s, his Daughter Jerusha is to be Joind in wedlock with a mr Webber of Varsalboro. I have done [] my house work and Planted Cramby Beens, W. squash, musk and watir mellons, Some Beens I had of mrs Edson. at home. Son Lambard, his wife, Babe & Son Ephmwent to mr Capin*s.
