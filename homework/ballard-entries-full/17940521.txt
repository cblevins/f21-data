Cloudy & Some rain. I was Calld to See mr Moses*s White*s wife in Labour, there all day & night. Edmond Fortis, a Black man, was Conducted to Pounalboro Jail for Commiting a Rape on mrs Tilton & afterwds murdering her on y#e$ evening of y#e$ 18th inst. A most Shocking Sein which he has Confest. he has a wife & 2 children. at mr White*s. Edmond Fortis Conductdto Jaile for murdering mrs Tilton.
