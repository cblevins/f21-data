Cloudy. my Self & Parthenia & Dolly tarried at home; we had roast veal for Dinner & Bak#t$ Some Pumpkin pies, mrs Young from Sandy river here. mr Savage & wife Drank Tea here. they have been to See mrs Foster; find her very Low. mr Williams much the Same as he has been. we hear mrs Hains & mrs Pullen are Both like to recover. I have been at home. Son Town was here yesterday,he was after Dr Williams for Cap#t$Haills Child.
