Clear and warm. Son Ephraim left his hors for me to go and See his children. they are not very well. I Calld to See mrs Young, find her very low. my Son*s children Some beller. I went to See mrs Ranlet, find her feeble. was at mr Orock*s, their infant is very Sick. Calld at mr Livermore*s, he has children Sick allso. I Slept at my Son*s. at Son Ephraim*s and visited others who were Sick. Captain Gartand Cut his throat.
