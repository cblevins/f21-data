I tarried at Son Pollard*s till after Dinner, then went to the Stores and Son Lambard*s. was at mr Crag*s and Calld on Sally Hamlin; and at Old mr Pollard*s, his Grand Son David put a puter Buttun up his Nose. Dr Coleman took it out. mrs Smith Calld me in to See her, made me a present of 3/4 yd Lawn. little Rhoda Seems a little more Comfortable. at my Son*s and other Neighbrs. Son Cyrusis 39 years old this day.
