Clear and pleast. mr Waid worked for us. Son Jonathan Came and Conducted me to his house after we Dind. mr Dingley here, paid me 12/ for service done for his wife & famely and medisin. I was Calld from my Son*s between 10 & 11h evening to See the wife of Timothy Branard. I Should have wrote Reuben. mr Floid attended me. at Son Jonathan*s & Reuben Branard.
